This regulation grants tax remission to Innu individuals, Innu First Nations, and certain Indians or bands for taxes paid on lands designated as Innu Settlements in 2002 or during specific periods, based on the assumption that those lands had been reserves throughout the relevant period. The remission applies to income tax, goods and services tax, and provincial taxes, with conditions including no prior rebates and a requirement for a written claim within two years of tax payment or the order's coming into force.
