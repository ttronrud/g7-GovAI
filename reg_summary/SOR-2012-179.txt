The Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, has enacted the CJFTA Rules of Origin Regulations to implement specific provisions of the Canada–Jordan Free Trade Agreement, including Articles 4-1 to 4-15 and Annex 4-1, which have the force of law in Canada. These regulations come into force on the date section 32 of the Canada–Jordan Economic Growth and Prosperity Act is enacted, with the regulations becoming effective on October 1, 2012.
