This regulation grants privileges and immunities to representatives of foreign states and officials of international organizations participating in specific meetings related to the Montreal Protocol and the Vienna Convention for the Protection of the Ozone Layer, held in Montreal in November 2017, as provided under the Foreign Missions and International Organizations Act. These privileges and immunities are based on the Vienna Convention on Diplomatic Relations and the United Nations Convention on Privileges and Immunities, and apply during a defined period from November 16 to November 26, 2017.
