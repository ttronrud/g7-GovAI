These regulations implement the CPTPP's rules of origin in Canada, specifying that goods in heading 87.03 qualify as originating if they undergo a change in heading or meet a regional value content of at least 40% (net cost method) or 50% (build-down method), with special rules for Canada-Australia and Canada-Malaysia trade. The regulations come into force on the date section 43 of the CPTPP Implementation Act comes into force or upon registration if later.
