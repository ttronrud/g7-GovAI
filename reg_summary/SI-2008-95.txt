This regulation authorizes the Minister of National Defence to make one-time ex gratia payments of $24,000 to living veterans or science and technology workers who participated in nuclear weapons testing or nuclear decontamination activities during specified periods, or to eligible beneficiaries of deceased individuals in similar circumstances, with applications due by December 31, 2009, and payments ceasing by December 31, 2010, unless extended for exceptional reasons. The payments are made without admission of liability by the Crown.
