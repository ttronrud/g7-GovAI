This proclamation establishes the standard time zones for Nunavut, Canada, defining Eastern Standard Time for areas east of the 85th meridian west, Central Standard Time for areas between the 85th and 102nd meridians west (excluding Southampton Island), and Mountain Standard Time for areas west of the 102nd meridian west and within the Kitikmeot Region. It is issued by the Governor General of Canada on behalf of the federal government, pursuant to Order in Council P.C. 2001-804.
