This regulation approves the Class II Nuclear Facilities and Prescribed Equipment Regulations under the Nuclear Safety and Control Act, establishing safety requirements for the design, operation, licensing, and decommissioning of Class II nuclear facilities and equipment, including irradiators, teletherapy machines, and brachytherapy remote afterloaders. It outlines detailed application requirements for licences, certification criteria for equipment, and safety measures such as access controls, radiation monitoring, emergency stop systems, and worker training.
