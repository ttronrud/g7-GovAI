This regulation grants a 75% remission of rent and fees paid between April 1, 2020, and September 30, 2020, to eligible non-residential leaseholders and licensees of public lands under Parks Canada, including those who would have qualified for the Canada Emergency Commercial Rent Assistance program, provided they submit an attestation by December 31, 2020. The remission applies to commercial leases or licenses and specific agreements involving Banff, Jasper, and the Rouge National Urban Park.
