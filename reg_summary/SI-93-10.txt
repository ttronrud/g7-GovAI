This regulation grants a tax remission to eligible non-profit organizations under the Excise Tax Act for taxes paid or payable in respect of property or services used in a business of supplying grazing land, provided certain conditions are met, including registration, an election to take effect before January 1, 1994, and a timely written application to the Minister of National Revenue. The remission is limited to taxes that were payable before November 6, 1991, or paid before that date without becoming payable.
