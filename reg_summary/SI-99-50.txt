This regulationproclaims that the Canada-Korea Agreement on Social Security enters into force on May 1, 1999, establishing rules for the mutual recognition of social security benefits, including pensions and disability benefits, for Canadians and Koreans who work or reside in either country. It ensures equal treatment, allows for the crediting of contributions from one country toward benefits in the other, and provides mechanisms for cooperation, information sharing, and administrative implementation between the two governments.
