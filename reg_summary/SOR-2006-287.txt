These regulations implement United Nations Security Council Resolution 1718 (2006) by prohibiting Canadian individuals and entities from engaging in transactions involving the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea (DPRK), including the sale, transfer, or supply of weapons, luxury goods, and other prohibited items, as well as the movement of bulk cash or financial services that support the DPRK’s military or weapons programs. Exceptions are made for humanitarian activities, diplomatic missions, and activities approved in advance by the Security Council’s Committee.
