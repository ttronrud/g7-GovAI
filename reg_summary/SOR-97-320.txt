This regulation establishes quantity limits for certain fruit and vegetable imports from Chile under the Canada-Chile Free Trade Agreement, setting specific aggregate and individual quantity caps for designated tariff items during defined periods. The order came into force on July 5, 1997, and applies to imports of goods in tariff items 0703.10.92, 0810.10.11, and 0810.10.92, with limits adjusted annually for the specified time periods.
