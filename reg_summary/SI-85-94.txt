This regulation grants a remission of customs duties and sales tax on specific goods imported between January 1, 1983, and March 31, 1998, in connection with the Canadian Patrol Frigate Project, to support the development and construction of 12 patrol frigates and associated shore facilities. The remission is subject to strict conditions, including timely claims, reporting requirements, and verification that Crown contracts with Saint John Shipbuilding and Drydock Company Limited have been fulfilled.
