This 1996 regulatory order grants a remission of customs duties on specific billiard cloth imported into Canada between July 1, 1995, and December 31, 1997, for use in manufacturing or repairing billiard tables, provided a claim is made to the Minister of National Revenue within three years of importation. The remission applies to tariff items 5111.19.00 and 5112.19.10 under Schedule I of the Customs Tariff.
