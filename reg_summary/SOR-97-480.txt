This regulation grants remission of anti-dumping duties on specific hot-dipped galvannealed steel sheet used in manufacturing non-exposed motor vehicle parts, effective October 23, 1997, subject to conditions including import date, timely claim filing, and proof of use in motor vehicle parts.
