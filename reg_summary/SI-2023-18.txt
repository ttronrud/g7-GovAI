This regulationproclaims that the Agreement on Social Security between Canada and the Republic of Austria enters into force on July 1, 2023, allowing both countries to recognize and credit periods of residence or contribution for social security benefits, including pensions and allowances, under specific conditions and rules that ensure equal treatment for eligible individuals regardless of where they reside. It also establishes procedures for administrative cooperation, medical examinations, and the handling of personal information between the two countries.
