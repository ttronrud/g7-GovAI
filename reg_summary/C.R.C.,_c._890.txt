This regulation establishes grading standards for grain and screenings that do not meet standard grades, including off-grades and classifications like "Sample" or "Sample Salvage," and specifies detailed quality requirements for feed screenings and mixed grain products, with moisture, stone content, and weed seed limits defined by region and grade. It also introduces specific grading names and specifications for screenings and mixed grain, including limits on contaminants and allowable components based on type and condition.
