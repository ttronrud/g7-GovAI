These regulations, under the Privacy Act, outline procedures for accessing and correcting personal information held by government institutions, including requirements for forms, identification, examination rights, and notification of corrections. They also specify which investigative bodies are authorized to handle personal information and establish retention periods, disclosure rules, and protections for sensitive health-related information.  

*Note: This regulation has been partially repealed or revoked by subsequent amendments (e.g., SOR/2018-39), and certain provisions are no longer in force.*
