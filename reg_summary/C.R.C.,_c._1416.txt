This regulation establishes definitions and rules for vessels, including pleasure crafts, ocean data acquisition systems (ODAS), and seaplanes, outlining safety requirements, navigation rules, and compliance standards under the Canada Shipping Act, 2001. It applies to Canadian and foreign vessels and ODAS in Canadian waters and on the high seas, with specific provisions for visibility, traffic separation, safety equipment, and special circumstances such as fishing, dredging, or restricted maneuverability.
