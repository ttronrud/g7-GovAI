This regulation establishes the rules and criteria for the Agricultural Marketing Programs under the Agricultural Marketing Programs Act, including definitions of key terms, rules for determining producer relationships, criteria for assessing market conditions and product processing status, security requirements for advances, and specific amounts and timelines for program payments, defaults, and repayment obligations. It also sets fixed financial thresholds for different program years and product types, such as canola producers, and outlines procedures for handling defaults, deaths, and legal incapacity of producers.
