These regulations establish a loan insurance program in Atlantic Canada to support commercial projects in designated sectors and regions, providing up to 90% (or 95% in exceptional cases) insurance on loans for eligible projects, with specific requirements regarding project viability, capital costs, and borrower eligibility.
