These regulations establish strict controls on archaeological and palaeontological activities in Nunavut, requiring permits for surveying, excavation, or removal of artifacts and fossils, with Class 1 permits allowing non-invasive work and Class 2 permits permitting excavation and removal. They also prohibit the possession or sale of artifacts or fossils removed after June 15, 2001, and mandate the submission of detailed reports and curation of collected materials to ensure cultural and scientific preservation.
