These regulations implement Customs Notice 13-006, establishing the procedures for verifying the origin of goods eligible for preferential tariff treatment under the CPAFTA, including requirements for verification letters, questionnaires, visits, record-keeping, and consent, and they come into force on April 1, 2013. They outline the conditions under which a verification visit can be conducted, including notice, consent, postponement, and observer participation, and specify the responsibilities of exporters, producers, and customs authorities.
