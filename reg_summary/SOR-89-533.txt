This regulation revokes a previous order and establishes the Miawpukek Band as a recognized Indigenous band under the Indian Act, comprising individuals of Canadian Indian ancestry who were ordinarily resident in Conne River, Newfoundland, on June 28, 1984, or are descendants of such residents. The band is officially named "Miawpukek Mi’Kamawey Mawio’mi" or "Miawpukek Band."
