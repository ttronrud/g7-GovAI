This regulation grants remission of customs duties on the temporary importation of mobile offshore drilling units (such as drilling platforms and jack-ups) into Canada for exploration, delineation, or development activities between May 4, 2004, and May 4, 2014, with specific conditions regarding use, eligibility, and timely claim submission. The remission applies only to units used in offshore drilling activities and must be claimed to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness before May 4, 2016.
