This regulation establishes the critical habitat for the endangered Roseate Tern, excluding areas under the jurisdiction of the Minister of Fisheries and Oceans, and comes into force upon registration. It designates specific coastal areas in Nova Scotia, including Country Island, North and South Brother Islands, and nesting colonies on Sable Island, within a 200-meter seaward extent of the mean high tide line, while excluding portions within the Sable Island National Park Reserve.
