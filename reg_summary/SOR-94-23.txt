This regulation establishes the rules for determining the country of origin of goods imported from NAFTA countries, specifically for marking purposes under the Canada–United States–Mexico Agreement (CUSMA), and applies when goods are produced in a CUSMA country. It outlines criteria such as the use of domestic versus foreign materials, changes in tariff classification, and specific thresholds for disregarding foreign materials, with special provisions for fungible goods, commingled items, and certain personal or household goods.
