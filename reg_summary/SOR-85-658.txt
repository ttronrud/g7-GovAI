This regulation exempts specific property, rights, and interests of the Northern Transportation Company Limited from disposal under the Northern Transportation Company Limited Disposal Authorization Act and transfers control and management of those assets to the Minister of Public Works or the Canadian Commercial Corporation. It includes real property in Fort McMurray and the Northwest Territories, certain agreements with the U.S. Air Force, and specific vehicles and equipment, including those related to Tuktoyaktuk and the Dew Line radar stations.
