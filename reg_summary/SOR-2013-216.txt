These regulations implement Canada’s verification procedures for determining the origin of goods eligible for preferential tariff treatment under the Canada–Peru Free Trade Agreement (CPFTA), setting out requirements for verification letters, questionnaires, visits, record-keeping, and consent, all coming into force on August 1, 2009. They establish the conditions under which customs officers may conduct verification visits in Peru, including notice requirements, consent, observer participation, and timelines for responses and extensions.
