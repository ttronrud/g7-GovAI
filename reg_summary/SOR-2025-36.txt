The Governor in Council has enacted the Certain Products Containing Toxic Substances Regulations under the Canadian Environmental Protection Act, 1999, establishing strict controls on products containing coal tars, polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs), and 2-butoxyethanol, requiring permits for manufacturing, importing, and selling these products, with phased bans taking effect starting October 1, 2025, and full restrictions by December 31, 2025, except for export or transitional provisions.
