This regulation grants tax remission to Nisga’a Indians for taxes on their estate, personal property, or use of former reserve lands, provided the property would have been exempt under section 87 of the Indian Act and the tax has not already been refunded or rebated. The remission applies from the date the Nisga’a Final Agreement Act comes into force until May 31, 2008, for transaction taxes and December 31, 2012, for all other taxes.
