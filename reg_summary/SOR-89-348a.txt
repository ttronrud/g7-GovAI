This regulation approves the exclusion of individuals appointed on or after July 1, 1989, for short-term (less than six months in any 12-month period) positions in the Technical, Operational, and Administrative Support categories within the Department of Forestry during the period July 1, 1989, to June 30, 1991, from the provisions of the Public Service Employment Act. It also establishes specific regulations governing the employment of such short-term personnel during that period.
