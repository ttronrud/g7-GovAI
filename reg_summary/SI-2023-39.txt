The Governor General, on the Prime Minister's recommendation, transfers control of the Canadian Intergovernmental Conference Secretariat from the Minister of Infrastructure and Communities to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness under the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act. This transfer is outlined in Section 207 of S.C. 2003, c. 22, and is part of the broader regulatory framework in R.S., c. P-34.
