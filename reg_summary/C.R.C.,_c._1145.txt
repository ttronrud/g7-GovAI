These Regulations establish safety standards for the construction, operation, and location of ammonium nitrate and ammonium nitrate mixed fertilizer storage facilities on railway right-of-way, requiring Commission approval for facilities exceeding 200 tons in capacity, specifying minimum distances from residential, commercial, and industrial structures, and prohibiting open flames, certain chemicals, and combustible materials. The regulations emphasize fire safety, non-combustible construction, proper ventilation, and strict controls on electrical and heating equipment to protect public safety and property.
