This regulation expands the grant of land in Yellowknife, Northwest Territories, by including any portion of specified lots within 30.48 metres (100 feet) of the ordinary high water mark of Yellowknife Bay. The affected lots are Lots 1, 2, 5, 6, 7, 9, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, and 16 in Block 19, as shown on survey plan number 40264.
