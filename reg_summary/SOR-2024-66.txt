The Attorney General of Canada has approved a list of alcohol screening devices under section 320.39(a) of the Criminal Code, including models like the Alcolmeter S-L2, Alco-Sûr, and Intoxilyzer 400D, for use in determining alcohol presence in a person’s blood. This Approved Screening Devices Order comes into force upon registration and replaces a previous order dated SI/2023-1.
