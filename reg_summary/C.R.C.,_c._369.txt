This regulation outlines the procedures for consolidation orders under Part X of the Bankruptcy and Insolvency Act, including application fees, filing requirements, creditor notices, consolidation account management, and court procedures. It specifies that debtors must pay a $20 application fee, creditors may be required to provide affidavits if related to the debtor, and all payments are held in a separate court account with interest at 5% per annum, while also detailing notice requirements, objection periods, and the conditions under which proceedings can be transferred or terminated.
