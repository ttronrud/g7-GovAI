On August 12, 2021, the Government of Canada transferred the control and supervision of the Federal Economic Development Initiative for Northern Ontario from the Department of Industry to the Federal Economic Development Agency for Northern Ontario, with the Minister of Economic Development and Official Languages appointed to preside over the new agency. This transfer is authorized under paragraph 2(a)a of the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act and is codified in S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 207 and R.S., c. P-34.
