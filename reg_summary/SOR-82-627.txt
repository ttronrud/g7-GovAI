These regulations authorize the Minister of Industry, Trade and Commerce to provide insurance for loans made by private lenders to The Lake Group Ltd., up to a total of $18 million at any time, with the requirement that insurance payments are limited to the amount in effect on the date of repayment demand and that applications for insurance must include required information. The insurance is governed under the Appropriation Act No. 1, 1980-81, and the total coverage is subject to Treasury Board approval.
