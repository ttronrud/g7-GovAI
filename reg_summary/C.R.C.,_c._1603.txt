These regulations establish the framework for licensing water power developments in Canada, defining key terms such as "actual construction" and "actual cost," and outlining the requirements for applications, including detailed plans, financial disclosures, and public notice. The process includes stages from initial application and public hearings to the issuance of interim or final licences, with emphasis on feasibility, public interest, and proper cost accounting, while ensuring that only authorized and necessary works are included in construction expenditures.
