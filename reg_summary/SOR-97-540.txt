This regulation prohibits the issuance of interests in specific frontier lands in the Yukon Territory designated as the Rampart House Historic Site, effective December 4, 1997, to protect its historical significance. The prohibition applies to the entire area of Lots 1 and 2, Group 1301, except for portions within 60 feet of the Canada-United States border and parts of Lot 1 where fee simple title has already been granted.
