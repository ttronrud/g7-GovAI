The Sechelt Indian Band approved constitutional amendments in a May 25, 2019 referendum, which updated membership qualifications, expanded voting and candidacy rights to non-resident members, clarified referendum and election procedures, and established a separate election law to allow future procedural changes without constitutional amendments. These amendments were declared in force by the Governor in Council on the advice of the Minister of Crown-Indigenous Relations under section 12 of the Sechelt Indian Band Self-Government Act.
