These regulations govern the staking, leasing, and permitting of territorial lands in Canada for the extraction of materials such as carving stone, loam, and sand, gravel, or stone, with specific limits on quantities and areas, and require applicants to meet certain conditions, including compliance with marking requirements and submission of detailed applications. Leases and permits are issued by territorial land agents, with terms not exceeding 10 years, and royalties or fees apply to most individuals, except for government, educational, religious, or charitable entities.
