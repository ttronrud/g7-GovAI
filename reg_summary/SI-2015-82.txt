This regulationproclaims the entry into force of a supplementary convention between Canada and the United Kingdom to amend their 1978 double taxation avoidance treaty, updating provisions on tax residency, profits from permanent establishments, interest and royalties, and tax credits, with specific adjustments to ensure fair taxation and prevent double taxation for residents and enterprises in both countries. The key changes include broadening the definition of "person," clarifying tax treatment of international transport, and introducing limits on tax on interest and royalties for residents of the other country.
