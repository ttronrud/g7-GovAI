This regulation grants temporary remission of customs duties, goods and services tax, and excise taxes on specific goods imported into Canada for the 2010 Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games, including exemptions for authorized importers, Games family members, media accreditation holders, and certain official entities, with conditions on import timing, use, and export or disposal. The remission applies to goods and vehicles used exclusively in connection with the Games and is subject to strict eligibility criteria and time limits.
