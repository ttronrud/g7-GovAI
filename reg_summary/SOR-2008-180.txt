These regulations, effective July 1, 2008 (with updates in 2024), require financial institutions to provide clear, comprehensive, and non-misleading disclosures to investors before issuing principal-protected notes, including details on terms, risks, interest accrual, redemption terms, and potential amendments. The disclosures must be made orally, in writing, and on websites or upon request, with special provisions for electronic or telephone transactions and advertising requirements.
