This regulation grants a remission of excise tax paid by individuals between October 1, 1997, and March 31, 2001, to the First Nation of Nacho Nyak Dun, in accordance with specific refund provisions in their self-government agreement, but only if those provisions are formally consented to by the First Nation by April 1, 2001. The order comes into force on April 2, 2001, subject to that consent.
