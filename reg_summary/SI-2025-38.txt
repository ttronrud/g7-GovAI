The Governor General, on the Prime Minister's recommendation, transfers certain powers, duties, and functions related to emergency management from the Minister of State (Emergency Preparedness) to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness, as previously transferred under Order in Council P.C. 2024-1183. This reallocation is made under section 7 of the Emergency Management Act and is authorized by the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act.
