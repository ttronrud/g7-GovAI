This regulationproclaims that the Canada-China Agreement on Social Security enters into force on January 1, 2017, establishing reciprocal social security rights and obligations for individuals employed or residing in either country, with specific rules on coverage, contributions, and mutual recognition of benefits under Canada’s Old Age Security and Canada Pension Plan and China’s Basic Old-Age Insurance schemes. The agreement includes provisions for administrative cooperation, confidentiality, dispute resolution, and mutual recognition of residency periods for pension purposes.
