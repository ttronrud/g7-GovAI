This regulation grants a fee remission for individuals whose passports, permanent resident cards, or citizenship certificates were lost, damaged, or destroyed due to the 2016 Fort McMurray wildfires, provided they meet specific conditions related to residency, application timing, and documentation. To qualify, applicants must have resided in or been present in Fort McMurray or surrounding areas during the wildfires, submit an application within specified deadlines, and provide evidence linking the loss to the fires.
