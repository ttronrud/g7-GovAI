This 1990 regulatory order grants tax and duty remission for goods, real property, and services used by or supplied to U.S. military personnel, their families, and U.S. civilian employees at American bases in Newfoundland, as well as for supplies to U.S. military establishments in the region. Remission is subject to conditions, including the provision of information by the U.S. government, prevention of resale to non-eligible parties, and timely claims submitted within two years of purchase or final accounting.
