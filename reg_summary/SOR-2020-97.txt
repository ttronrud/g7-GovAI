This regulation declares an amnesty period from May 1, 2020, to October 30, 2026, allowing eligible individuals and businesses to safely possess, store, transport, or destroy specified prohibited firearms and devices without criminal penalties, particularly those who owned such items on May 1, 2020, or were involved in their transfer, repair, or destruction under specific conditions. The amnesty aims to facilitate the lawful disposition of these items while ensuring public safety through strict storage, transportation, and handling requirements.
