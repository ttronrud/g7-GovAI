These regulations establish standard working hours for shipping employees on ships operating from East Coast or Great Lakes ports in Canada, limiting daily hours to eight and weekly hours to 40, with exceptions for lay-day plans and averaging periods. Employees may work more than 40 hours per week or exceed eight hours per day under specific conditions related to lay-days, provided such work does not exceed 12 hours per day or 45 lay-days, with special permissions available in exceptional circumstances.
