This regulation declares an amnesty period from the date of issuance to October 30, 2026, allowing certain individuals and businesses to legally possess, transport, store, or destroy specified prohibited firearms without criminal penalties, provided they meet specific conditions related to licensing, ownership, and use. The amnesty applies to those who previously held or were in possession of such firearms, including those who transferred, stored, repaired, or intended to deactivate or destroy them, with specific rules governing transportation and storage.
