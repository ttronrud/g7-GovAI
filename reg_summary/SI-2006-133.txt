This regulation reaffirms the status of a list of terrorist entities established in 2006, confirming that each organization on the list continues to be considered a terrorist entity under Canadian law due to reasonable grounds to believe they have engaged in or supported terrorist activities. The list includes well-known groups such as Al-Qaeda, Hamas, the PKK, the LTTE, and others, as recommended by the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness and approved by the Governor in Council.
