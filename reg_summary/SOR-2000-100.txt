These regulations, enacted under the Federal-Provincial Fiscal Arrangements Act, establish rules for recovering overpayments in fiscal equalization and Canada Health and Social Transfer payments from provinces due to methodological revisions in data, with specific amounts, timelines, and caps on recoveries, and they come into force on March 15, 2000. Overpayments are recovered in equal monthly installments over specified periods, with adjustments and deferrals where necessary, and certain provinces are identified with specific overpayment amounts.
