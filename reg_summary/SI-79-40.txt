This proclamation declares the Canada-Italy Social Security Agreement in force effective December 20, 1978, establishing rules for how workers and their dependants in both countries can access social security benefits, including old age, disability, and survivor benefits, based on residence, employment, and contribution periods. It ensures that individuals employed or residing in either country are entitled to benefits under the applicable legislation, with specific provisions for temporary employment, government employees, and the treatment of residence periods for benefit calculations.
