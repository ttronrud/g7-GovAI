This regulation, now repealed, listed specific weapons, components, and toxic chemicals—both under the Chemical Weapons Convention (CWC) Schedules 1A, 1B, 2A, 2B, 3A, and 3B—as prohibited items, including small arms, large-calibre weapons, munitions, and various chemical agents and precursors, with detailed chemical formulas and CAS numbers. It also defined prohibited mixtures containing these substances, with exceptions for certain consumer goods.
