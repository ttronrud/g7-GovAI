This regulation grants temporary remission of customs duties, excise taxes, and goods and services tax on goods imported into Canada for the FIFA Men's U-20 World Cup Canada 2007, including items for athletes, officials, sponsors, suppliers, and media rights holders, with specific conditions on use, distribution, and export or destruction of goods. The remission applies to goods imported between February 1, 2007, and July 22, 2007, and requires a claim submitted within two years of importation.
