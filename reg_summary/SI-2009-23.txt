This regulation establishes the rules for appeals from summary conviction proceedings in Nova Scotia, outlining the timing, filing requirements, and procedures for filing a notice of appeal, including the need for a standard heading, specific content, and a motion to set the hearing date. It also specifies that appeals are governed by the Criminal Code and modified versions of Rule 91 of the Nova Scotia Court of Appeal, with particular attention to notice requirements, transcript submission, and the conduct of the appeal under section 822 of the Criminal Code.
