The Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, transfers the powers, duties, and functions related to the Great Lakes Fishery Commission from the Minister of Fisheries and Oceans to the Minister of Foreign Affairs under the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act. This transfer is made pursuant to section 33 of the Government Organization Act, 1979, and is formalized in the legislation S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 207.
