This regulation establishes controls for the import, export, and transit of controlled nuclear substances, equipment, and information in Canada, defining what constitutes controlled items and outlining licensing requirements, exemptions, and reporting obligations under the Nuclear Safety and Control Act. It specifies detailed categories of controlled materials and equipment, including special fissionable materials, source materials, and nuclear reactor components, and provides exemptions for certain low-risk activities, with specific reporting requirements for exports involving certain nuclear substances.
