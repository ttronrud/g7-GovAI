These Regulations establish the Assistance Fund (W.V.A. and C.W.A.) and outline the structure, responsibilities, and operations of the Assistance Fund Committee and District Authorities, including the allocation of funds, coordination of services, and eligibility criteria for financial assistance to war veterans and civilians in Canada. Financial assistance of up to $1,000 per calendar year may be granted to eligible residents facing emergencies or financial distress, with the District Authority having discretion to manage payments and refer applicants for counselling.
