This proclamation certifies that the listed countries and their territories are High Contracting Parties to the 1929 Warsaw Convention on international air carriage, the 1955 Hague Protocol amending it, and certain states have also adopted the Additional Protocol, which modifies Article 2 of the Convention to exclude specific provisions from applying to domestic or direct air carriage. It outlines the dates when these agreements came into force for each party, with some dates unspecified.
