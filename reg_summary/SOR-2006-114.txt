These regulations establish the employment rules for specific positions in the Office of the Governor General’s Secretary, excluding certain provisions of the Public Service Employment Act, and provide special rules regarding appointments, dismissal, layoff, and continued eligibility for employment processes and complaints after leaving the position. The regulations come into force upon registration and apply to positions designated 00025450 to 00025460, with key protections including at least one day’s notice before dismissal, dismissal only for cause, and a one-year right to participate in appointments and file complaints after three years of service.
