These regulations establish the criteria and requirements for reimbursing eligible expenditures incurred by donors of sperm or ova, individuals maintaining or transporting in vitro embryos, and surrogate mothers under the Assisted Human Reproduction Act, including travel, medical services, and loss of income, with specific documentation and record-keeping requirements. Reimbursements are subject to limits, documentation, and a six-year record-keeping period, and the regulations come into force on the date section 12 of the Act becomes effective.
