This regulation establishes a fee remission for certain Canadian mission workers and their spouses or common-law partners from Afghanistan who faced extraordinary risks or suffered injuries due to work in Kandahar province after October 9, 2007, or who are partners of those who died in the line of duty. Remission is granted if the applicant meets specific criteria, including a confirmed employment relationship, submission of an application to the Joint Referral Committee by December 31, 2011, and approval of their immigration application.
