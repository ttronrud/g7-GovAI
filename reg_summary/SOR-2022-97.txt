The Government of Canada has issued a regulation excluding a portion of the Cerulean Warbler's critical habitat located on federal lands within Gatineau Park from the designated critical habitat due to its location in a place specified under subsection 58(2) of the Species at Risk Act. This Order, made under subsections 58(4) and (5) of the Act, comes into force upon registration and reflects the species' endangered status and the inclusion of its critical habitat in the Species at Risk Public Registry.
