This regulation establishes the procedural rules for applications for leave, judicial reviews, and appeals under the Immigration and Citizenship Acts in the Federal Court of Canada, outlining requirements for filing, service, hearing, and decision-making, while exempting certain procedures such as time extensions and costs. It came into force on February 1, 1993, and governs how applicants seek judicial review of administrative decisions, including requirements for documentation, notice, and the handling of sensitive personal information.
