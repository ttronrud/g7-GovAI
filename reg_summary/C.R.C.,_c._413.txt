This regulation outlines the conditions and requirements for foreign fishing vessels to obtain licences to enter Canadian fisheries waters, including permitted activities such as commercial fishing, processing, and transhipment, as well as specific restrictions on species, gear, and areas. It defines key terms, establishes obligations for vessel masters and crews, and sets out rules for compliance with international conservation measures, reporting requirements, and enforcement powers for protection officers. The regulation also specifies licensing conditions, fees, and exceptions for vessels under special agreements or in distress.
