This regulation grants remission of customs duties and partial remission of sales tax on specific defence supplies imported by Oerlikon-Buehrle AG or its agents/subcontractors for use in manufacturing a Low Level Air Defence System, subject to conditions including timely application and certification by the Minister of Supply and Services. The remission applies to imports between April 1, 1986, and April 1, 1995, and is designed to support defence production under a contract with the Department of National Defence.
