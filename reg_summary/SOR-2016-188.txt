These regulations limit the amount of certain phthalates in vinyl used in toys and child care articles, prohibiting levels exceeding 1,000 mg/kg of specific phthalates (DEHP, DBP, BBP) in toys and child care products, and stricter limits (1,000 mg/kg of DINP, DIDP, or DNOP) for parts that can be placed in a child's mouth. The rules apply to products intended for children under four years of age and come into force upon registration.
