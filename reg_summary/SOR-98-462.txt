This regulation designates specific firearms, weapons, components, and ammunition as prohibited or restricted under the Criminal Code, based on their design or model, with particular emphasis on semi-automatic weapons and those resembling military or assault-type firearms. It comes into force on December 1, 1998, and includes a comprehensive list of prohibited and restricted firearms, such as the AK-47, UZI, and various semi-automatic rifles and pistols, with some exceptions for specific models.
