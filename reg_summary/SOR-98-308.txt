These regulations, enacted on May 1, 1998, govern the transition of Public Service employees who move from Theratronics International Limited to a new employer under a government agreement, including provisions on eligibility for pension options, death benefits, allowances, and pensionable service, while excluding certain re-employed individuals or those who made prior elections. They ensure continuity of benefits and define key terms for employees who cease public service employment and begin work with a new employer.
