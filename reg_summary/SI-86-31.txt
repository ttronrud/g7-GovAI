This regulation grants a remission of customs duties and a portion of sales and excise taxes on computer equipment imported by Canadian companies for use in systems software development contracts, provided certain conditions are met, including timely export or destruction of equipment, export of developed software (or retention of one copy for support), and compliance with reporting requirements. The order applies to equipment imported on or after January 1, 1984, and requires claims for remission to be made within three years of importation.
