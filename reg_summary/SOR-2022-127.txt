This regulation grants customs duty remission for goods originating in Ukraine, excluding those subject to the General Tariff or listed in a schedule, provided the goods undergo a last production process in Ukraine (excluding minimal operations such as packaging or preservation). Remission applies to imports between June 9, 2022, and June 9, 2026 (for customs duties) or June 9, 2022, and June 10, 2025 (for Special Import Measures), with claims due within two years of importation, and the order expires on June 9, 2028.
