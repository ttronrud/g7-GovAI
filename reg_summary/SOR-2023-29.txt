This regulatory order establishes specific criteria that must be met for individuals to apply for expungement of certain historical convictions related to bawdy-houses, miscarriage, and drug procurement, emphasizing that consensual sexual activity, absence of monetary exchange, and consent or request from the individual involved are key factors. It applies only to offences listed in the Schedule to the Expungement of Historically Unjust Convictions Act and comes into force upon registration.
