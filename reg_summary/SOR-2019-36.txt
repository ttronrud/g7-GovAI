This regulation grants remission of the surtax paid on certain steel goods imported from Mexico, specifically those in classes 3 and 7 of the schedule, provided they were imported between October 25, 2018, and the Order's coming into force, and the importer meets specific conditions including having a valid import permit and submitting a timely, supported claim. The Order comes into force on February 2, 2019, or on the date of registration if it is registered later.
