This regulation establishes electronic filing requirements and specific reporting rules for builders involved in qualifying housing supplies under the Excise Tax Act, particularly focusing on specified housing supplies valued at $450,000 or more, with defined prescribed amounts and penalties for non-compliance, including transitional rebates and reporting obligations tied to key dates such as March 30, May 3, and June 16, 2016. It also outlines penalty structures and special rules for reporting tax in different provinces, especially in relation to changes in tax rates and applicability of HST.
