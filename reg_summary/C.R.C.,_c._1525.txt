These regulations govern the leasing, purchasing, and management of unassigned territorial lands in the Northwest Territories, specifying requirements for applications, surveys, sales, leases, and property rights, including mineral, timber, and right-of-way provisions, as well as rules for lease renewals, rental payments, and disposal of abandoned structures. They also establish fees, reservations of Crown rights, and restrictions on land use, particularly for grazing and hay cutting by residents.  

*Note: This regulation has been repealed by SOR/2003-116, section 13.*
