This regulation grants privileges and immunities to participants, officials, and experts of the United Nations and its agencies attending the Fourth Session of the Intergovernmental Negotiating Committee on Plastic Pollution (INC-4) in Ottawa from April 23 to 30, 2024, ensuring the inviolability of UN Environment Programme premises and documents during the relevant period. The protections are based on the Convention on the Privileges and Immunities of the United Nations and apply to representatives of foreign states, UN members, specialized agencies, and UN experts performing missions related to the meetings.
