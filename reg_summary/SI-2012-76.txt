This regulation designates a strip of land, up to 100 feet wide along the shoreline of Hecla and Griper Bay and any adjacent unnamed waterbodies, as part of a land grant in the Northwest Territories, specifically within Lot 1000, Quad 79B/06, as recorded in the Canada Lands Surveys. The order was issued under section 13a of the Territorial Lands Act, based on recommendations from the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development.
