This regulation grants Alaska Marine Lines, Inc. a 97% remission of customs duties paid or payable on the temporary importation of vessels engaged in coasting trade, for up to 15 months, provided the vessels operate under a coasting trade licence and are imported into Canadian waters between October 1, 1996, and December 31, 1997. The remission is subject to specific conditions, including timely claim submission and proof of entitlement, and the order comes into force on June 11, 1998.
