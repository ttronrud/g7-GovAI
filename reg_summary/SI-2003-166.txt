This regulation grants a remission to eligible registrants of any tax collected or collectible under Division II of Part IX of the Excise Tax Act for eligible supplies made between January 1, 1991, and April 23, 1996, provided the registrant made an election under section 227 of the Act during that period. The remission is subject to conditions related to unpaid net tax, lack of prior assessment, and timely application, and also includes remission of interest and penalties associated with the tax.
