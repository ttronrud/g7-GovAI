These regulations establish the interest rates applicable to the Air Travellers Security Charge, based on the average yield of Government of Canada Treasury bills, with different rates applied depending on whether the interest is paid to the Receiver General, to individuals, or to corporations, and they came into force on April 1, 2002. After March 2007, interest rates are set as the basic rate plus 4% (for the Receiver General), plus 2% (for individuals), or just the basic rate (for corporations).
