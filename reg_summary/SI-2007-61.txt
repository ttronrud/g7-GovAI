This regulation grants rent remission to lessees of public land within the 12.39 ha seasonal campground at Waskesiu in Prince Albert National Park, reducing their leasehold rent by varying percentages (75%, 50%, and 25%) for periods starting in 2007, 2008, and 2009, respectively, for amounts exceeding $300.00 per period. The remission is authorized under the Financial Administration Act and applies to leasehold interests governed by the National Parks of Canada Lease and Licence of Occupation Regulations.
