The Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Defence and the Treasury Board, remits a total of $147,487,513 including interest to beneficiaries of unauthorized payments made to Canadian Forces members between 2001 and 2011, covering various expenses such as separation costs, lodging, travel, and family assistance, where payments exceeded authorized amounts or were improperly made. This remission applies to eligible individuals and their dependents, with accounting officers responsible for remitting the unrecovered amounts.
