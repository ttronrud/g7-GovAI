These regulations designate specific persons and organizations as prescribed under sections 30 and 37 of the Veterans Review and Appeal Board Act, including the Army, Navy, and Air Force Veterans in Canada, the Bureau of Pensions Advocates, the Merchant Navy Coalition for Equality, the Minister of Veterans Affairs, the National Council of Veteran Associations in Canada, and the Royal Canadian Legion. The regulations are known as the Prescribed Persons and Organizations Regulations, S.C. 1995, c. 18.
