These regulations establish the requirements and procedures for issuing authorizations that allow certain works, undertakings, or activities to proceed without harming fish or their habitats, including detailed application and review processes, timelines, and conditions for amendments, suspensions, or cancellations of authorizations. They emphasize environmental protection by requiring comprehensive assessments of fish and habitat impacts, mitigation measures, monitoring plans, and offsetting strategies, with special provisions for emergencies and national security situations.
