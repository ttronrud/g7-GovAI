The Nunavut Mining Regulations, enacted under the Territorial Lands Act and in the public interest, establish the framework for prospecting, claim recording, mining activities, and royalty obligations in Nunavut, defining key terms, setting application and licensing requirements, and restricting activities on certain lands. These regulations apply to the Nunavut Mining District and govern the rights, responsibilities, and procedures for individuals and corporations seeking to prospect, record claims, and operate mines, with provisions for transparency, environmental baseline studies, and public access to records.
