These regulations establish the requirements for operating a duty-free shop in Canada, including eligibility criteria for applicants (such as being a Canadian citizen or permanent resident with sufficient residency and good character), licensing conditions, security requirements, and restrictions on the sale and transfer of goods, particularly excisable products and intoxicating liquor. The regulations also outline procedures for licence renewal, suspension, cancellation, and the mandatory provision of bilingual notices regarding export requirements and duty exemptions.
