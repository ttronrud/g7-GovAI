These regulations restrict the manufacture, import, sale, and use of defoamers containing dibenzofuran or dibenzo-para-dioxin in pulp and paper mills using chlorine bleaching processes, limiting concentrations to 40 ppb for dibenzofuran and 10 ppb for dibenzo-para-dioxin, and prohibit the use of wood chips treated with polychlorinated phenols, while requiring detailed reporting and record-keeping by manufacturers, importers, and mill operators.
