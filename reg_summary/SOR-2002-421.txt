These regulations adapt the Employment Equity Act to the Canadian Forces, establishing specific definitions, reporting requirements, and employment equity obligations tailored to the military’s operational needs, while ensuring that recruitment, promotion, and workforce analysis account for underrepresentation of Aboriginal peoples, visible minorities, and persons with disabilities—without compromising operational effectiveness. The Chief of the Defence Staff is responsible for conducting workforce surveys, analyzing representation, reviewing employment systems, and preparing an employment equity plan, all while protecting the privacy and security of personnel information.
