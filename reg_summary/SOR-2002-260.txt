This regulation grants a customs duty remission for certain chloroprene sheets with specific characteristics—those laminated with knitted nylon fabric, weighing over 1500g/m² and containing less than 50% textile material—imported into Canada between January 1, 1998, and December 31, 2005, provided a claim is made to the Minister of National Revenue within four years of import. The remission is subject to the conditions outlined in the order.
