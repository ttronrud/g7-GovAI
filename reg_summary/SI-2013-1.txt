This regulation grants a remission of income tax, along with related interest and penalties, to eligible taxpayers in British Columbia for taxation years when the Subtrust paid an eligible amount attributable to an FRT Contractor Mitigation Amount. The remission applies to tax paid or payable under Part I of the Income Tax Act for those years and is based on the terms of the 2011 Contractor Mitigation Account Subtrust established on January 3, 2012.
