The Governor in Council has issued the Form of Proxy (Banks and Bank Holding Companies) Regulations, 2023, which mandates that management and dissident proxy circulars for banks and bank holding companies comply with specific formatting and content requirements under National Instrument 51-102, including disclosures about board approvals, insurance, indemnifications, and financial statements, while exempting certain information for non-distributing banks or those under specific exemptions. These regulations come into force upon registration, with certain provisions activating on July 11, 2024.
