This regulatory order authorizes Petro-Canada and its wholly-owned subsidiaries to undertake specific transactions in 1990, including the formation of new corporations, acquisitions, asset purchases, and disposals, all subject to a cap of 0.5 per cent of Petro-Canada’s consolidated gross assets as of the end of its 1989 financial year. The transactions must be for the purpose of carrying out Petro-Canada’s objectives and must not exceed the specified asset threshold.
