These regulations authorize the Minister of Regional Industrial Expansion to provide an interest-free loan of up to $110 million to General Motors of Canada Limited for the modernization and expansion of its Sainte-Thérèse facility, with a requirement for a $37.5 million partial early repayment if GM fails to guarantee continued operations by December 31, 1990. The loan is due on April 1, 2017, and GM must submit quarterly progress reports on expenditures related to the Renaissance Project.
