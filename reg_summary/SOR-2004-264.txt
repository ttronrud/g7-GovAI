These rules establish a detailed procedural framework for applications to reduce the number of years of imprisonment in New Brunswick without eligibility for parole, outlining requirements for filing, service, parole eligibility reports, hearings, and evidence presentation, all under the authority of subsection 745.64(1)a of the Criminal Code. The process includes a pre-hearing conference, a formal hearing before a judge, and specific rules on evidence, jury empanelling, and judicial discretion.
