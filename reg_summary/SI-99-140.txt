The Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Fisheries and Oceans and the Treasury Board, remits to certain commercial salmon fishing licence holders the fees they paid in 1999 under the Pacific Fishery Regulations, 1993, specifically for Category A licences that allow salmon fishing by trolling in Areas F, G, or H, or by seine in Area B or by gillnet or gillnet in Areas D or E, under specified conditions. This action is taken in the public interest pursuant to the Financial Administration Act.
