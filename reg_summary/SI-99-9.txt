This regulation expands a land grant to include portions of two parcels near Fry Inlet at Contwoyto Lake that lie within 30.48 metres (100 feet) of the ordinary high water mark, as identified on a sketch plan filed in the Department of Indian Affairs and Northern Development. The affected parcels are Lot 1000 in Quad 76E/07 and Lot 1000 in Quad 76E/08, located on the east and west sides of Fry Inlet.
