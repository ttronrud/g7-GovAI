The Governor General, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, the Cree Regional Authority, and the Naskapi Band of Quebec, designates the City of Ottawa, Ontario, as the head office location for the Cree-Naskapi Commission under section 161 of the Cree-Naskapi (of Quebec) Act. This designation is set out in S.C. 1984, c. 18.
