These regulations define "prescribed deposits" under the Cooperative Credit Associations Act, specifying that deposits of less than $150,000 from certain entities—such as governments, international agencies, financial institutions, or entities meeting specific financial thresholds—qualify as prescribed deposits, and establish rules for how such deposits are treated, particularly in relation to lending, foreign exchange, or securities activities. The regulations come into force on the date section 160 of S.C. 2007, c. 6 comes into force or upon registration if later.
