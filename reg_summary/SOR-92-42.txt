This regulation establishes the Tax Court of Canada Rules of Procedure Respecting the Excise Tax Act (Informal Procedure), which governs appeals under Part IX of the Excise Tax Act, outlining procedures for filing notices of appeal, serving documents, expert witness requirements, cost taxation, and the rights of parties, including the ability to extend time for filing objections or appeals under specific circumstances. The rules are effective upon registration and apply to informal procedures, with provisions for electronic filing, judgment and order issuance, and cost awards, while allowing the Court discretion to address irregularities or exceptional cases.
