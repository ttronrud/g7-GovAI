This regulation designates specific parcels of public land as part of various national wildlife areas in Canada, assigning their administration, management, and control to the Minister of the Environment under the Canada Wildlife Act, based on their importance for wildlife research, conservation, and interpretation. It includes detailed descriptions of land parcels for areas such as Wallace Bay, Shepody, Mary’s Point, New Horton, Cape Jourimain, Baie de l’Isle-Verte, and Meanook, with specific exclusions and measurements.
