This proclamation officially notifies the public that the Canada-Romania Convention for the Avoidance of Double Taxation and the Prevention of Fiscal Evasion on Income and Capital came into force on December 31, 2004, and that a specific provision in the convention ceased to apply as of that date, in accordance with Article 29. The convention establishes rules for taxing income and capital, defines residency and permanent establishments, and prevents double taxation between Canada and Romania.
