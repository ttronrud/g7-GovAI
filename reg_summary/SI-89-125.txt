This regulation approves the Public Service Commission's exclusion of certain air traffic control training and operational positions in the Department of Transport from key provisions of the Public Service Employment Act—specifically section 10 and subsection 21(1)—for the period May 1, 1989, to April 30, 1991, to facilitate appointments under the Selection by Seniority and Transfer Down Programs without the usual requirements for qualifications (other than language skills) or the right to appeal.
