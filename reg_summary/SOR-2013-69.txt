This regulation establishes the framework for the approval of water use and waste deposition without a licence in Nunavut, requiring applications with detailed information, adherence to environmental safeguards, and site restoration. It also outlines licensing criteria, fees, reporting obligations, and the creation of water management areas, all under the authority of the Nunavut Water Board and in accordance with the Nunavut Waters and Nunavut Surface Rights Tribunal Act and the Nunavut Land Claims Agreement Act.
