These regulations adjust the indexing ratios for excise duties on spirits, beer, tobacco, cigars, and cigarettes by multiplying the base ratio by specific factors—1.35559 and 1.99924 for September 1, 1983, and 1.37078 and 1.88464 for September 1, 1984—respectively, to reflect changes in cost or inflation. The adjustments apply to duties listed in Parts I, III, and IV of the Excise Act schedule, excluding certain specified provisions.
