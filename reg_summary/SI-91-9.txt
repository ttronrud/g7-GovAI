This regulation revokes an old remission order and establishes a new one granting tax and duty remission for goods, real property, or services used in joint Canada-United States Government projects, subject to specific conditions including exclusive use, proper disposition of goods, and timely claims. The remission applies to taxes under Parts III, IV, and IX of the Excise Tax Act and duties on imported or purchased goods, services, or supplies, with claims required to be submitted within two years of the relevant event.
