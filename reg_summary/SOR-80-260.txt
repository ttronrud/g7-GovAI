This 1980 Order, issued under the Plant Quarantine Act, establishes a quarantine to prevent the spread of the Golden Nematode in a defined area of Central Saanich, British Columbia, prohibiting the movement of plants, soil, and related materials that could carry the pest. Exceptions are made for certain uncontaminated plants, processed products, and soil samples used for research, with specific rules for machinery and vehicles based on the land category they have been used on.
