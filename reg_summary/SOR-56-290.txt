This proclamation declares a specific tract of land in Saskatchewan as a penitentiary under the Penitentiary Act, to be known as the Saskatchewan Penitentiary, effective from July 19, 1956, and includes detailed descriptions of five parcels and a road allowance totaling approximately 1,784 acres. The declaration is issued by the Governor General of Canada on the advice of the Privy Council, establishing the land's status for use by the Canadian penitentiary system.
