These regulations establish detailed requirements for the reporting and record-keeping of goods imported or exported for specific purposes under the Proceeds of Crime (Money Laundering) and Terrorist Financing Act, including mandatory retention of records for up to six years and penalties for non-compliance. They also outline procedures for issuing retention notices, enforcing violations through penalties, and appealing decisions, with penalties ranging from $150 to $500 for minor violations or the greater of the goods' value, declared value, or transaction value in more serious cases.
