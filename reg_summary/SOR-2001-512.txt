This regulation establishes definitions and provisions under the Canada Business Corporations Act, including criteria for what constitutes a "distributing corporation" and a "going-private transaction," defines "participating securities," outlines requirements for electronic notices and consents, specifies conditions under which certain corporate names are prohibited, and sets out prescribed periods and circumstances for various corporate filings and exemptions. It also clarifies rules regarding the use of names that may confuse with trademarks, trade names, or government-related designations.
