This regulation establishes that the Canada-Estonia Agreement on Social Security enters into force on November 1, 2006, enabling reciprocal recognition of social security benefits, creditable periods, and residency rules for citizens and residents of both countries. It ensures equal rights and obligations, allows for the totalization of pensionable periods, and provides for cooperation between the competent authorities to administer the agreement, including the exchange of information and mutual assistance in determining eligibility and benefit amounts.
