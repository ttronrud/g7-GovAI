This regulatory order remits fees for permanent residency, certificate of citizenship, and temporary passport applications for individuals who were in Afghanistan or en route to Canada before August 2021 and urgently needed to travel due to the fall of Kabul, with applications made between August 25, 2021, and December 31, 2023. The remission applies to those who were Canadian citizens or permanent residents and whose travel was facilitated by Canada as a result of the crisis.
