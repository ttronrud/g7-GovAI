This joint notice, issued on April 11, 2016, prohibits exploration, drilling, production, and transportation of petroleum in a specified portion of Georges Bank from April 15, 2016, through December 31, 2022, pursuant to the Canada-Nova Scotia Offshore Petroleum Resources Accord Implementation Act. The prohibition period begins on the date the notice comes into force, which is April 15, 2016, or the date of registration if issued later.
