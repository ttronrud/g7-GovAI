These regulations establish the procedures for conducting votes by First Nations under the First Nations Oil and Gas and Moneys Management Act, including voter eligibility, notice requirements, mail-in ballot processes, information meetings with legal and financial advisers, and voting safeguards to ensure fairness, transparency, and accessibility. They apply to votes on ratifying oil and gas or financial codes and approving transfers of management or payments, with strict requirements for voter lists, ballot administration, and record-keeping.
