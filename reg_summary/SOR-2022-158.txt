This regulatory order brings into effect four individual agreements between Canada and specific First Nations in British Columbia, granting those bands jurisdiction over education, as provided under the First Nations Jurisdiction over Education in British Columbia Act. The agreements, signed on May 25 and 26, 2022, with the ?aq’am Community, Cowichan Tribes, Lil’wat Nation, and Seabird Island Band, come into force on July 1, 2022, or on the date of registration if later.
