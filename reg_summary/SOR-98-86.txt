This regulation grants customs duty remissions to Canadian producers of finished outerwear fabrics for greige fabrics imported during specific periods between 1998 and 2012, with remission rates decreasing over time (from full remission to 50% in later years), subject to conditions including timely claims and annual caps based on 1995 remittances. The order applies to specific types of outerwear fabrics and is effective from December 29, 1997.
