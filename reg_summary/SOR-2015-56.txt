These regulations govern the treatment of former employees of Atomic Energy of Canada Limited (CANDU Reactor Division) who transitioned to Candu Energy Inc. under a 2011 agreement, specifying how their public service superannuation rights are applied, including pensionable service, death benefits, and the right to return contributions or exercise options, all effective from October 2, 2014. They ensure these individuals are treated as Group 1 contributors and are entitled to specific benefits as if they had remained in public service, with certain exceptions and time limits.
