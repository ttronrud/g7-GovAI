This regulation grants the Organisation internationale de la Francophonie (Agency) and the Institut de l’énergie et de l’environnement de la Francophonie (Institute) specific privileges and immunities in Canada, including immunities from legal process, tax exemptions, and diplomatic privileges for their officials and representatives, as outlined in the United Nations Convention on Privileges and Immunities and their agreement with Canada. These privileges are subject to certain conditions, such as local authorities' right to enter premises in case of fire and the non-recognition of periods of presence as residence for tax purposes.
