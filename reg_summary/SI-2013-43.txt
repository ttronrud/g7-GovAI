On April 1, 2013, the Government of Canada transferred the control and supervision of the End User Devices Procurement Unit within the Acquisitions Branch of the Department of Public Works and Government Services to Shared Services Canada, pursuant to the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act. This transfer was made under the authority of section 207 of the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act and the Public Service Act (R.S., c. P-34).
