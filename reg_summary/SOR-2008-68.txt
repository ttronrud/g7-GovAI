These regulations allow a trust or loan company, affiliated with a member institution of the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation, to operate on shared premises in Canada, provided that when a customer requests to open a deposit account in person, the company opens the account in a separate and distinct workspace from where the member institution conducts business. The regulations came into force on the date section 358 of the Statutes of Canada, 2007, came into force, or on the registration date if registered later.
