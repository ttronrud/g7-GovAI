This regulation establishes export permit fee structures for softwood lumber products exported to the United States under the Softwood Lumber Agreement, with fees varying based on export volume, production levels, and market prices, and includes provisions for exemptions, inflation adjustments, and fee remissions under certain conditions. Fees range from $9 to $14 for basic permits and increase to US$50 or US$100 per thousand board feet depending on export levels, with special provisions for British Columbia producers and price-based fee waivers.
