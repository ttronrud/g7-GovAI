This regulation grants customs duty and excise tax remissions to individuals and organizations involved in the 13th IAAF World Junior Championships in Athletics held in Moncton, Canada, in 2010, including athletes, officials, sponsors, and suppliers, for goods imported temporarily or for specific purposes such as competition, display, distribution, or donation, with strict conditions on import dates, usage, and export or destruction of goods. The remissions apply only to goods imported between March 1, 2010, and July 25, 2010, and must be claimed within two years of importation.
