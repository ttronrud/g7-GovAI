This regulation establishes the rules for determining where a supply of goods or services is deemed to occur for harmonized value-added tax purposes under the Excise Tax Act, particularly focusing on intangible personal property, services, and passenger transportation. It defines key factors such as the Canadian element, location of the recipient, and the use of Canadian rights to determine tax jurisdiction, with specific rules for participating and non-participating provinces, and includes provisions for tiebreakers when tax rates are equal.
