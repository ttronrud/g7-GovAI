These regulations establish the procedures for verifying the origin of goods eligible for preferential tariff treatment under the Canada-Costa Rica Free Trade Agreement (CCRFTA), including requirements for verification visits, questionnaires, letters, record-keeping, and the conditions under which preferential treatment may be denied or withdrawn. The regulations came into force on November 1, 2002, and outline the rights and obligations of exporters, producers, and customs authorities in conducting and responding to origin verifications.
