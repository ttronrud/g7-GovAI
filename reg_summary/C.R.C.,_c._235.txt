This regulation establishes the authority of the Prince Edward Island Tobacco Commodity Marketing Board to impose and collect levies or charges on tobacco producers and marketers within the province for interprovincial and export trade, classify producers into groups, and use the collected funds for reserves, expenses, losses, and equalizing revenues among producers. The Order is part of the Prince Edward Island Natural Products Marketing Act and governs the marketing and financial management of unmanufactured flue-cured tobacco produced in the province.
