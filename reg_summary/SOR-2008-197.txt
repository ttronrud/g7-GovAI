This regulation establishes environmental standards for the storage and operation of petroleum and allied petroleum products in Canada, prohibiting releases into the environment and requiring the permanent removal and replacement of single-walled underground and partially buried tanks installed before June 12, 2008. It mandates compliance with the CCME Code of Practice, including requirements for secondary containment, leak detection, corrosion protection, and system design, with specific obligations for owners and operators to prevent pollution and ensure safe, compliant storage.
