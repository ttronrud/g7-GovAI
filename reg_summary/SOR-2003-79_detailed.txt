### 2. **Dry-Cleaning Machine Requirements**
To legally use tetrachloroethylene for dry cleaning, a machine must meet **all of the following criteria**:

## ğŸšš Waste Management Requirements

### 1. **Wastewater Handling**
The owner/operator must either:
- **Transport wastewater** to a **waste management facility at least once every 12 months**, OR
- **Treat wastewater on-site** using a full treatment system that includes:
  - A **second tetrachloroethylene-water separator** to recover solvent.
  - An **initial activated carbon filter** to remove tetrachloroethylene.
  - A **monitor-alarm system** that automatically shuts down the system when the initial filter becomes saturated.
  - A **second activated carbon filter** to further purify the water after the first filter.

> âœ… When transporting wastewater, **all wastewater present in the facility at the time of transport** must be moved â€” even if not yet treated.

### 2. **Residue Handling**
- All **residue** must be transported to a **waste management facility at least once every 12 months**.
- All residue present in the facility at the time of transport must be removed.

## ğŸ“ Reporting and Recordkeeping Obligations

All persons involved in the use, import, recycling, or sale of tetrachloroethylene must maintain records and submit annual reports to the **Minister of the Environment** by **April 30 of the following calendar year**.

---

### 1. **Importers**
- Must maintain records and submit a report including:
  - Business contact details (name, address, email, phone, fax)
  - Authorized representative details
  - **Confidentiality request** (if applicable under section 313 of CEPA)
  - Quantity of tetrachloroethylene imported annually:
    - Total imported
    - For own use in dry cleaning
    - For dry cleaning (including own use)
    - For degreasing
    - As chemical feedstock
    - For other uses (with identification)

### 2. **Recyclers**

  - Business contact details
  - Authorized representative details

    - For reuse in dry cleaning
    - For reuse in degreasing
