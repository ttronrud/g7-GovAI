This regulation designates specific public lands in Québec and Saskatchewan as part of national wildlife areas for wildlife research, conservation, and interpretation, and assigns administrative, management, and control of these lands to the Minister of the Environment under the Canada Wildlife Act. In Québec, it includes several islands and lots in the St. Lawrence River and the parish of Contrecoeur, as well as Mousseau Island; in Saskatchewan, it covers a designated parcel in township 12, range 9, west of the second meridian.
