These regulations establish the terms and conditions for guaranteed business improvement loans to small businesses in Canada, effective April 1, 1993, defining loan types (land, equipment, premises, and fee loans), eligibility criteria, interest rates, repayment terms, security requirements, and loss recovery procedures, with specific provisions for certain industries and loan renewals or modifications. They also outline the lender’s obligations, including the requirement for promissory notes, appraisals in cases of non-arm’s-length transactions, and reporting to the Minister.
