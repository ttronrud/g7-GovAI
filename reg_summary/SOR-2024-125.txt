The Governor General, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, grants a remission to Gestion C.T.M.A. Inc. for the difference in customs duties owed under the General Tariff versus the Most-Favoured-Nation Tariff for the vessel CTMA Ranger imported in 2022, subject to specific conditions including filing required evidence and making a claim within two years of the Order's coming into force. The Order comes into force upon registration.
