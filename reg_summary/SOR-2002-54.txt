These regulations establish the procedures for notices, requests, and communications between parties in labour disputes under the Canada Labour Code, including requirements for notices to commence collective bargaining, notices of dispute, strike or lockout, and requests for arbitration, all of which must be in writing and properly served. They also designate the Federal Mediation and Conciliation Service (FMCS) and its officials as authorized representatives of the Minister for certain actions, and specify how notices and reports are to be delivered and filed.
