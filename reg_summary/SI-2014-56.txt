This regulation announces the entry into force of a 2010 protocol amending the Canada-France double taxation avoidance convention, effective December 27, 2013. It expands information exchange obligations between Canada and France on income and capital taxes, clarifies that such information must be treated as confidential and used only for tax administration and enforcement purposes, and extends the convention’s application to French overseas territories, with provisions taking effect for tax years beginning on or after January 1 of the year following the protocol’s entry into force.
