This regulation establishes a detailed tariff of fees, costs, allowances, and expenses for individuals employed in the conduct of a federal referendum under the Referendum Act, effective July 1, 1992. It specifies remuneration and expense allowances based on electoral district population, types of roles (e.g., returning officers, enumerators, poll clerks), and services performed, including travel, living expenses, and special assignments, with adjustments allowed for special circumstances or additional needs.
