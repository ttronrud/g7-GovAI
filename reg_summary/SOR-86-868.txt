These regulations establish the procedures for electing and appointing an Inuk of Fort George as an observer for the Chisasibi Band, including nomination, voting, eligibility criteria, and the circumstances under which the position becomes vacant. The observer is elected by eligible Inuks of Fort George at a public meeting convened by the Kiggaluk Corporation, with a minimum of 15 voters required for a valid election, and serves until the next general election or until the position becomes vacant under specified conditions.
