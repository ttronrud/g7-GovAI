This regulation imposes a 25% surtax on certain steel and aluminum goods imported into Canada if the steel or aluminum was melted and poured in China or smelted and cast in China, respectively, with exceptions for small-value goods, goods already subject to other surtaxes, and specific categories. The surtax applies unless the importer provides a certificate proving the materials were not produced or processed in China, and the Order comes into force on July 31, 2025.
