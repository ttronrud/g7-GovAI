These regulations amend the Criminal Code to exclude certain loans from the application of section 347 (unlawful interest) under specific conditions, such as business loans over $10,000 up to $500,000 with interest rates up to 48%, or pawnbroking arrangements with limited credit and interest, and establish a maximum total cost of borrowing of 14% for payday loans, excluding certain default-related fees. The regulations come into force on January 1, 2025.
