This regulation orders the seizure of the An-124-100-150 aircraft (registration RA-82078) in Canada, owned or controlled by entities linked to Volga-Dnepr Airlines or the Volga-Dnepr Group, citing Russia's actions as a grave breach of international peace and security. The seizure is authorized under the Special Economic Measures Act, with the Minister of Public Works and Government Services granted exclusive control and management authority over the aircraft.
