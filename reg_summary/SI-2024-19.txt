This regulation grants a fee remission for certain identity and travel document fees to individuals affected by the Port of Beirut explosions, provided they meet specific conditions related to residency in an affected area, loss or damage of their document due to the explosions, and timely application for a replacement document. The remission applies to specific fees under various regulations, including those for passports, citizenship certificates, and permanent resident cards, and is limited to applications made between August 13, 2020, and January 31, 2021.
