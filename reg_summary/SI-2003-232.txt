On December 12, 2003, the Government transferred control and supervision of several divisions within the Chief Information Officer Branch of the Treasury Board Secretariat—包括 GOL Analysis, Gateways and Clusters, GOL Assistant Secretary’s Office, Service Delivery Improvement Division, Secure Channel Client Engagement and Project Management Office, Organizational Readiness Division, and Communications and Public Relations—to the Department of Public Works and Government Services. This transfer was made under the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act, based on recommendations from the Prime Minister and approved by the Governor General in Council.
