This regulation designates certain federal institutions as parts of the public service and treats their employees (who are not otherwise in the public service) as if they were employed in the public service for the purposes of subsection 35(2) of the Public Service Employment Act. The listed organizations include entities such as Export Development Canada, the Royal Canadian Mint, and various government territories.  

*(Note: This regulation has been repealed by SI/2015-48, section 2.)*
