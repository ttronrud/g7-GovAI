This regulation grants a remission of the $75 visitor visa fee for athletes, coaches, medical staff, national team officials, judges, or members of sports federations participating in the 2001 IAAF World Championships in Edmonton or the 2001 Games of La Francophonie, or for performers invited by the organizing committee to take part in a cultural festival associated with those events. The remission is made in the public interest and under the authority of the Financial Administration Act.
