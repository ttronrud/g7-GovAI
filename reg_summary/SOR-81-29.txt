This regulation establishes the criteria and scope of federal property eligible for grants in lieu of real property, frontage, or area taxes under the Payments in Lieu of Taxes Act, including specific categories such as properties leased to employees, members of the Canadian Forces, diplomatic missions, international organizations, and certain government or Indigenous-owned properties. It also amends Schedules III and IV to include specific corporations responsible for managing or occupying federal real property. [Repealed in 2001.]
