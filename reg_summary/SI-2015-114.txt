This regulation establishes detailed procedural rules for the Court of Québec, ensuring the proper administration of justice through standardized practices in pleadings, document formatting, court conduct, and access to records. It applies across all judicial districts in Québec and emphasizes case management, decorum, technology use, and the rights of parties—especially those unrepresented or with disabilities—while maintaining strict rules on record-keeping, hearings, and the handling of sensitive or youth-related cases.
