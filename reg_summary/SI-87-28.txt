The regulation establishes the Criminal Appeal Rules of the Supreme Court of Newfoundland, Trial Division, outlining the procedures for filing a notice of appeal, serving the respondent, obtaining transcripts of trial evidence, submitting statements of facts and legal arguments, and setting the hearing date. It also addresses special circumstances such as guilty pleas, trial de novo applications, abandonment of appeals, and the handling of trial documents, all governed by section 748 of the Criminal Code of Canada.
