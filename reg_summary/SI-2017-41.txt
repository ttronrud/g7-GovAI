This regulationproclaims the entry into force on August 1, 2017, of the Agreement between Canada and France on Social Security and its Implementing Agreement, establishing rules for the mutual recognition of social security rights, including pension and benefit eligibility, insurance periods, and coverage for workers and dependants, with provisions for totalization of periods and protections against reductions in benefits due to residence in the other country. It ensures that nationals of both countries receive equivalent treatment and benefits when working or residing in the other country, while respecting national legislation and specific exclusions.
