This regulatory order exempts certain property, rights, interests, obligations, and liabilities of Her Majesty held or incurred by Eldorado on September 30, 1988, from the application of a specific provision in the Eldorado Nuclear Limited Reorganization and Divestiture Act, excluding only specific shares transferred to successor corporations. The order allows these assets and liabilities to be excluded from reorganization and divestiture provisions effective as of that date.
