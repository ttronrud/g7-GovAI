This regulation authorizes Canadian exporters to export certain nuclear-related dual-use goods and technologies to specific eligible destinations under General Export Permit No. 44 (GEP-44), subject to strict conditions, including compliance with end-use requirements, record-keeping, and reporting obligations. The permit excludes exports to countries on the Area Control List and items listed in specific sections of the Guide, and requires exporters to provide detailed information to Canadian authorities before and after exports.
