This regulation orders the inclusion of certain lands—specifically Lots 1088 to 1115 in Quad 85 J/9, Prosperous Lake—within 30.48 metres (100 feet) of the Ordinary High Water Mark of Prosperous Lake, in the sale of those lands, pursuant to the Territorial Lands Act. The lands are defined by a recorded survey plan and are registered in the Northwest Territories Land Registration District at Yellowknife.
