This regulation approves the exclusion of certain temporary employees in the Department of Public Works—those appointed on or after April 1, 1993 for periods of less than three months totaling less than six months in any 12-month period—from the provisions of the Public Service Employment Act, specifically during the period April 1 to December 31, 1993. It also establishes rules governing the employment of such temporary personnel in specified groups across administrative, operational, technical, and scientific/professional categories.
