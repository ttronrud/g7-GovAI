This regulation authorizes Patricia Jane Knutson, an employee of the Department of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, to acquire an interest in territorial lands in the Northwest Territories through an assignment of lease to her spouse, Dana Benjamin Knutson, covering Lot 1012 in Quad 85 I/12, while reserving all mineral rights, including hydrocarbons. The Order, known as the Government Employees Land Acquisition Order, 1996, No. 1, is made under section 29(1)(a) of the Territorial Lands Act.
