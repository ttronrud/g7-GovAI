This regulation directs the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission (CRTC) to implement paragraphs 1 and 4 of Annex 15-D of the Canada–United States–Mexico Agreement, specifically by listing U.S. home shopping television programming services (including modified versions for Canada) as authorized non-Canadian services and allowing them to negotiate affiliation agreements with distributors. The Direction comes into force on July 1, 2020.
