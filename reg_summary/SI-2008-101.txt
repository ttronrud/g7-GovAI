This regulation withdraws certain tracts of territorial lands in the Northwest Territories from disposal to support the establishment of an expanded Nahanni National Park Reserve, with the withdrawal effective from the date of enactment until October 31, 2010. Specific parcels totaling approximately 28,800 km² and a smaller parcel of about 367 km² are excluded from disposal, except for lands already designated under the Canada National Parks Act or in Schedule 2 of this Order.
