This regulation grants tax remission to the Rainy River First Nations for certain taxes, interest, and penalties related to the supply of additional land or replacement land, as well as associated costs and third-party interests, under specific conditions and timeframes, with the remission limited to the amount of land entitled under the settlement agreement. The remission is subject to written claims, confirmation by the Department of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, and strict eligibility criteria based on the timing and nature of the transactions.
