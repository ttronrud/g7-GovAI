This regulation establishes the requirements for maintaining registers of directors, officers, members, and debt obligation holders for not-for-profit corporations under the Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act, specifying the information to be included, update periods, and procedures for electronic communication. It also outlines various timeframes for reporting, disclosures, and approvals, and defines when a corporate name is prohibited due to confusion with trademarks, trade names, or existing body corporates, with certain exceptions for consent or dissolution.
