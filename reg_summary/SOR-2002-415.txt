This regulation establishes the Mushuau Innu First Nation as a recognized band under the Indian Act, including individuals of Canadian Innu ancestry who were ordinarily resident in Davis Inlet, Newfoundland and Labrador, on November 19, 2002, or were related by blood or adoption to such individuals. The band's territory is defined as Iluikoyak Island, including the village of Davis Inlet, with boundaries based on the ordinary high water mark.
