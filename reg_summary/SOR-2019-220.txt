This regulation establishes the Brokering Control List under the Export and Import Permits Act, identifying specific goods and technologies that may not be brokered if their properties or end-use could lead to their misuse in the development or production of chemical or biological weapons, nuclear or radiological weapons, or missile systems. The list comes into force on the date section 5 of S.C. 2018, c. 26 becomes effective, or upon registration if later.
