The Governor General, on the Prime Minister's recommendation, transferred control of Canada Business Ontario from the Department of Industry to the Federal Economic Development Agency for Southern Ontario, effective June 6, 2013, under the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act. This transfer is authorized by section 207 of the Act (S.C. 2003, c. 22) and section 2(a) of the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act (R.S., c. P-34).
