This regulation establishes safety, construction, and operational requirements for Canadian fishing vessels not exceeding 24.4 meters in length and 150 gross tonnage, including definitions of key terms, mandatory safety equipment, maintenance standards, fuel handling procedures, and safety drills. It emphasizes vessel seaworthiness, crew safety, environmental protection, and compliance with international standards such as SOLAS and the IS Code, with specific provisions for ice conditions, stability, and emergency preparedness.
