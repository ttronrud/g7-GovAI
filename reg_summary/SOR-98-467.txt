This regulation declares an amnesty period from December 1, 1998, to December 31, 2005, under which certain individuals and businesses who possessed prohibited handguns acquired between February 14, 1995, and December 1, 1998, are not criminally liable for possession of those firearms if they complied with specific lawful actions, such as deactivating, exporting, or turning in the weapon, or transferring it to authorized entities. Additionally, a separate amnesty applies to certain firearms businesses that held unregistered firearms for consignment sales, allowing them to lawfully dispose of those firearms by December 31, 2003.
