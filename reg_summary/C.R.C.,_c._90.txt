The Lakehead Airport Zoning Regulations establish height restrictions for buildings and structures near Lakehead Airport in Thunder Bay, Ontario, limiting all constructions to the elevation of specified surfaces, including a horizontal surface, approach surfaces adjacent to runways (12-30 and 7-25), and transitional surfaces, all defined by precise geographic boundaries and elevations. These restrictions apply to all lands adjacent to or in the vicinity of the airport, as detailed in the schedule, with the airport reference point set at 635 feet above sea level.
