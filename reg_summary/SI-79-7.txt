This proclamation declares that an agreement between the United Kingdom and Canada, concerning the adjustment of retirement pensions for individuals who received benefits under British legislation based on time spent in Canada, came into force on October 13, 1978. The agreement, originally proposed in a letter dated November 10, 1977, and accepted by both governments, reduces the weekly rate of certain UK retirement pensions by the equivalent of the Canadian Old Age Security pension, subject to specific conditions and limits.
