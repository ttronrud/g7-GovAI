This regulation establishes reporting requirements for the importation or exportation of currency and monetary instruments into or out of Canada, mandating that certain individuals or entities submit detailed written reports to customs within specific timeframes, with exemptions for specific situations such as travel to or from outside Canada, mail shipments by financial entities, or transactions involving the Bank of Canada. The reports must include specific information about the currency, monetary instruments, travelers, and shipment details, and failure to comply may result in penalties ranging from 5% to 50% of the value of the seized items.
