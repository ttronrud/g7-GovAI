This regulation revokes a previous order and establishes fees that the Commissioner of the Bermuda Police Force must pay to the Royal Canadian Mounted Police for forensic and identification services, including case examinations and member presence for court testimony. Fees are set at $1,000 per forensic examination, $250 per identification examination, and $175 per day (including travel) for a member testifying in court, with additional travel and lodging expenses covered at Government of Canada rates.
