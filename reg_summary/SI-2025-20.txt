The Governor General in Council has approved the exclusion of certain Canadian Forces members who completed three years of service and were honourably released between November 13, 2020, and January 18, 2024, from the application of section 35.11b of the Public Service Employment Act, provided they created a Public Service Resourcing System account by January 18, 2024. This exclusion was determined to be practicable and in the best interests of the public service.
