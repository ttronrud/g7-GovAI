These Regulations establish requirements for notifying the public and relevant authorities of proposed railway works that could impact safety, including construction or alteration of railway lines, bridges, tunnels, road crossings, or structures affecting drainage, with notices to be issued at least 60 days before commencement and containing detailed information such as location, plans, safety impacts, and timelines. The notice must be delivered to municipalities, landowners, railway companies, and road authorities as applicable, and must include a drawing, description of works, and a specified objection period.
