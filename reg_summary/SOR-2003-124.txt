This regulation grants remission of customs duties on specified quantities of refined sugar imported from Costa Rica into Canada, under the terms of the CCRFTA, with the conditions that the goods originate in Costa Rica, were not processed in a Costa Rican free zone, and a claim is made within four years of importation. The remission applies to increasing annual quantities of sugar from 20,000 tonnes in 2003 to 40,000 tonnes in 2010.
