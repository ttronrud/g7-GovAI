The Governor General, on the Prime Minister's recommendation, transfers specific powers and duties from the Minister of Industry, Science and Technology to the Minister of Energy, Mines and Resources to oversee the Hibernia Development Project, including developing and implementing the project through loan agreements with the project owners. This transfer is authorized under the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act and relates to the project as approved by the Canada-Newfoundland Offshore Petroleum Board.
