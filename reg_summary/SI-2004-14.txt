On April 1, 2004, control and supervision of certain public service units within Communication Canada—specifically the Regional Operations Branch (excluding Outreach, Analysis and Information Management), the Information Services (excluding Electronic Media Monitoring Service), and the Communications Support Group—were transferred to the Privy Council Office under the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act. This transfer was made pursuant to the Prime Minister's recommendation and paragraph 2(a) of the Act.
