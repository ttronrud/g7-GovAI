This regulation designates, under the National Housing Act, all rural areas in specified provinces and territories outside incorporated cities, towns, or villages with a population exceeding 5,000 (based on the most recent decennial census) as "designated areas" for specific housing-related purposes. The Order establishes these areas to apply the provisions of subsections 34.121(2), 34.161(2), 34.17(1), and 55(7) of the Act.
