This proclamation, issued by the Administrator of the Government of Canada, requests that the people of Canada observe April 17, 2021, as a day to honour the memory of Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh, following his passing on April 9, 2021. It was published under the authority of the Privy Council for Canada and affixed with the Great Seal of Canada.
