The Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the President of the Treasury Board, grants a remission of the Northern Pipeline Agency Cost Recovery Charge to Foothills Pipe Lines Ltd. for charges and related interest due on invoices issued on September 30, 2004, and December 31, 2004, under the Northern Pipeline Act. This decision is made in the public interest pursuant to section 23(2.1)a of the Financial Administration Act.
