This proclamation declares the Agreement on Social Security between Canada and the Commonwealth of Dominica in force as of January 1, 1989, enabling citizens of both countries to access social security benefits under each other’s legislation, including pensions, disability benefits, and survivors’ benefits, with periods of residence or contributions being totaled for eligibility. The agreement ensures that benefits are payable in the territory of the other country and provides specific rules for calculating and awarding these benefits, while also allowing for mutual recognition and coordination between the respective authorities.
