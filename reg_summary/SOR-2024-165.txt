The Minister of Fisheries and Oceans designates the Tuvaijuittuq Marine Protected Area in the Arctic Ocean off northern Ellesmere Island, in accordance with the Oceans Act and without conflicting with the ratified Inuit land claims agreement, while prohibiting activities that harm marine features or organisms, except for national defence, scientific research, and certain foreign navigation and infrastructure activities. The area includes the seabed, subsoil, and water column below low water, and the Order comes into force upon registration.
