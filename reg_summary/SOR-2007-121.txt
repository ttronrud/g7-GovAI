This regulation establishes the requirements for registering and renewing registration of money services businesses and foreign money services businesses under the Proceeds of Crime (Money Laundering) and Terrorist Financing Act, including detailed information submissions about ownership, directors, activities, and financial relationships, with specific eligibility criteria and record-keeping obligations. It mandates that applicants provide comprehensive identifying and operational information, including personal and corporate details of owners and directors, and requires the retention of relevant documents for five years.
