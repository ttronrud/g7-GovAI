This Act enacts the Eeyou Marine Region Land Claims Agreement, ratifying the 2010 Agreement between the Crees of Eeyou Istchee and the Government of Canada, recognizing their aboriginal rights and title to the Eeyou Marine Region, and establishing the legal framework, governance bodies, and implementation mechanisms for the Agreement, including provisions for review and judicial interpretation. It declares the Agreement a treaty under the Constitution Act, 1982, and gives it supremacy over conflicting legislation.
