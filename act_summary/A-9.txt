This Act establishes the Department of Agriculture and Agri-Food, creating a Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food responsible for all matters related to agriculture, agricultural products, and agricultural research, with authority over inspections and the ability to delegate powers, while also transferring relevant functions from the former Department of Agriculture to the new department. It also ensures continuity for existing employees and reassigns certain powers and duties to the new department, unless otherwise designated by the Governor in Council.
