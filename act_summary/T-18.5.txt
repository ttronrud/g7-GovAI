This Act establishes the Transportation Appeal Tribunal of Canada, a body with jurisdiction to hear reviews and appeals in various transportation sectors, including aviation, railways, marine, and motor vehicles, as well as administrative monetary penalties under relevant federal transportation laws. The Tribunal is composed of appointed members with expertise in transportation, including a Chairperson and Vice-Chairperson, who oversee its operations, and it conducts proceedings with flexibility in procedure, public hearings, and binding decisions, while also ensuring transparency, fairness, and the protection of partiesâ€™ rights.
