The Economic Action Plan 2015 Act enacts a range of fiscal, budgetary, and security-related measures, including the Federal Balanced Budget Act and the Prevention of Terrorist Travel Act, with most provisions coming into force on or after July 1, 2015, and subject to specific transitional or delayed implementation dates. Key provisions, such as those related to budgetary controls and anti-terrorism measures, were activated on specific dates determined by order of the Governor in Council or as noted in statutory instruments.
