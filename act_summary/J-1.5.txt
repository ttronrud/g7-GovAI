This Act establishes the Jules and Paul-Émile Léger Foundation as a non-profit corporation dedicated to humanitarian, educational, religious, and philanthropic causes in Canada and abroad, with a focus on alleviating human suffering and supporting vulnerable populations. The Foundation is authorized to manage its affairs independently, create subsidiaries, invest funds, and operate globally, with all profits reinvested in its mission and its dissolution property transferred to the Roman Catholic Diocese of Montreal for charitable use.
