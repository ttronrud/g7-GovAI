The Resources and Technical Surveys Act establishes the responsibilities of the Minister of Natural Resources (or relevant ministers) to conduct technical surveys, collect and publish mineral and resource data, carry out scientific research, and develop national policies on energy, minerals, water, and other natural resources. It also authorizes the Minister to coordinate research, formulate conservation and development plans, collaborate with provinces, industry, and academic institutions, and provide logistical and financial support for Arctic-related scientific and sovereignty activities.
