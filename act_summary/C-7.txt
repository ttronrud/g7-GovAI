The Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation Act establishes the Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation (CMHC) as a federal corporation tasked with administering housing policies and providing mortgage-related services, including lending, research, and financial management, on behalf of Her Majesty in right of Canada. It outlines the structure, powers, funding, and operational responsibilities of the Corporation, including its ability to borrow, issue securities, enter into financial transactions, and manage real and personal property.
