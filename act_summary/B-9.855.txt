The Budget Implementation Act, 2006 establishes a $650 million payment to provinces for early learning and child care, allocates funds to each province and territory, and modifies interest calculations under the Customs and Excise Acts as if certain sections had taken effect on July 1, 2006. It also transfers environmental responsibilities and administrative control related to the Mackenzie gas project from the Department of the Environment to the Department of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, effective February 16, 2011.
