The Budget Implementation Act, 2018, No. 1 enacts various provisions related to the Canada Pension Plan, greenhouse gas pollution pricing, and the transition of certain government operations, including the transfer of functions from the Canadian Cyber Incident Response Centre and the Security Operations Centre to the Communications Security Establishment, with many provisions coming into force on specific dates, including April 1, 2019, or through orders of the Governor in Council. It also includes transitional provisions and references to the Cannabis Act, with certain sections activating upon royal assent.
