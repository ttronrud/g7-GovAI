This Act enacts the Déline Final Self-Government Agreement as a valid and binding treaty under the Constitution Act, 1982, establishing the legal framework for self-government of the Déline First Nation, including the creation of the Déline Got’ine Government and the supremacy of the Agreement over conflicting federal laws. It confirms the Agreement’s force of law, provides for judicial review, and ensures that the Déline First Nation has full rights and powers under its self-governance framework.
