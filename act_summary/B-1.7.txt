This Act establishes a framework for the sale of the assets of the Bank of British Columbia to a purchasing bank, outlining the conditions, approvals, and shareholder procedures required for such a transaction, while ensuring that the sale proceeds and related obligations are handled in a structured and legally binding manner. It provides for the release of trust funds to the Bank or the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation based on an independent assessment of the Bank’s net value, with finality in the assessor’s determination.
