This Act enables the amalgamation of the Canadian Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors and the Canadian Association of Financial Planners into a single non-profit corporation named The Financial Advisors Association of Canada, which is granted broad powers to promote professionalism, set standards, and advance public understanding of financial planning. The new corporation operates without share capital, assumes all rights, liabilities, and obligations of the original entities, and is governed by a board of directors with specific initial appointments and by-laws.
