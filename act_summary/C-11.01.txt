The Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit Act provides a $300 weekly benefit to eligible workers who lost income due to COVID-19-related lockdown measures between October 24, 2021, and May 7, 2022, including those who experienced a 50% reduction in income or were unable to work due to public health restrictions, with eligibility tied to income thresholds, residency, and compliance with quarantine requirements. The benefit is subject to strict eligibility criteria, including the requirement to file a return of income, and includes provisions for repayment of overpayments and review of decisions.
