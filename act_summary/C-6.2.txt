This Act authorizes Canada Development Investment Corporation and its subsidiary, Cartierville Financial Corporation Inc., to sell their shares in Canadair Limited to Bombardier-Canadair Inc., and to dispose of the resulting shares in Bombardier-Canadair Inc., all under conditions approved by the Governor in Council. It also allows the Minister of Regional Industrial Expansion to acquire 500,000 Class B shares of Bombardier-Canadair Inc. on specified terms, with those shares held in trust for Her Majesty in right of Canada.
