The Fall Economic Statement Implementation Act, 2022 repeals the First Nations Land Management Act and introduces amendments to various regulations, including those related to the Underused Housing Tax and apprentice loans, with key provisions coming into force in 2022 and 2023 to support economic and land management policy changes. It also enables the Minister of Finance to acquire shares in a subsidiary of the Canada Development Investment Corporation for the Canada Growth Fund, with specific funding limits and transitional rules.
