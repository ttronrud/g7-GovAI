The Nunavik Inuit Land Claims Agreement Act enacts and ratifies the Nunavik Inuit Land Claims Agreement, recognizing it as a treaty under the Constitution Act, 1982, and establishes its legal effect, including the rights, responsibilities, and governance structures for the Nunavik Inuit, while ensuring the Agreement prevails over conflicting laws. It also provides for oversight, review, and implementation mechanisms to ensure the Agreement is properly carried out.
