The Canadian Agricultural Loans Act provides that the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food will reimburse lenders up to 95% of losses incurred when they lend to farmers or farm products marketing cooperatives for approved agricultural purposes, such as purchasing equipment, livestock, land, or improving farm structures, subject to strict conditions including loan limits, repayment terms, interest caps, and application requirements. The Act also includes safeguards against excessive lending, limits on total compensation, and provisions for regulatory oversight and adjustments.
