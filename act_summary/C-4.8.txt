This Act implements the Canada–European Union Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement (CETA) by establishing the legal framework for trade, investment, labour, environmental, and intellectual property rights between Canada and the EU, while defining the roles of federal ministers and the CETA Joint Committee. It outlines the responsibilities of the Government of Canada in enforcing the Agreement, managing tribunals and panels, and ensuring compliance with its objectives, including fair competition, sustainable development, and the protection of workers’ and environmental rights.
