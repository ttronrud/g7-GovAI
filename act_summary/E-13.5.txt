This Act approves Canada’s participation in the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) by authorizing the acceptance of the EBRD Agreement, enabling the Governor in Council to manage Canada’s obligations under the Agreement, including appointments, regulations, and financial contributions. It outlines Canada’s financial commitments, reporting requirements, and the EBRD’s mandate to support market-oriented economic reforms, private sector development, and sustainable growth in Central and Eastern European countries, Mongolia, and the Southern and Eastern Mediterranean.
