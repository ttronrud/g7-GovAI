The Tsawwassen First Nation Final Agreement Act enacts and ratifies the Tsawwassen First Nation Final Agreement, a treaty and land claims agreement under Section 25 and 35 of the Constitution Act, 1982, which establishes a new relationship between the Tsawwassen First Nation, the Government of Canada, and the Government of British Columbia, granting the First Nation ownership of certain lands, removing the application of the Indian Act, and providing for the legal validity and enforceability of the agreement and related instruments.
