This Act authorizes the disposal of territorial lands and assets in the Yukon Territory by the Northern Canada Power Commission to the Yukon Power Corporation, including the forgiveness of significant debts owed by the Commission to Her Majesty in right of Canada, and provides for the application of pension laws to certain former Commission employees. It also includes provisions for the assignment of a promissory note, the reporting of forgiven debts, and the continuation of prior orders under the Act.
