The Budget Implementation Act, 2022, No. 1 introduces various amendments to existing Canadian laws, including new regulations on vaping products that come into force on October 1, 2022, and establishes a $750 million funding mechanism for provincial transit and housing needs. It also repeals certain acts, such as the Prohibition on the Purchase of Residential Property by Non-Canadians Act and the Safe Drinking Water for First Nations Act, and includes provisions that take effect on specific dates, including January 1, 2023, or by order of the Governor in Council.
