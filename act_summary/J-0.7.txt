The Jobs and Economic Growth Act establishes rules for the allocation of Input Tax Credits under the GST/HST and sets various implementation dates for its provisions, with many sections coming into force on specific dates, including March 16, 2012, or later, depending on orders from the Governor in Council. It also includes transitional provisions and repeals, with certain sections taking effect on dates such as April 1, 2007, April 15, 2009, or July 1, 2010.
