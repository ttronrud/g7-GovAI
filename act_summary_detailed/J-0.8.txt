### 5. **Establishment and Role of PPP Canada Inc.**

A major component of the Act is the **creation and regulation of PPP Canada Inc.**, a Crown corporation established to facilitate public-private partnerships (PPPs) in government projects.

#### Key Provisions:

- **Purpose of PPP Canada Inc.**:
  - To assess public-private partnership opportunities for federal departments and Crown corporations.
  - To advise on the implementation of PPP projects.
  - To provide expertise and guidance on PPP-related issues.
  - To conduct activities specified in orders made under section 211.1 of the Act.

- **Authority to Act**:
  - The Minister of Infrastructure, Communities and Intergovernmental Affairs may acquire shares in PPP Canada Inc.
  - The appropriate Minister holds the shares acquired.
  - With **Governor in Council approval**, PPP Canada Inc. may:
    - Sell or dispose of all or substantially all of its assets.
    - Any of its subsidiaries may sell or dispose of assets (even if it constitutes all or substantially all of the group's assets).

- **Liability**:
  - Her Majesty in right of Canada is **not liable** for obligations incurred by PPP Canada Inc. **except** in relation to activities specifically authorized under section 211.

- **Agent Status**:
  - PPP Canada Inc. is **not an agent of Her Majesty in right of Canada** in general.
  - It **is an agent** only in relation to the activities listed above and any activities specified in a **Governor in Council order**.

> This structure ensures **accountability and risk limitation** for the federal government while enabling PPP innovation and efficiency.
