### ðŸ”¹ 5. **Objectives of the Act**
The Act aims to achieve the following goals:

1. Protect the **health and well-being** of individuals (especially crew members).
2. Promote **safety** in marine transportation and recreational boating.
3. Protect the **marine environment** from pollution and damage.
4. Develop a **regulatory scheme** that supports viable, effective, and economical marine commerce.
5. Promote an **efficient marine transportation system**.
6. Encourage **recreational boating** in Canadian waters.
7. Ensure Canada fulfills its **international obligations** under bilateral or multilateral agreements.
8. Encourage **harmonization of marine practices**.
9. Establish an **effective inspection and enforcement program**.

### ðŸ”¹ 8. **Interim Orders (Emergency Powers)**

- May contain any provision that could be included in a future regulation.
