The primary purpose of the *Federal-Provincial Fiscal Arrangements Act* is to:

- Establish a fair and equitable system of **fiscal equalization** so that all provinces can maintain a reasonable level of public service provision regardless of their economic strength.
- Provide mechanisms for **coordinated tax administration** between federal and provincial governments.
