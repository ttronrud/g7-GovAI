### 4. **Age and Time Periods**

- **Age Determination Rule**:  
  For any provision referencing reaching a specified age (e.g., retirement age), the person is deemed to have reached that age at the **beginning of the month following** the month in which they actually reached it.  
  - **Periods ending** with the age are **inclusive** of the month they reached the age.  
  - **Periods beginning** with the age are **exclusive** of that month.

> This ensures consistency in calculating time-based benefits (e.g., retirement age, disability claims).
