## üèõÔ∏è **Overview of the National Defence Act**

The *National Defence Act* (NDA) serves as the primary statute governing the Canadian Armed Forces (CAF), including the **regular force**, **reserve force**, and any **special forces**. It establishes the **Department of National Defence (DND)**, defines the roles and responsibilities of key officials, sets out the legal framework for **military justice**, and provides definitions for critical terms used throughout the Act and the **Code of Service Discipline** (CSD).

The Act also integrates with other laws such as the **Criminal Code**, particularly in relation to **serious offences**, **terrorism**, **criminal organizations**, and **mental health** issues.
