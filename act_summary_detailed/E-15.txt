### 15. **Free Samples and Goods Given Away**  
- A **licensed wholesaler** who gives away goods (e.g., free samples, replacement parts) **without payment of tax** is deemed to have **retained the goods for own use**.
- **Exception**: If the giveaway is under a **written warranty** from the manufacturer and the **warranty cost is included in the sale price** (or duty-paid value for imported goods).
- **Purpose**: Prevents tax evasion by ensuring that tax is paid on goods that are effectively distributed or used.

- It comes into force **no more than four years before** the date of the determination.
- **Purpose**: Allows for retroactive application of tax rules to ensure consistency and fairness in tax obligations.
