## ðŸ“Œ ELIGIBILITY FOR A FULL MONTHLY PENSION

A full monthly pension may be paid to a person if they meet **one of three categories** of eligibility:

### 1. **People who were pensioners on July 1, 1977**
- Automatically eligible without further conditions.

### 2. **People who were not pensioners on July 1, 1977**
Must meet **all** of the following:
- Attained **65 years of age**.
- Resided in Canada for the **ten years immediately before** the approval date of their application, **OR**
  - If they did not reside in Canada, they must have:
    - Resided in Canada **after age 18** for an aggregate period **equal to at least three times** their total absence from Canada during those ten years.
    - Resided in Canada for **at least one year immediately before** the application approval date.
- **Additional condition**: Must have attained 25 years of age on July 1, 1977, or have lived in Canada after age 18 or held a valid immigration visa.

> âš–ï¸ This rule emphasizes **long-term residency** in Canada as a key eligibility factor.

### 3. **People who were not pensioners on July 1, 1977**
Must meet:
- Attained **65 years of age**.
- Resided in Canada **after age 18** and **before application approval** for an aggregate period of **at least 40 years**.

> âœ… This category is designed to recognize long-term residents who may have lived abroad but maintained a strong connection to Canada.
