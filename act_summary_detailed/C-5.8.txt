### **8. Permanent Establishment (PE)**

A **permanent establishment** is a fixed place of business through which business is carried on.

#### **Examples of PE:**
- Place of management;
- Branch;
- Office;
- Factory;
- Workshop;
- Mine, oil/gas well, quarry;
- Building site or construction project lasting **over 6 months**;
- Fixed place used for **collecting information** or **publicity** (if that is the actual business activity).

#### **Exclusions (Not a PE):**
- Use of facilities **solely for storage or display** of goods;
- Maintenance of stock **for storage or display**;
- Maintenance of stock **for processing by another enterprise**;
- Fixed place used **only for purchasing goods or collecting information** (if preparatory/auxiliary);
- Any combination of the above if the overall activity is **preparatory or auxiliary**.

> âœ… **Exception**: A person acting on behalf of a company (not an independent agent) who has **authority to conclude contracts** in a Contracting State is deemed to have a PE in that State **unless** their activities are limited to those excluded above.

> âŒ **No automatic PE due to control**:
