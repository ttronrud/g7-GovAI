## üìö **Detailed Summary of the Framework Agreement on First Nation Land Management Act**

### üîç **1. Purpose and Background**

This Act serves as the **modern legal embodiment** of the *Framework Agreement on First Nation Land Management*, originally signed on **February 12, 1996**, between Her Majesty in right of Canada and **13 First Nations**. Since then, additional First Nations have joined the Agreement.

- The original **First Nations Land Management Act (1999)** was enacted to ratify and implement the Framework Agreement.
- However, this new Act replaces the 1999 legislation to:
  - Reflect the **primacy and legal force** of the Framework Agreement;
  - Ensure **continuity and stability** in land management practices;
  - Improve clarity, accessibility, and legal consistency in how First Nations manage their lands.

> ‚úÖ **Key Objective**: To establish a clear, legally binding, and enforceable framework that affirms First Nations' authority over their lands and resources ‚Äî while maintaining a strong relationship with the federal government.
