## **Comprehensive Summary of the Insurance Companies Act**

### **1. Introduction and Citation**
- **Official Title**: The Act is formally titled *The Insurance Companies Act*.
- **Enactment**: It was passed by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and House of Commons of Canada.
- **Purpose**: The Act establishes the regulatory framework governing the formation, operation, governance, financial reporting, and oversight of insurance companies in Canada.
