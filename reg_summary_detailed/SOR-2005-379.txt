## âœ… Assessable Activities (What Requires Assessment)

Projects that fall under the following categories are **subject to mandatory environmental and socio-economic assessment** under the ESEAA:

1. **Industrial Development**  
   - Mining (including placer and quartz mining)  
   - Energy resource development (e.g., oil, gas)  
   - Agricultural land development  
   - Commercial forestry (cutting standing or fallen trees)  
   - Townsite development  
   - Construction, modification, operation, decommissioning, or abandonment of related facilities

2. **Oil or Natural Gas Pipeline or Field Facilities**  
   - Includes pipelines, compressor stations, separators, injection stations, waste disposal  
   - *Excludes* well-head separators, treaters, dehydrators

3. **Storage Tank Systems**  
   - Any system with tanks >230 L installed in fixed locations  
   - Includes diking, spill containment, pumps, dispensers, overfill protection

4. **Quartz Exploration Programs**  
   - Exploration within a 20 km radius over 12 months  
   - Conducted by the same operator on one or more quartz grants

5. **Placer Mining Operations**  
   - Mining or prospecting for gold or precious minerals in natural strata  
   - Applies to individual or grouped claims (subject to grouping certificate)

6. **Projects Affecting Fish Habitats**  
   - Any activity that may impact spawning, nursery, migration, or food supply areas for fish

7. **Projects Involving Contaminated Sites**  
   - Activities at sites where soil or groundwater exceeds contaminant thresholds (as per *Contaminated Sites Regulations*)

8. **Projects Involving Endangered or Threatened Species**  
   - Any activity that could harm species listed in the *Species at Risk Act*

9. **Projects Involving Migratory Birds**  
   - Activities that may affect bird habitats or migration paths
