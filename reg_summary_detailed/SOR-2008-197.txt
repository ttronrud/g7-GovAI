## ğŸš« **Prohibited Releases of Petroleum Products**

### **General Prohibition (s. 1)**
- A person **must not release** (or permit/cause) any **petroleum product or allied petroleum product in liquid form** into the environment from a storage tank system.

#### Exceptions:
- If the release **does not reach outside the secondary containment** of the system.

### **Transfer Prohibitions (s. 3)**
- A person **must not release** liquid during transfer **to or from** the tank system.

#### Exceptions:
- If the release **does not reach outside the transfer area**, and:
  - The transfer area is designed in accordance with **section 15(1)**.

## ğŸ›‘ **Leak Response and Withdrawal Requirements**

### **General Leak Response (s. 1, 2, 3)**
- Upon detecting a leak:
  - The owner/operator must **temporarily withdraw** the leaking component (if isolatable), or the **entire system**.
  - The system/component may **only return to service** after **necessary repairs** ensure no further leakage.

### **Special Requirements for Tanks Installed Before June 12, 2008**

#### **Single-Walled Underground Tanks (s. 3(2))**
- Must be **permanently withdrawn from service** and **removed** within **two years** after:
  - June 12, 2008; or
  - The date of leak discovery (whichever is later).

- **Removal requirements**:
  - For **vertically-oriented underground tanks**: Remove **piping and other external components** (s. 45).
  - For **all other systems**: Remove the **entire single-walled underground tanks and components**.

#### **Single-Walled Underground Piping (s. 3(3))**
- Must be:
  - **Temporarily withdrawn** (s. 43);
  - **Permanently withdrawn** (s. 44);
  - **Removed** (s. 45);
  - **Replaced** (s. 14);
  - OR
  - **Permanently withdrawn** and system removed (within 2 years of leak discovery or June 12, 2008, whichever is later), depending on tank type.

- **Exemptions**:
  - If, as of June 12, 2008, the piping had:
    - **Steel piping**: cathodic protection, leak detection, groundwater/vapour monitoring wells, single vertical check valves, or mechanical leak detection devices;
    - **Non-metallic/copper piping**: leak detection, groundwater/vapour monitoring, check valves, or mechanical leak detection.
