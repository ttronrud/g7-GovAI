The primary objective of this regulation is to **withdraw specific tracts of territorial land in the Northwest Territories from disposal (i.e., from being available for sale, lease, or other forms of transfer)** because these lands may be required as a **candidate protected area** under the **Northwest Territories Protected Areas Strategy**.

### **Duration of Withdrawal**

### **Repeal Clause (Important Clarification)**

The regulation includes a **repeal clause** stating that:

> In the Northwest Territories, all the described land parcels (the large area and the specific Sahtu sites) are **repealed from disposal** under the Territorial Lands Act.
