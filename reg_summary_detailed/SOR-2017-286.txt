### 1. **Applicability**
Applies to:
- Canadian vessels in **polar waters**  
- Foreign vessels in **shipping safety control zones**

> Based on the **Arctic Waters Pollution Prevention Act**, which governs waste disposal and pollution control.

### 2. **Permitted Waste Deposition (Under Exceptional Circumstances)**

Waste may be deposited if:
| Waste Type | Permitted Condition |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Oil** | Minimal, unavoidable leakage from underwater machinery; not due to negligence |
| **Fishing gear** | Accidental loss with all reasonable precautions taken; or for environmental protection or crew safety |
| **Garbage** | Damage to vessel/equipment; all precautions taken before and after to minimize loss |
| **Life-saving** | Necessary to save a life or secure vessel safety |

> **No deposit allowed** if due to negligence or failure to follow ordinary practice.

---

### 3. **Pollution Emergency Planning Requirements**
Operations in polar waters must be reflected in:
- Oil Records Books  
- Shipboard Oil Pollution Emergency Plan  
- Marine Pollution Emergency Plan  

> Ensures that emergency preparedness accounts for unique Arctic conditions.
