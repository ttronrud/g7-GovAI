## üìù Application Requirements for Release of Information

All applications under **Part I** of the Act must include the following information:

1. **Applicant Type and Affiliation**:
   - Indicate whether the applicant is:
     - An official of a **court**
     - A **peace officer**
     - A **provincial enforcement service**
     - A **provincial child support service**
     - A **designated authority**
   - If applicable:
     - Name of the **court** (for court officials)
     - Name of the **police force** (for peace officers)
     - Name of the **service** and name of the **other entity** it is acting on behalf of (under section 6.2)
     - Name of the **service or authority** (for child support or designated bodies)

2. **Applicant Details**:
   - Full name of the person completing the application

3. **File Reference**:
   - Reference number of the file, if applicable

4. **Personal Information of the Individual (Subject of Request)**:
   - Full name
   - Date of birth
   - Gender
   - Phone number and email address (if known)
   - Social Insurance Number (SIN), if known
   - Surnames at birth of parents (if known)

5. **Purpose of the Application**:
   - Clear statement of why the information is being requested (e.g., enforcement of child support, parenting arrangement, etc.)

6. **Indication of Requested Information**:
   - Specific data points being sought

7. **Declaration (for certain applicants)**:
   - For **provincial enforcement services**, **provincial child support services**, or **designated authorities**, a declaration must be made confirming that all information provided is accurate and truthful.

> üîç **Additional Requirement for Corporate Control**:
> If the application relates to a **corporation controlled indirectly** by the named individual, the applicant must provide an explanation of how indirect control is established (e.g., through shares, voting rights, or governance).
