## ðŸŽ¯ Purpose and Scope

The Convention establishes a framework for **recognition and coordination of social security benefits** between Canada and Peru, ensuring that:

- Contributions made in one country are **recognized** in the other.
- Individuals are not penalized for residing or working abroad.
- Benefits are **calculated fairly and consistently**, based on totalized periods of contribution.
- **Eligibility and benefit amounts** are determined using a harmonized system of creditable periods.

It applies to **three main types of benefits**:
1. **Old Age Benefits** (pensions)
2. **Disability Benefits**
3. **Survivor and Funeral Costs Benefits**
