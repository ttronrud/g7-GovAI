### **Detailed Summary of the Regulation**

This regulation, issued under the **Canada Wildlife Act** (R.S. 1985, c. W-9), establishes the administrative transfer of specific public lands in the province of **Saskatchewan** to the **Minister of the Environment** for the purposes of **wildlife research, conservation, and interpretation**. The transfer is formalized through a government instrument signed by the Governor in Council, acting on the recommendation of the Minister of the Environment, pursuant to **subsection 4(1)** of the Canada Wildlife Act.

The regulation designates **four distinct land units** located in the **Last Mountain Lake area**, each with precise legal descriptions, exceptions (notably regarding mineral rights), and total acreage. These units are collectively referred to as the **Last Mountain Lake Unit**, **Saline Creek Unit**, **West Unit**, and **East Unit**.

---

## **1. Legal Basis and Purpose**

- **Authority**: The Governor in Council (representing the federal government) acts under **subsection 4(1) of the Canada Wildlife Act**, which allows for the assignment of public lands to the Minister of the Environment when the Governor in Council determines that such lands are required for:
  - Wildlife research
  - Wildlife conservation
  - Wildlife interpretation (e.g., public education, habitat monitoring)

- **Objective**: To ensure that these lands are protected and managed for the benefit of wildlife and ecosystems, supporting scientific study, habitat preservation, and public awareness.

- **Administering Authority**: The **Minister of the Environment** is granted full **administration, management, and control** of the designated lands.

---

## **2. Land Units and Descriptions**

### **A. Last Mountain Lake Unit**

This is the largest and most comprehensive unit, encompassing three parcels of land with precise legal descriptions.

#### **Firstly: Parcels A–Z, AA, BB, CC, DD, EE**
- **Location**: Townships 27 and 28, Ranges 23 and 24, west of the second meridian, Saskatchewan.
- **Legal Description**: Parcels A through Z (inclusive), AA, BB, CC, DD, and EE, as shown on a survey plan signed by **Walter T. Volohatuke**, a registered Saskatchewan land surveyor.
- **Plan Reference**:
  - Saskatoon Land Titles Office: No. 91-S-25402
  - Humboldt Land Titles Office: No. 91-H-06231
- **Area**: Approximately **9,416.9 hectares (23,269.7 acres)**.
- **Note**: This parcel is the core of the Last Mountain Lake Unit and is intended for wildlife conservation and research.

#### **Secondly: Southwest Quarter of Section 8, Township 27, Range 23**
- **Certificate of Title**: No. 90-H-10071, registered November 15, 1990.
- **Area**: Southwest quarter of section 8.
- **Exception**:
  - A strip of land **5.03 meters (16.5 feet)** wide along the southernmost boundary is **excluded**.
  - **Mines and minerals are excluded** by reference to **Instrument AY5988**.
- **Purpose**: The land is reserved for conservation, with mineral rights retained by the Crown.

#### **Thirdly: Southwest Quarter of Section 5, Township 27, Range 23**
- **Certificate of Title**: No. 90-H-10070, registered November 15, 1990.
- **Reference**: As shown on the **Township Plan dated December 2, 1903**.
- **Exception**:
  - **0.17 hectare (0.42 acre)** is reserved for a **roadway**, as per **Plan 71-H-08678**.
  - **Mines and minerals are excluded** by **Instrument U3435**.
- **Purpose**: The land is dedicated to conservation, with access and infrastructure limited to support wildlife activities.

#### **Total Area of Last Mountain Lake Unit**:
- Approximately **9,547.47 hectares (23,592.28 acres)**.

> ✅ This unit forms the primary wildlife conservation area and is likely used for habitat protection, species monitoring, and public education programs.

### **B. Saline Creek Unit**

A smaller, focused unit located in the adjacent area.

#### **Firstly: Northeast and Southeast Quarters of Section 4, Township 29, Range 23**
- **Certificate of Title**: No. 90-H-08591, registered September 26, 1990.
- **Exception**:
  - **0.22 hectare (0.55 acre)** is reserved for a **roadway** (Plan P5396).
  - **Minerals in the Crown** (i.e., mineral rights remain with the federal government).
- **Purpose**: Conservation and research related to riparian and wetland habitats.

#### **Secondly: Southeast Quarter of Section 2, Township 29, Range 23**
- **Certificate of Title**: No. 68-H-03222, registered May 30, 1968.
- **Minerals**: In the Crown (rights reserved).

#### **Thirdly: Southeast and Southwest Quarters of Section 3, Township 29, Range 23**
- **Certificate of Title**: No. 68-H-03222A, registered May 30, 1968.
- **Exception**:
  - All **mines and minerals** are excluded.
  - Specific exclusions are tied to two transfers:
    - **Transfer No. 60-H-05892** (southeast quarter)
    - **Transfer No. AT3465** (southwest quarter)

#### **Total Area of Saline Creek Unit**:
- Approximately **324 hectares (800 acres)**.

> ✅ This unit supports research and conservation in a sensitive riparian zone and is likely focused on wetland and migratory bird habitats.

### **C. West Unit**

A smaller parcel located in the western portion of the region.

- **Exception**:

#### **Secondly: Northeast Quarter of Section 26, Township 28, Range 24**

- **Minerals**: In the Crown (rights retained).

#### **Total Area of West Unit**:
- Approximately **194 hectares (480 acres)**.

> ✅ This unit supports localized wildlife studies and may serve as a buffer or corridor between larger conservation areas.

### **D. East Unit**

A complex unit with multiple parcels and several exceptions.

#### **Firstly: Whole of Section 23, Township 28, Range 23**
- **Certificate of Title**: No. 77-03562, registered April 21, 1977.
- **Exceptions**:
  - **0.53 hectare (1.31 acre)** from the northeast quarter
  - **0.41 hectare (1.02 acre)** from the northwest quarter
    - Both reserved for **roadway development** (Plan No. 70-H-05056)
  - **All mines and minerals** are excluded via **Transfer No. 77-H-03562**
    - Applies to northwest and southwest quarters
  - Minerals in the Crown for northeast and southeast quarters

#### **Secondly: Southwest Quarter of Section 35, Township 28, Range 23**
- **Certificate of Title**: No. 67-H-03829, registered June 13, 1967.
- **Exception**:
  - All **mines and minerals** excluded via **Transfer No. C-4782 (115-29)**

#### **Thirdly: Northwest and Southwest Quarters of Section 26, Township 28, Range 23**
- **Certificate of Title**: No. 77-H-03562A, registered April 21, 1977.
- **Exceptions**:
  - **1.36 hectares (3.35 acres)** from the southwest quarter reserved for **roadway** (Plan No. 70-H-05056)
  - All **mines and minerals** excluded via **Transfer No. 77-H-03562** (northwest and southwest quarters)

#### **Total Area of East Unit**:
- Approximately **453 hectares (1,120 acres)**.

> ✅ This unit is likely used for habitat connectivity, species movement, and research on forested or grassland ecosystems.
