### ğŸ“ 4. **Permit Issuance and Conditions**

- Upon application, the **Superintendent** must issue a camping permit authorizing:
  - The applicant (individual), or
  - The group represented by the applicant.

- **No reapplication** is allowed during the validity period of an existing permit.

- The Superintendent may **refuse** a permit if:
  - It is necessary for **park preservation, safety, or management**.

- A permit must include:
  - The **specific campsite** to which it applies.
  - The **validity period** (start and end dates).

### ğŸ“… 5. **Permit Validity and Transferability**

- A camping permit is **not transferable** and is valid **only during the period the campsite is occupied**.

- A campsite is deemed **unoccupied** if:
  - A tent or trailer has been **unoccupied for over 48 hours**, or
  - Has been **removed for over 24 hours**.

- A permit **expires** when:
  - The expiry date specified in the permit, or
  - The date specified by the Superintendent via notice at the campground entrance.
