### **3. Prescribed Types of Eligible Financial Contracts**

Under subsection 2(1) of the CCAA, the following financial agreements are **prescribed as eligible financial contracts**:

#### **(a) Derivatives Agreements**
- Must either:
  - Trade on a **futures or options exchange**, **board**, or **regulated market**, or  
  - Be part of **recurrent dealings** in derivatives markets (including over-the-counter (OTC) markets for securities or commodities)

> This ensures that only actively traded or market-standardized derivatives are eligible, reducing risk and complexity in restructuring.

#### **(b) Agreements to:**
- **Borrow or lend securities or commodities**, including:
  - Agreements where the borrower can repay with other securities, commodities, cash, or cash equivalents  
  *(e.g., short-term lending in securities markets)*
- **Clear or settle** securities, futures, options, or derivatives transactions  
  *(e.g., clearing houses facilitating settlement)*
- **Act as a depository** for securities  
  *(e.g., holding securities on behalf of clients)*

#### **(c) Repurchase, Reverse Repurchase, or Buy-Sellback Agreements**
- With respect to **securities or commodities**  
  *(e.g., repo transactions where one party sells securities with an agreement to repurchase them later)*

#### **(d) Margin Loans**
- Only those **in respect of a securities account or futures account** maintained by a **financial intermediary**  
  *(e.g., a broker allowing a client to borrow against securities in their account)*

> Margin loans are excluded if they relate to personal or non-intermediary accounts.

#### **(e) Master Agreements**
- A **master agreement** is eligible **if it covers** any of the agreements listed in (a) to (e) above  
  *(e.g., ISDA master agreements governing derivatives or securities lending)*

> Master agreements provide a framework for multiple underlying contracts and are treated as eligible when they relate to the above-mentioned instruments.

#### **(f) Guarantees, Indemnities, or Reimbursement Obligations**
- A guarantee, indemnity, or reimbursement obligation **with respect to liabilities** under any of the agreements in (a) to (g) above  
  *(e.g., a third party guaranteeing a derivative obligation)*

> These are eligible if they support or cover obligations under the prescribed contracts.

#### **(g) Agreements Relating to Financial Collateral**
