## ğŸš¨ **Core Sulphur Concentration Limits**

These are the **primary environmental standards** established under **section 139 of CEPA**:

| Scenario | Sulphur Limit |
|--------|---------------|
| **Per batch of gasoline (general rule)** | **12 mg/kg** (if not using pool average) |
| **Per batch using pool average** | **80 mg/kg** |
| **Pool average (for primary suppliers who elect this method)** | **10 mg/kg** |
| **Final concentration in gasoline sold to consumers** | **80 mg/kg** |

> ğŸ” **Important Notes**:
> - The **12 mg/kg** limit applies to **individual batches** unless the primary supplier chooses to use a **pool average**.
> - If a primary supplier elects to use a **pool average**, the **average concentration across the entire pool must not exceed 10 mg/kg**.
> - If **sulphur compliance units** are used to adjust sulphur levels (e.g., through use of low-sulphur oxygenates), the **adjusted pool average must still not exceed 10 mg/kg**.
> - The **80 mg/kg** limit applies to **gasoline sold to end users**, even if the batch itself is below 12 mg/kg â€” this ensures compliance at the point of sale.

## ğŸ“ˆ **Pool Average System (Election-Based Compliance)**

Primary suppliers may **choose to calculate sulphur levels using a pool average** instead of individual batch testing. This allows for greater flexibility in managing large volumes of fuel.

### How to Elect:
- Submit a **notice of election** to the Minister:
  - **First-time producers/importers**: No later than **60 days before first production/import**
  - **Existing suppliers**: No later than **November 1 of the year before** the year in which the calculation will begin
  - **Exception**: For the year 2020, notice must be submitted within **30 days of the regulation coming into force**

### Required Information in the Notice:
- Pool definition (refinery, blending facility, or imported gasoline)
- Sampling locations, frequency, and methods
- Location where sulphur concentration is determined
- Method for measuring batch volume
- Location where samples and records are stored
- Method for adjusting sulphur concentration (if using sulphur-limited oxygenates or butane)
