### **3. Prohibited Classes of Applicants**

1. **Her Majesty in right of any province**

2. **Agents of Her Majesty in right of any province**  
   ‚Üí Any entity acting on behalf of the provincial government (e.g., Crown agencies) is ineligible.

   ‚Üí Local governments (e.g., city councils) are barred from holding broadcasting licences.

### **8. Practical Impact**

- **Distribution rights** (e.g., cable or satellite distribution) may still be granted to municipal entities under strict conditions.
