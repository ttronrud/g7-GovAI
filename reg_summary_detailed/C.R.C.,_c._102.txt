### **3. Scope of Application**

These regulations apply to:
- All **land and underwater areas** (including public road allowances)  
- Located **adjacent to or in the vicinity of Pitt Meadows Airport**  
- Within the **outer limits defined in Part II of the Schedule**  
- **Excluding** land that is formally part of the airport (e.g., active runways, taxiways, or designated operational zones)

This means that any development or construction in the surrounding area must comply with the height restrictions set by the regulation.

---

### **4. Prohibited Development**

No person may:
- Erect or construct  
- Add to an existing building, structure, or object  

**On any land or underwater area covered by these regulations**, if the **highest point** of the proposed object would exceed any of the following surfaces at the point of location:

1. **Approach Surfaces**  
2. **Outer Surface**  
3. **Transitional Surfaces**

> These surfaces are defined geometrically in the schedule and represent safe zones for aircraft operations. Exceeding them poses a risk to flight safety.

---

### **5. Natural Growth Exceptions**

If a **natural growth** (e.g., trees, shrubs, or vegetation) on regulated land exceeds the height of any of the defined surfaces, the **Minister of Transport** may issue a direction requiring the **owner or occupier** of the land to **remove the excessive growth**.

> This provision ensures that natural features do not become hazardous obstructions during aircraft operations.
