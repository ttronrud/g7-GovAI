## ðŸ·ï¸ **Classification of Violations: Types A to E**

Each violation is classified as **Type A, B, C, D, or E**, based on the **gravity** of the violation, as defined in **column 2 of the relevant schedule**.

- **Classification Logic**:
  - The **gravity increases alphabetically** (e.g., "A" is less serious than "E").

> Example: A violation related to unsafe working conditions may be classified as Type E (most serious), while a minor wage calculation error may be Type A.
