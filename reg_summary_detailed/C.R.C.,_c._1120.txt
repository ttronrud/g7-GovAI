## 3. **Prohibited Fishing Activities (Without Proper Permits)**

No person may fish in any of the following situations **without the appropriate permit or licence**:

| Situation | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| In **any park water listed in Column I of Schedule II** | Must hold a **fishing permit** |
| For **salmon in Terra Nova National Park** | Must have a **salmon licence** and **at least one unused salmon tag** |
| For **lake trout in Prince Albert National Park** | Must have both a **lake trout endorsement** and a **fishing permit**, plus **at least one unused lake trout tag** |
| For **soft-shell clams in Kouchibouguac National Park** | Must have a **soft-shell clam permit** |
| For **salmon in Cape Breton Highlands National Park** | Must have a **salmon licence** |
| For **salmon in Gros Morne National Park** | Must have a **salmon licence** AND: <br> - at least one unused salmon tag <br> - a salmon licence issued under *Consolidated Newfoundland and Labrador Regulation 1156/96* (Wild Life Act), with an unused tag from that regulation |

## 4. **Age Exceptions (Under 16 Years Old)**

A person under 16 may fish **if accompanied by a person 16 or older** who holds the required permit or licence:

| Activity | Allowed if accompanied by adult holder of: |
|--------|--------------------------------------------|
| Fishing in general (any park) | Fishing permit |
| Salmon fishing | Salmon licence with tag |
| Lake trout fishing | Lake trout endorsement with tag |
| Soft-shell clam fishing | Soft-shell clam permit |
| Salmon fishing (Gros Morne/Terra Nova) | Salmon licence |

> This ensures youth are supervised and compliant with regulations.

## 5. **Issuance of Permits and Licences**

### Who Can Apply?
- Any person can apply to a **Superintendent**.

### Fees
- Applicant pays the **applicable fee** under **Section 24 of the Parks Canada Agency Act**.

### What Can Be Issued?
- **Fishing permit**  
- **Salmon licence** (with one salmon tag, where applicable)  
- **Soft-shell clam permit**

### Lake Trout Endorsement
- Issued **on application** by someone holding a fishing permit.
- Includes **up to two lake trout tags**.

> All permits, licences, and endorsements are:
- Valid only for the **period issued**.
- Must be **signed by the holder**.
- **Lost?** A new one is issued **free of charge** upon proof of ownership.

---

## 6. **Carrying and Production of Permits**

- Every person must **carry** their permit, licence, or endorsement while fishing.
- Upon request by a **superintendent or park warden**, the document must be **produced for inspection**.
