## ðŸ¢ **Provisional Licensing and Prospectors**

### 1. **Eligibility for a Licence to Prospect**
- A person may obtain a licence if:
  - They are **18 years or older**, or
  - They are a **company incorporated** under:
    - *Business Corporations Act*, S.N.W.T. 1996, c. 19 (NWT)
    - *Canada Business Corporations Act* (federal)

> âŒ Licences are **not transferable**.

### 2. **Validity of Licence**
- Valid from **date of issue** until **March 31** of the following year.
- **Renewable** for one additional year starting **April 1** of the renewal year.

### 3. **Access to Licence Information**
- A licensee may request a **copy of their licence** upon payment of the applicable fee (as per Schedule 1).
