**Detailed Summary of the Small Manufacturers or Producers Exemption Regulations**

---

### **1. Purpose and Authority**
The *Small Manufacturers or Producers Exemption Regulations* are a set of rules established under the **Excise Tax Act**. They were enacted by **His Excellency the Governor General in Council**, on the recommendation of the **Minister of Finance** and the **Minister of National Revenue**, pursuant to **subsection 31(2)** of the Excise Tax Act.

**Objective**:  
To exempt certain small-scale manufacturers or producers from paying **consumption tax** (commonly known as GST/HST) or **sales tax** on goods they produce or sell, provided they meet specific criteria related to volume, nature of production, and business activity.

These regulations may be cited as:

### **3. Exempt Classes of Small Manufacturers or Producers**

The following categories of individuals or businesses are exempt from consumption or sales tax on goods they manufacture or sell, **provided** they meet the specified conditions:

---

#### **(a) General Exemption for Low-Value Sales or Self-Use**
- Applies to **manufacturers** (excluding those who have elected to operate under a licence).
- Exemption applies if:
  - The value of goods **sold** or **manufactured for their own use** does **not exceed $50,000 per calendar year**.
- This exemption covers goods that would otherwise be subject to consumption or sales tax.

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important Condition**:  
> If a manufacturer or producer whose sales fall under this category exceed **$50,000 in value** during a calendar year, the exemption **ceases automatically**.

---

#### **(b) Manufacturers of Custom Goods (Personalized or One-of-a-Kind)**
Exempt for goods produced to meet **individual customer specifications**. Includes:
- **Blacksmiths** ‚Äì producing custom metalwork.
- **Opticians** ‚Äì making custom eyewear or lenses.
- **Plumbers** ‚Äì creating custom plumbing fixtures or fittings.
- **Cider millers** ‚Äì producing cider from a customer‚Äôs apples (not mass production).
- **Manufacturers of show cards and signs**:
  - Made from **cardboard, cotton, paper, or other non-durable materials**.
  - **Hand-drawn or hand-lettered** (not printed or mass-produced).
- **Portrait photographers** ‚Äì producing hand-made photographic prints.

> ‚úÖ Note: These exemptions apply only when goods are made **to individual customer requirements**, not in bulk or for resale.

---

#### **(c) Professionals Manufacturing Printed Matter in Connection with Services**
- **Barristers, solicitors, notaries** ‚Äì when manufacturing printed materials (e.g., legal documents, forms).
- **Accountants** ‚Äì when producing accounting-related printed materials (e.g., reports, statements).
- **Engineers** ‚Äì when producing technical drawings or printed outputs related to engineering services.

> üìå The exemption is limited to **printed matter directly related to professional services rendered to the public**.

---

#### **(d) Original Design Models (Excluding Replicas)**
- Exempt for manufacturing **original engineering and planning design models** of:
  - Ships
  - Buildings
  - Industrial plants
  - Construction layouts
- **Excluded**:  
  - Merchandise display models  
  - Replica models (e.g., scale models of cars or buildings for retail)

> ‚úÖ Only **original, non-replica, design models** are covered.

---

#### **(e) Artificial or Natural Flower Arrangements in Retail**
- Persons who:
  - Manufacture arrangements of **artificial or natural flowers or foliage**.
  - Do so **in a retail establishment**.
  - Sell them **directly and exclusively to end users** (not to other businesses).

> ‚úÖ Exemption applies only when the arrangements are made **in-store** and sold **directly to customers**.

---

#### **(f) Field Coils for Motor/Transformer Repair**
- Manufacturers who produce **field coils** solely for their own use in repairing motors and transformers for customers.

> ‚ö†Ô∏è Exemption applies only if the coils are **used internally** in repair work, not for resale.

---

#### **(g) Media Production by Broadcast Operators**
- **Radio and television station operators** who:
  - Manufacture **discs, film, slides, magnetic tapes, photographs, props, and sets**.
  - These are used for **broadcasting or program production**.

> ‚úÖ Exemption applies to **production for internal use or broadcast**, not for commercial resale.

---

#### **(h) Picture Framing Services**
- **Picture framers** who:
  - Frame pictures, photographs, diplomas, or similar items **owned by customers**.
  - Use **frames they manufacture themselves** or **purchase from others** for this purpose.

> ‚úÖ Exemption applies only when framing is done **for a customer‚Äôs personal property**.

---

#### **(i) Microfilming Operations**
- Persons engaged in **microfilming** (e.g., converting documents into microfilm for archival or preservation purposes).

> ‚úÖ Exemption applies to the **production of microfilm** as part of professional services.

---

#### **(j) Theatre Arts Groups (Non-Resale Prop and Set Production)**
- Theatre groups who:
  - Manufacture **props and sets**.
  - Use them **solely for their own productions**.
  - Do **not sell or resell** these items.

> ‚úÖ Exemption applies only when the props and sets are **for internal theatrical use**, not for commercial sale.

---

#### **(k) Potting Soil and Mixtures**
- Persons who manufacture **potting soil or potting soil mixtures**.

> ‚úÖ Exempt from consumption/sales tax on such products.

---

#### **(l) Handcrafted Goods (Non-Industrial, Non-Repetitive)**
- Persons who:
  - Design and produce goods **from basic materials**.
  - Use **hand tools and manual skill** only.
- **Excluded from exemption** if the goods are:
  - Made wholly or partly from **precious metals** (e.g., gold, silver).
  - Made wholly or partly from **precious or semi-precious stones** (e.g., diamonds, rubies).
  - Made in a **repetitive manner** using **jigs, templates, moulds, dies, or similar devices**.
- **Exception**:  
  - Jigs, templates, or dies **may be used to produce the original or first unit** (i.e., prototype), but not for mass production.

> ‚úÖ This exemption supports **artisanal, handmade craftsmanship** and protects small-scale, non-industrial production.

---

#### **(m) Steam Production (Not for Own Use)**
- Persons who produce **steam** (e.g., for heating, power generation).
- **Exemption applies only if the steam is not used for their own manufacturing processes**.
- Excluded: Manufacturers who produce steam **for use in manufacturing goods**.

> ‚úÖ Exemption applies only to **steam produced for external purposes**, not for internal production.

---

#### **(n) Import and Manufacturing of New Motor Vehicles**
- Persons who:
  - **Purchase or import** new motor vehicles (designed for highway use) or their **chassis**.
  - Have these vehicles or chassis **further manufactured** by a **licensed manufacturer** (who pays sales tax on the manufacturing charge).
  - The final product is used for:
    - Their own use
    - Sale or lease to consumers or users

> ‚úÖ Exemption applies to the **importer and final user** in this scenario, even though the licensed manufacturer pays tax.

---

#### **(o) Motorhome Importers (Direct Sale or Lease)**
- Persons described in **paragraph 2(1)(g)** of the Excise Tax Act (definition of "manufacturer or producer") who:
  - **Sell directly and exclusively to consumers or users**, or
  - **Rent, lease, or take for their own use**
  - Motorhomes they have **imported into Canada**

> ‚úÖ Exemption applies to **direct-to-consumer or personal use** sales or leases of imported motorhomes.

> ‚ùå Does **not** apply to resale or commercial distribution.

---

### **4. Key Conditions and Limitations**

| Condition | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Annual Sales Cap** | For exemptions under (a), sales must not exceed **$50,000 per calendar year**. Exceeding this amount **terminates the exemption**. |
| **No Resale or Mass Production** | Most exemptions apply only to **custom, one-off, or handcrafted goods**. Mass production or use of automated tools (moulds, dies) disqualifies. |
| **Exclusions Based on Materials** | Goods involving precious metals or stones are **not exempt**. |
| **Professional Services Link** | Exemptions for professionals (e.g., accountants, engineers) are tied to **services rendered to the public**. |
| **No Tax on Licensed Entities** | The exemption does **not apply** to manufacturers who have elected to operate under a licence. |
| **Revoked Provisions** | One section (related to radio/TV station manufacturing) was **revoked** by SOR/85-619, section 1. It is no longer in effect. |
