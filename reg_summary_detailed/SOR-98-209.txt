### 2. **Antique Firearms**
- **Sections 5 to 13 do not apply** to antique firearms.
- Individuals may **store, display, or transport** antique firearms **only if unloaded**.
- **Transportation in unattended vehicles**:
  - If the vehicle has a secure trunk: firearm must be in it and trunk locked.
  - If no secure trunk: firearm must not be visible and vehicle must be securely locked.
- **Handguns** must be transported in a **locked, opaque container** that cannot be easily broken open.

> Note: Antique firearms are treated differently due to historical and cultural significance.
