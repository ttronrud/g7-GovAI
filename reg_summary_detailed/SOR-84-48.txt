### **5. Handling of Applications with Incorrect or Incomplete Recipient Data**

- If the recipient named in the application is **not a valid plan member** at the time of receipt:
  - The application is **retained by the Minister for 12 months**.
  - After this period, it may be dismissed or reprocessed if new information becomes available.

### **6. Notification of Diversion Approval (Section 34)**

The Minister issues a **notification** that includes:

- Statement that a duly completed application has been made for a recipient domiciled in a specific province, territory, or foreign country.
- The address of the recipient to be used for future payments.
- Confirmation that a diversion of a stated amount or portion of the net pension benefit will be made **no later than a specified date**.
- Right of the recipient to apply at any time to **vary or terminate** the diversion under the Act and these Regulations.

> üìé The notification must be accompanied by:

### **7. Diversion of Lump Sum and Periodic Payments**

The rules for calculating diverted amounts depend on the structure of the pension benefit and the financial support order.

#### **Case 1: Lump Sum Payment Paid Before Periodic Payments**
> Treat as if only a **lump sum** is payable.  
> Apply the relevant provisions of the Act (section 36) as if only a lump sum exists.

#### **Case 2: Lump Sum and Periodic Payments Paid Simultaneously**

##### **A. Order provides only for lump sum payment**:
- First, satisfy the lump sum from the **lump sum portion** of the net pension benefit (per section 36).
- If any remains unsatisfied, treat the remainder as a **new lump sum** to be satisfied under section 37 (as if only periodic payments exist).

##### **B. Order provides only for periodic payments**:
- Satisfy the payments from the **periodic portion** of the net pension benefit (section 36).

##### **C. Order provides for both lump sum and periodic payments**:
- **Lump sum portion**: Satisfied from the lump sum portion of net pension benefit (section 36).
- **Periodic portion**: Satisfied from the periodic portion (section 36).

> üìå This ensures that the financial support order is fulfilled in a structured, equitable manner based on the actual payment structure.
