### 1. **Purpose and Scope of the Agreement**
- To promote cooperation in the field of social security.
- To ensure that individuals who have contributed to or resided in either country are entitled to benefits from the other country under **equal and fair conditions**.

> **Core Principle**: Mutual recognition of eligibility and rights to benefits regardless of where a person resides.
