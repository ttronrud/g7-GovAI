## ðŸ“ Reporting Obligations

### 1. **Initial Notice Submission (Within 90 Days)**
A **responsible person** must submit a notice to the Minister within **90 days** of either of the following events:

- The **total quantity** of a substance at a facility reaches or exceeds the threshold in **Column 4** of Part 1 or 2 of Schedule 1.
- A quantity is placed in a **container system** whose **maximum capacity** is equal to or greater than the threshold in Column 4.

> The notice must include information in **Schedule 2**.
