**Detailed Summary of the Regulation:**

**Regulation Title:**  
Order Fixing the Day for the Purposes of Section 143 of the Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 1  
**Statutory Reference:**  
S.C. 2014, c. 20  
**Issuing Authority:**  
His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Labour  

---

### **Purpose of the Regulation**

This statutory instrument establishes the specific date that will serve as the "day" referenced in **Section 143** of the *Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 11* (the "Act"). Section 143 of the Act likely pertains to a time-bound provision related to economic policy implementation, such as the commencement of certain measures, the deadline for compliance, or the effective date of specific actions taken under the economic plan.

By fixing a specific date, this Order ensures legal clarity and certainty regarding when the provisions of Section 143 are to take effect, which is essential for administrative, fiscal, or regulatory planning.

---

### **Key Provisions**

1. **Fixed Date:**  
   The day designated under Section 143 of the Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 11 is officially set as **December 1, 2018**.

2. **Effective Date of the Order:**  
   The Order comes into force on:
   - The **first day** on which **Sections 111, 139, and 140** of the Economic Action Plan 2014 Act, No. 11 are all in force; **OR**
   - If the Order is registered (officially published and recognized) **after** that day, then it comes into force on the **day of registration**.

   This provision ensures that the Order only takes effect when the relevant enabling provisions of the Act are already operational, thereby preventing premature or inconsistent application.

3. **Note on Enforcement and Legal Status:**  
   The Order is noted to have come into force on **February 11, 2015**, as confirmed by subsequent statutory instruments SI/2015-5 and SI/2015-6. This indicates that despite being issued in 2014, the Order was formally activated in early 2015 due to the timing of its registration and the conditions for its effective date.
