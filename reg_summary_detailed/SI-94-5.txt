### ðŸ”¹ **2. Purpose of the Agreement**

The primary objective is to **promote cooperation in the field of social security** by:

- Establishing **mutual recognition of benefits**.
- Ensuring **non-reduction or suspension** of benefits due to residency in the other country.
- Enabling **totalization of creditable periods** (contributions or residence) for pension eligibility.
- Providing **clear rules for determining eligibility and benefit amounts** across both jurisdictions.
- Facilitating **cooperation between administrative authorities**.
