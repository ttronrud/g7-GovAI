## ğŸ’¡ Purpose of the Investment Limits

- To **prevent overexposure** to high-risk investments (especially in real estate or equity).
- To ensure that **liabilities (especially long-term annuities and participating policies)** are adequately backed by capital.
- To maintain **financial resilience** in the face of market downturns or adverse claims.
- To promote **prudent risk management** across the insurance sector.

## ğŸ¢ Regulatory Capital and Subsidiary Investments (Section 506 Subsection)

For the purpose of determining the investment limits, the **regulatory capital** of a company includes:

1. **Regulatory capital of the company itself**.
2. **Excess of total assets over total liabilities** (including deferred taxes and subordinated debt) in:
