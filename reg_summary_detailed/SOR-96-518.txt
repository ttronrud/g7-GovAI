## ðŸ” **Purpose of the Regulations**

These regulations govern the **superannuation rights of public servants who cease employment in the federal public service and are subsequently employed by an airport authority** as a result of the transfer of an airport to that authority (e.g., a private corporation or body).

They establish transitional rules to ensure **fair treatment of public servants** whose employment transitions from the federal government to a private airport operator, particularly regarding pension eligibility, service credit, age calculations, death benefits, and child allowances.
