## âš–ï¸ 2. **Clarifications and Exceptions**

To avoid ambiguity:

- An **application for reissue of a patent** is **not** considered an application for a patent.
- A **patent granted based on a divisional application** (resulting from a split of an original application) is **not** considered a patent granted on the basis of the original application.

> This clarifies the distinction between original and derivative patents.

## ðŸ•’ 3. **Extension of Time Periods for Fees and Actions**

The **Commissioner of Patents** has broad authority to **extend time periods** for various actions â€” including fee payments â€” under specific conditions.

### A. General Extension Authority (Section 3)

The Commissioner may extend any time period for doing something (e.g., paying fees, responding to requests) **if**:
- The circumstances justify the extension
- The extension is **applied for before the period expires**
- The **fee in item 1 of Schedule 2** is paid (unless otherwise specified)

> This applies to most procedural deadlines.

### B. Extension for Fee Payments â€“ Specific Cases

#### (1) When Small Entity Fee Was Paid
Applies to fees under:
- Subsections 44(1), 68(1)/(2), 80(1), 85.1(5), 86(1)/(1.1)/(6)/(10)/(12), 112(1), 112(5)(a)/(c), 154(1)(c), 154(2), or 154(3)(a)(iii)/(b)(i)/(ii)

**Conditions for extension:**
- Small entity fee was paid **before the deadline**
- It is later determined that **standard fee should have been paid**
- Applicant/patentee files a **statement** declaring:
  - The small entity fee was paid in **good faith**
  - The extension is filed **without undue delay** after becoming aware of the error
- The applicant pays the **difference** between:
  - Small entity fee paid
  - Standard fee applicable on the day of payment
- Pays the **fee in item 1 of Schedule 2**

> âš ï¸ If the Commissioner waives the difference payment under **section 139.1**, the requirement is waived.

#### (2) When Payment Was Insufficient (e.g., under subsection 80(1))
Applies when:
- Applicant paid **insufficient amount** (not due to error)
- Commissioner sent a notice acknowledging request for examination under **subsection 35(2)**
- It was later determined that the payment was insufficient
- Applicant files a **statement** declaring:
  - Payment was **unintentional**
  - Extension is filed **without undue delay**
- Pays the **difference** between:
  - Amount paid
  - Amount due on the day of insufficient payment

#### (3) When Commissioner Provided Erroneous Fee Information
Applies when:
- Commissioner gave **erroneous written information** about fee amount
- Applicant paid **insufficient amount** due to error
- Applicant identifies **source of error** and files a statement
- Extension is filed **without undue delay**
- Pays the **difference** between paid and correct amount
- Commissioner may **waive** the difference under section 139.1

> If extended, the fee is **deemed paid** on the day the **small entity fee** or **insufficient payment** was made.

> âŒ **Exception**: Subsection (5) does **not apply** to determining the **date of payment** for subsection 128(1)

#### (4) Extension of Period under Subsection 18(2) of the Act
The Commissioner may extend the period for compliance with subsection 18(2) (e.g., for filing or responding) if:
- Circumstances justify it
- Extension is applied for **before expiry**
- Fee in **item 1 of Schedule 2** is paid
