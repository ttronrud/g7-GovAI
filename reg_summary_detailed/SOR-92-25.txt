### 2. **Lease Duration and Renewal (Section 3(3))**

- **Initial term**: Up to 42 years.
- **Renewals**: Total term (initial + renewals) **cannot exceed 49 years**.
- **Exception**: If the land is part of a **duly registered condominium plan in Alberta**, the 49-year limit **does not apply**.

> ðŸ“Œ This ensures long-term stability while preventing indefinite monopolization of park land.

### 3. **Survey and Land Description Requirements**

A lease **cannot be granted unless**:

- The land has been **surveyed under the Canada Lands Surveys Act**, and
- The lease description is based on an **official plan** under that Act, **OR**
- The lease uses an **approved explanatory plan** (by Surveyor General), or
- A **metes and bounds description** (or equivalent) approved by the Surveyor General.

> âœ… Ensures legal clarity, accuracy, and consistency in land boundaries.

### 4. **Release Fee (Section 4)**

At lease inception, the lessee must pay a **release fee** equal to the **market value of the leasehold interest**.

- For leases under **Article 9.4 of the Banff Incorporation Agreement**, the fee is determined per that article.

> ðŸ” The market value is determined via **appraisal** or comparison to similar leasehold interests.
