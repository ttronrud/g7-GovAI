> **Earnings = Amount received from sale of catch**, after:
> - Deducting the value of any portion of the catch **not caught** by the crew,
> - Deducting **25% of the gross value** of the catch (if the fisher owns or leases the vessel or employs others),
> - Deducting **wages and shares** of other crew members.

- **Value of uncaught portion** is set by the **head fisher** or, if agent is employer, by the agent.

- If records are inadequate, the **Department of National Revenue (DNR)** must estimate earnings using:
