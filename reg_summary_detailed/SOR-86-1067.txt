### ðŸ”¹ **10. Suspension or Cancellation of Licence**

The Minister (or a designated person) may **suspend or cancel** a licence if the customs broker:
- Contravenes any **Act of Parliament or regulation** relating to import/export of goods  
- Acts to **defraud Her Majesty or a client**
- Suggests a plan to **evade duties or debts** to Canada (involving contravention of laws)
- Fails to comply with these regulations
- Becomes **insolvent or bankrupt**
- Engages in **dishonest conduct**
- Ceases to carry on business or fails to perform duties competently
- Is no longer qualified under these regulations
