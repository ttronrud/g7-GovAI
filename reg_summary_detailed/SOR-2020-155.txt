## ðŸ” Core Purpose of the Regulations

To define **what constitutes an "originating good" or "originating material"** â€” meaning goods or materials that are **produced in one of the three CUSMA countries (Canada, Mexico, or the U.S.)** and meet specific criteria that allow them to qualify for **preferential tariff treatment** under CUSMA.

- The **production process**
- The **location of production**
