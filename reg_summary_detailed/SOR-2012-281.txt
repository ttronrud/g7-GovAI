## üìö **Detailed Summary of the Eligible Mortgage Loan Regulations**

### üîπ **1. Regulatory Authority and Purpose**
The Minister of Finance, in consultation with the Governor of the Bank of Canada and the Superintendent of Financial Institutions, issued these regulations under **subsection 42(1)** of the *Protection of Residential Mortgage or Hypothecary Insurance Act*.  
**Purpose**: To define and establish the eligibility criteria for residential mortgage or hypothecary loans that are **not insured under a policy deemed to be a "policy" under section 19** of the Act. These regulations ensure that mortgage loans meet standards for affordability, risk, transparency, and borrower protection.

> ‚öñÔ∏è **Key Point**: The regulations apply to **all mortgage or hypothecary loans not covered by insurance under section 19**, meaning loans that are either uninsured or fall outside the scope of standard mortgage insurance.

### üîπ **3. Scope of Application**

These regulations apply to:
- All **mortgage or hypothecary loans** that are **not insured under a contract of insurance** that could be considered a policy under **section 19** of the Protection Act.

> üìå **Exception**: Loans that are modified or restructured in specific ways (e.g., loan workouts, refinancing) may still be eligible under certain conditions.

---

### üîπ **4. Eligibility Criteria: General Principles**

A mortgage loan is **eligible** only if it meets the criteria in **section 4**, and where applicable, **section 5, 6, or 6.1**.

#### ‚úÖ **General Eligibility Requirements**
- Must be **underwritten and administered by a qualified mortgage lender**.
- Must be **held in a registered retirement savings plan (RRSP)** or **registered retirement income fund (RRIF)** *only if* administered by a qualified mortgage lender.
- Must be **secured in first or second priority** by an eligible residential property.

> The 600 score requirement **does not apply** if **no more than 3%** of the lender‚Äôs HRLs and LRLs approved and funded during any of the following periods had borrowers/guarantors with scores <600:  
> - First 4 quarters of the last 5 quarters  
> - First 4 quarters of the last 6 quarters  
> - First 4 quarters of the last 7 quarters  

> üîç **Interest Rate Calculation for GDS/TDS**:  
> Used to calculate ratios based on **the greater of**:  
> - Loan interest rate + 2%  
> - **5.25%**  
> This ensures borrowers are assessed under stress conditions (e.g., rising interest rates).

> üìù **Income Verification Requirement**:  
> Lender or insurer must make **reasonable efforts to verify income and employment status** (or plausibility of self-employed income). Failure to do so invalidates the loan's eligibility.
