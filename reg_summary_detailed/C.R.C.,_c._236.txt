**4. Authority to Impose Levies and Charges**  
A central provision of the Order grants the Commodity Board the power to:

**7. Scope and Limitations**  
- The Order applies **only to turnips** produced in Prince Edward Island.
- It does **not extend** to other vegetables or crops.
- It applies only to **persons and property within the province**.
- The powers are **derived from and limited by** the *Natural Products Marketing Act* and the approved marketing plan.

**8. Purpose and Policy Implications**  
The overall goal of this Order is to:

- Ensure fair and stable marketing conditions for turnip producers
- Protect producers from market volatility
- Promote equitable distribution of income
- Provide a mechanism for financial resilience through levies and reserves
- Support the economic viability of the turnip industry in Prince Edward Island

It functions as a **market stabilization tool** and a **producer support mechanism**, blending regulatory control with economic policy.
