**Detailed Summary of the Canadian Forces Grievance Board Rules of Procedure (Review of a Grievance by Way of a Hearing)**  
*Issued: July 24, 2000 (S.C. 1998, c. 35, s. 7)*

---

### **Overview**

These Rules of Procedure govern the conduct of hearings before the **Canadian Forces Grievance Board (Grievance Board)** for the review of grievances under **Section 29 of the National Defence Act (Act)**. The rules are designed to ensure that grievance reviews are conducted **fairly, efficiently, informally, and expeditiously**, while maintaining procedural integrity and access to justice for members of the Canadian Armed Forces.

The Chairperson of the Grievance Board issued these rules pursuant to **paragraph 29.26(1)(a)** of the National Defence Act, which empowers the Board to establish procedural rules for hearing grievances.
