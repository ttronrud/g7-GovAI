## üìö **Detailed Summary of the Book Importation Regulations**  
*(Under the Copyright Act, S.C. 1997, c. 24)*

---

### üîπ **1. Legal Authority and Purpose**

- **Enacted by**: His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Industry.
- **Legal Basis**: Pursuant to specific provisions in the *Copyright Act*, particularly:
  - Section 2.6b (definition of "exclusive distributor")
  - Subsections 27.1(5)c and (6)c
  - Section 62d of the Copyright Act
- **Objective**: To establish clear rules governing the importation of foreign books and Canadian editions into Canada, particularly in relation to **exclusive distribution rights**, **distribution timelines**, **pricing**, and **exceptions for special cases** such as remaindered books, damaged books, textbooks, and special orders.

> These regulations ensure that **exclusive distributors**‚Äîwho hold territorial rights to sell certain books in Canada‚Äîdo not unfairly restrict access to books by retailers, libraries, or other buyers, while still protecting the rights of copyright holders.

### üîπ **3. Scope of Application**

The regulations apply to:

1. **English- and French-language books** imported into Canada where:
   - Separate and distinct Canadian territorial rights have been created or contracted.
2. **Canadian editions** imported into Canada.

> **Clarification**: The rules apply to books added to an order *after* the regulations came into force, even if the order was placed *before* the regulations were enacted.

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important Limitation**: The regulations **do not authorize any act that would infringe copyright** under subsection 27(2) of the *Copyright Act*.

### üîπ **4. Notice Requirements for Exclusive Distributors**

To prevent unauthorized imports, exclusive distributors must provide **advance notice** to retailers or institutions before an order is placed.

#### ‚úÖ When and How to Provide Notice:

| Recipient Type | Method of Notice |
|---------------|------------------|
| **Retailer (not a bookseller)** | In writing (courier, mail, facsimile, or electronic means). |
| **Bookseller, Library, or Profit-Based Institution** | Must be included in one of the following: <br> - The latest edition of: <br>   - *Canadian Telebook Agency Microfiche and Books in Print Plus ‚Äì Canadian Edition* (for English books), or <br>   - *Banque de titres de langue fran√ßaise* (for French books); <br> OR <br> - A catalogue supplied by the exclusive distributor, copyright owner, or licensee ‚Äî at the institution‚Äôs request and in the form requested. |

#### üì¨ Delivery & Receipt Rules:

- **Delivered by courier**: Received on the day of delivery.
- **Sent by mail**: Received on the **10th day after mailing**.
- **Sent by facsimile/electronic means**: Received on the **date and time indicated** by the system.

> üîÑ **Notice is valid and binding** until revoked or amended by the exclusive distributor, copyright owner, or licensee.

#### üöÄ Exception: Bulk Representation

An exclusive distributor representing **all titles** of a specific publisher may send a **single notice** to all relevant parties (retailers, booksellers, institutions) at their last known address via courier, mail, or electronic means.

> This simplifies compliance for large publishers with multiple titles.

### üîπ **5. Distribution Criteria for Imported Books**

These rules govern **when** and **how** books must be delivered and priced.

#### ‚úÖ General Delivery Timelines:

| Source Country | Delivery Time After Order Received |
|---------------|-------------------------------------|
| **United States** (in stock) | Within **3 days** (English) / **5 days** (French) |
| **United States** (not in stock) | Within **12 days** |
| **Europe** (not in stock) | Within **30 days** (English) / **60 days** (French) |
| **Any other country** (not in stock) | Within **50 days** |

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Holiday Rule**: If a period is less than 7 days, holidays are **not counted** in the timeline.

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **End of Period**: If a deadline is specified, it ends at the **close of business** on that day.

#### ‚úÖ Format and Pricing Requirements:

- **Format**: Must be provided **as requested**, if available.
- **Pricing**:
  - **From the U.S.**:  
    List price in USD + current exchange rate + **10%** of converted price (after exchange), minus discounts.
  - **From Europe or other countries**:  
    List price in foreign currency + current exchange rate + **15%** of converted price (after exchange), minus discounts.

> üìå **Price Compliance**: Must not exceed the calculated amount.  
> üìå **Market Discounts**: For library purchases, discounts must reflect **generally prevailing North American market conditions**.

#### ‚úÖ Order Confirmation Requirements:

- **By phone**: Within **2 days** of order placement.
- **By mail or facsimile**: Within **5 days**.
- **By electronic means (non-facsimile)**: Within **1 day** of order placement.

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Failure to Confirm**: If the exclusive distributor cannot confirm the order within the required time, the order may be filled by another party.

### üîπ **6. Distribution Criteria for Canadian Editions**

Canadian editions must meet strict visibility and availability standards before being ordered.

#### ‚úÖ Requirements Before Order Placement:

1. The Canadian edition must be:
   - Clearly marked on the **cover or jacket** as a "Canadian edition".
2. It must be:
   - Listed in the latest edition of:
     - *Canadian Telebook Agency Microfiche and Books in Print Plus ‚Äì Canadian Edition* (English), or
     - *Banque de titres de langue fran√ßaise* (French), OR
   - Listed in a **catalogue** supplied by the exclusive distributor, copyright owner, or licensee ‚Äî at the request of the bookseller/institution.

> This ensures transparency and prevents unauthorized importation of non-Canadian editions.

### üîπ **7. Exceptions to Exclusive Distribution (Permitted Imports)**

Certain books may be imported **without going through the exclusive distributor**, even if they are otherwise restricted.

#### ‚úÖ 1. **Remained Books**
- A book may be imported if:
  - It is **marked as remaindered** (sold below production cost with no royalty), OR
  - The original foreign publisher has notified the exclusive distributor that it is remaindered.
- The book **must not be sold in Canada before 60 days** after first being offered for sale as a remaindered book in the country of origin.

#### ‚úÖ 2. **Damaged Books**
- A book may be imported if:
  - It is **marked as damaged** by the importer or retailer.

#### ‚úÖ 3. **Books for Re-export**
- A book may be imported solely for re-export if:
  - The importer can provide **evidence on request** that an order for re-export has been made **before importation**.
