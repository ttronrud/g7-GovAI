## ðŸ“„ **3. Fees for Individual Licences (Section 2.1 to 6)**

### **General Rule (Section 2.1)**
- For individuals applying for or renewing a **possession licence** (listed in **Column 1 of Schedule 1**), the fee is the **appropriate fee** specified in **Column 2** of Schedule 1.

### **Multiple Classes of Firearm (Section 2.2)**
- If a **possession and acquisition licence** covers **more than one class of firearm**, the fee is the **highest** of the applicable fees listed in **Item 4 of Schedule 1**.

### **Waiver for Non-Restricted Firearm Licences (Section 2.3)**
- Despite Section 3, the fee for:
  - Renewal of a **possession licence solely for non-restricted firearms**, or
  - Issuance/renewal of a **possession and acquisition licence solely for non-restricted firearms**
  
  is **waived** during a specified period **if**:
  - The individual previously held a **possession licence for firearms** (excluding those for individuals under 18 years old) **on or after December 1, 1998**.

> âš ï¸ This waiver does **not apply** if:
> - The previous licence was **revoked** for reasons **other than those in subsection 72(1.1) of the Firearms Act**, and
> - The revocation was **not cancelled**.

### **Waiver for Minors (Under 18 Years Old)**
- The fee for **renewal of a possession licence** for individuals under 18 is **waived** during the same period (September 18, 2012 â€“ May 16, 2013), **if** the individual is eligible under **subsection 8(2) of the Act**.

### **Fee Reduction for New Applicants with Short Licence History**
- **If an individual has held a possession licence for 3 years or less**, and applies for a **possession and acquisition licence**, the fee is **reduced by 50%**.
- **If an individual has held a possession and acquisition licence (solely for non-restricted firearms) for 3 years or less**, and applies for a licence for **restricted or prohibited firearms**, the fee is **reduced by 50%**.

### **Waiver for Hunting/Trapping Purposes**
- The fee for:
  - Issuance or renewal of a **possession licence for individuals under 18**
  - Renewal of a **possession licence for non-restricted firearms**

## ðŸ¢ **4. Fees for Business Licences (Section 7)**

### **General Rule**
- For businesses applying for or renewing a licence to carry on an activity listed in **Column 1 of Schedule 2**, the fee is the **appropriate fee** in **Column 2** of Schedule 2.

### **Multiple Activities in One Location**
- If a business operates **two or more activities** in the same location, the fee is the **highest** of the applicable fees for those activities.

### **Waiver for Wholesale Ammunition Business**
- If a business **only engages in wholesale selling and manufacturing of ammunition**, and:
  - Uses a **licensed factory or magazine** (as defined in Section 2 of the *Explosives Act*),
  
  then the fee for the licence is **waived**.

## ðŸ“ **5. Fees for Authorizations and Confirmations (Section 8)**

### **General Rule**
- For authorizations or confirmations listed in **Column 1 of Schedule 3**, the fee is the appropriate amount in **Column 2**.

### **Waiver for Repeated Authorizations in Same Profession**
- If an individual has:
  - Paid a fee for an **authorization to carry** for a **lawful profession or occupation**,
  - And that authorization was valid for **one year or less**,
  - And within **12 months** of issuance, they request **another authorization** for the same purpose,

  then the **fee for the second authorization is waived**.

> âš ï¸ This provision was **repealed** by **SOR/2004-272, s. 5**.
