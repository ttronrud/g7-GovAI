### 2. **Prohibition of Harmful Substances**

> **Section Principle:** No fertilizer or supplement may contain substances in quantities that pose a risk to human, animal, or plant health or the environment â€” **except for pests** â€” if used **as directed** or in **amounts necessary** to achieve intended purposes.

- A substance is prohibited if:
  - It could cause **immediate or long-term harm** to the environment;
  - It constitutes a **danger to human life or health**;
  - It threatens **ecosystems on which human life depends**.

> This reflects a precautionary principle in chemical safety regulation.

### 3. **Exemptions from Registration and Application**

The following fertilizers and supplements are **exempt** from registration under the Act and these regulations:

| Exemption | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Manure** | Natural manure sold without processing, conforming to standard in paragraph 9(a), **except** if it is a fertilizer or supplement listed in section 2.1 |
| **Manufacturing-use fertilizers** | Imported or manufactured in Canada for **manufacturing purposes only**, requiring further treatment (e.g., mixing, blending, repackaging, seeding) â€” *not* for end-use |
| **Export-only fertilizers** | Fertilizers not intended for sale or use in Canada, intended for export and labeled accordingly â€” *exempt except for section 5.5* |
| **Experimental-use fertilizers** | Fertilizers or supplements imported or manufactured in Canada for **experimental purposes** â€” exempt from the Act and all regulations **except sections 23.1 to 23.4** |
| **Experimental fertilizers (non-pesticide)** | Fertilizers used experimentally (not pesticides) are exempt from **section 3 of the Act** and other provisions **except section 2.1**, **provided** that residual fertilizer and all plants grown during the experiment are destroyed at the end |

> These exemptions allow flexibility for research, agriculture trials, and international trade.

### 4. **Exemptions from Registration Based on Composition**

#### A. Fertilizers (Exempt if no seeds/growing media)
A fertilizer is exempt from registration **if**:
- It does **not** contain:
  - A substance derived from a living organism
  - A pesticide
  - A supplement not registered and not in the *List of Materials*
  - A registered supplement whose directions for use differ from the product
  - An unregistered micronutrient fertilizer
  - A registered micronutrient fertilizer with inconsistent directions
- It is **listed in the List of Materials**
- It is a **customer formula fertilizer**
- Its active ingredients are **only mixtures** of exempt or registered fertilizers/supplements

#### B. Supplements (Exempt if no seeds/growing media)
A supplement is exempt from registration **if**:
- It is listed in the *List of Materials*
- Its active ingredients are a mixture where:
  - Each component is exempt or registered for the proposed use
  - If the mixture contains **viable microorganisms**, it is **not further cultured or manipulated**

#### C. Seeds Treated with Fertilizers/Supplements
Seeds treated with fertilizers or supplements are exempt from registration **if**:
- Each fertilizer or supplement used is **exempt** or **registered for use with those seeds**

#### D. Growing Media with Fertilizers/Supplements
Growing media containing fertilizers or supplements is exempt from registration **if**:
- Each ingredient is **exempt** or **registered for use in growing media**, and
- Its directions for use are consistent with the growing media

> These provisions ensure that only necessary or safe products require formal registration.

### 5. **Application Process for Registration**

Every application for registration must:

- Be submitted to the **President of the Agency (CFIA)**
- Include:
  - The **text of the label** to be used in the marketplace
  - Any other information needed to determine:
    - Whether it is a fertilizer or supplement
    - Its composition
    - Its safety

> **Special Requirements for Pesticide-Containing Products:**
- Must include proof that the fertilizer-pesticide or supplement-pesticide:
  - Complies with the *Pest Control Products Act* (PCPA) regarding human, animal, or plant health and environment
  - Includes any **Minister of Health decisions** related to pesticide components
