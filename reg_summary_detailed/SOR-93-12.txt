## üìÖ What Constitutes ‚ÄúMaintenance‚Äù of a Child?

A child is considered to be **maintained by an applicant in a given month** if **one of the following conditions applies**:

### Option 1: **Primary Care and Maintenance**
> The applicant, at the end of the month, provides for the child‚Äôs:
- Care
- Maintenance
- Education
- Training
- Advancement  
> To a **greater extent than any other department, agency, institution, Indigenous governing body, or individual**.

> ‚úÖ This establishes a standard of primary responsibility ‚Äî the applicant must be the **primary provider** of support.

### Option 2: **Transfer to Guardian or Custodian**
> The applicant is an entity listed in paragraphs 3(1)(a) to (c) of the Act and:
> - The child was **previously in foster care** or maintained by such an entity
> - The child has now been placed in **permanent or temporary custody** of:
>   - A guardian
>   - A tutor
>   - Another individual fulfilling a similar role
>   - Under a **court decree, order, or judgment** of a competent tribunal, or

> ‚úÖ This provision allows for continuity of support when children transition into formal guardianship or custody arrangements.
