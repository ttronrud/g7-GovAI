This regulation replaces the previous *Foreign Organizations Remission Order, C.R.C., c. 766*. It establishes a framework for the **remission (exemption)** of **customs duty, excise tax, and sales tax** on specific goods imported into Canada by **foreign organizations** for use in **meetings or conventions** held in Canada.

The policy aims to:
- Facilitate international business and cultural events.
- Reduce administrative and financial burdens on foreign organizations hosting events in Canada.
- Promote the free flow of goods related to meetings and conventions, while maintaining compliance with tax and customs laws.

The Order is made under the authority of the **Minister of National Revenue** and the **Treasury Board**, and is justified as being in the **public interest**.
