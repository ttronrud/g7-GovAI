These regulations establish the **framework for assessing financial institutions** under the *Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions Act (OSFI Act)*. They are made by **His Excellency the Governor General in Council**, on the recommendation of the **Minister of Finance**, pursuant to:

### âœ… **3.2 Inflation Adjustment (Subsection 2(2))**

The minimum assessment is **adjusted annually for inflation** using the **Consumer Price Index (CPI)**.

#### Formula:
$$
\text{Adjusted Minimum Assessment} = \text{Previous Year's Minimum} \times \left( \frac{\text{CPI}_{\text{current year}}}{\text{CPI}_{\text{previous year}}} \right)
$$

Where:
- **CPI** = Average of the 12-month CPI values for Canada (as published by Statistics Canada).
- The result is **rounded to the nearest multiple of $10**.

#### Exception:
- If the CPI ratio is **less than 1**, **no adjustment** is made â€” the prior yearâ€™s minimum applies.
- This rule **does not apply** to the fiscal year beginning **April 1, 2016** (a transitional provision).
