### ðŸ”¹ V. **Permanent Establishment (PE)**

#### 1. **Definition**
A **permanent establishment** is a fixed place of business through which an enterprise conducts business.

#### 2. **Examples of PE**
- Place of management;
- Branch;
- Office;
- Factory;
- Shop;
- Workshop;
- Mine, oil/gas well, quarry, or site for natural resource exploration/exploitation.

#### 3. **Exclusions (Not a PE)**
The following **do not constitute** a permanent establishment:
- Use of facilities solely for **storage, display, or delivery** of goods;
- Maintenance of stock **solely for storage, display, or delivery**;
- Maintenance of stock **solely for processing by another enterprise**;
- Stock displayed at **temporary trade fairs** and sold after fair ends;
- Fixed place used **only for purchasing goods or collecting information**;
- Fixed place used **only for preparatory or auxiliary activities**;
- Any combination of the above, **if the overall activity is preparatory or auxiliary**.

#### 4. **Special Rules**
- **Agents of independent status** (e.g., brokers, commission agents) do **not** create a PE, even if they have authority to conclude contracts.
- **Insurance companies**:

- **Control or ownership** between companies in different countries **does not** create a PE.
