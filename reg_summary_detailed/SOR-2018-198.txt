## ðŸ“Š Fee Structure and Adjustments

### 1. **Annual Fee Adjustments (Inflation Adjustment)**
- All fees in **Sections 3 to 5** are **adjusted annually on April 1**.
- The adjustment is based on the **percentage change** in the **April All-items Consumer Price Index (CPI)** for Canada over the **previous fiscal year**.
- The new fee is **rounded up to the next highest dollar**.
- This ensures fees keep pace with inflation.

> *Example*: If CPI increases by 3.5%, all applicable fees increase by 3.5% (rounded up).
