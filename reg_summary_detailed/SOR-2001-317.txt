## ðŸ“ Reporting Obligations

### 1. **Who Must Report?**
Any **person or entity** involved in a financial transaction or attempted transaction that raises **reasonable suspicion** of money laundering, terrorist financing, or sanctions evasion.

> âœ… Applies to **financial entities**, casinos, money services businesses, and others defined in the Act.

---

### 2. **When Must a Report Be Made?**

| Type of Transaction | Reporting Requirement |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| **Financial transaction** (under **section 7**) | Must be reported **as soon as practicable** after reasonable measures confirm suspicion. |
| **Attempted transaction** (under **section 7.1**) | Must be reported **immediately**. |

> âš ï¸ "As soon as practicable" means promptly, not necessarily within hours, but within a reasonable timeframe after suspicion arises.

### 4. **Exemptions from Reporting**

The following **exemptions apply**:

| Exemption | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Failure to obtain data** | If reasonable measures are taken but the information cannot be obtained, the item is not required to be reported. |
| **Attempted transaction** | For items marked with an asterisk, exemption applies if information cannot be obtained. |
| **Disclosure would inform counterparty** | If reporting would **reveal the transaction to a third party** (e.g., a customer), the reporting obligation does **not apply**. |

> This protects privacy and prevents disclosure of sensitive commercial or personal information to parties involved in the transaction.

---

### 5. **Format and Submission Requirements**

| Requirement | Details |
|----------|--------|
| **Electronic format preferred** | Reports must be sent **electronically** if the sender has the technical capability. |
| **Paper format fallback** | If electronic submission is not possible, the report must be sent in **paper form**, following Centre guidelines. |
| **Retention period** | The reporting person/entity must **retain a copy of the report for at least 5 years** after submission. |
| **Machine-readable format** | A machine-readable copy is acceptable **if a paper copy can be readily produced**. |
| **Employer/contractual exception** | If the copy belongs to an employer or contractual partner, the individual is **not required to retain it after employment or contract ends**. |

> All these details are **prescribed as mandatory** in the context of suspicious activity reporting.
