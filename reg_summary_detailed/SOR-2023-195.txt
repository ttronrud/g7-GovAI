## ðŸ¢ **Who is Subject to Assessment? (Prescribed Entities under subsection 51.1(3))**

Entities that must pay an assessment are categorized into four groups:

### 1. **Entities Covered by Banking or Insurance Laws**
- Every **bank** to which the *Bank Act* applies.
- Every **authorized foreign bank** (as defined in Section 2 of the *Bank Act*).
- Every **life company or foreign life company** to which the *Insurance Companies Act* applies.
- Every **company** to which the *Trust and Loan Companies Act* applies.

> These are large, regulated financial institutions with inherent compliance obligations.

### 2. **Entities That Made 500 or More Specified Reports in a Fiscal Year**
- Any **person or entity** (not covered in 1 above) that submitted **500 or more specified reports** during the fiscal year in which the expenses were incurred.

> This group includes smaller institutions, fintechs, or other businesses with significant transaction reporting activity.

> **Note**: If an entity made fewer than 500 reports, it is **not assessed** under this rule.
