### ðŸ”¹ **7. Key Purpose and Intent**

The primary objectives of these regulations are:

1. **Prevent consumption** of ethanol by making it unpalatable and toxic through denaturing.
2. **Ensure consistency and traceability** in the production and use of denatured alcohol across Canada.
3. **Support tax compliance** by clearly defining what constitutes "denatured" or "specially denatured" alcohol.
4. **Protect public health and safety** by using hazardous or unpleasant substances that deter human ingestion.
5. **Enable industrial use** (e.g., in solvents, fuels, cleaning agents) while maintaining regulatory oversight.
