## âœ… **Conditions for Receiving an Apprentice Loan**

An apprentice may receive a loan **only if** the following conditions are met:

1. **Loan Agreement & Training Period Alignment**  
   - The apprentice must have entered into an **apprentice loan agreement** for the technical training period **on or before the last day of training**.

2. **Confirmation of Enrolment**  
   - The apprentice must submit **proof of enrollment** in technical training with a technical training provider.

3. **Interest Payment (if applicable)**  
   - If interest had already started accruing **before** the beginning of the current technical training period:
     - The apprentice must pay the **unpaid interest** to the Minister **by the day before the first day of training**.
   - If interest started accruing **before** the apprentice entered the loan agreement for the current training:
     - Either:
       - Pay the unpaid interest to the day before the agreement date, **or**
       - Directly add that interest to the **outstanding principal balance**.

> âŒ *Note*: This clause was **repealed** in SOR/2023-273, s. 18 â€” so this may no longer be active. However, it is included in the original text and should be verified against current legislation.
