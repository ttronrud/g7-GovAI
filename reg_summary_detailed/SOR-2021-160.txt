## ðŸ” **Overview of the Regulation**

The **CRTC Accessibility Reporting Regulations** (2021) implement key accessibility obligations under the **Accessible Canada Act (S.C. 2019, c. 10)** for **broadcasting and telecommunications entities** in Canada. These regulations establish mandatory requirements for:

- Developing and publishing **accessibility plans**
- Establishing and publicizing **feedback processes**
- Submitting **progress reports**
- Ensuring accessibility in **digital formats** and **accessibility of information for persons with disabilities**

The regulations are designed to promote equitable access to information and services by ensuring that public-facing content and processes are accessible to all Canadians, including those with disabilities.
