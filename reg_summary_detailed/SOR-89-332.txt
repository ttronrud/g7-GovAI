## ğŸ’° Dock Charges and Financial Rules

### 6.1 **When Charges Apply**
- Charges apply when:
  - Vessel enters and exits the dock.
  - Services are provided (e.g., cleaning, preparation).
  - Vessel is docked (defined as: gate closed upon entry; cleaned and vessel leaves).

### 6.2 **Tonnage Calculation**
- Charges are based on **gross tonnage**:
  - If vessel has two tonnages: charge based on **greater tonnage**.
  - If vessel is **lengthened or modified**, charge is based on the **average of pre- and post-modification tonnages**.

### 6.3 **Charge Formula (Historical â€“ Pre-2019)**
- Base charges were set in the schedule (April 1, 2018 â€“ March 31, 2019).
- From **April 1, 2019 onward**, charges increased annually by:
  > **(Average of Government of Canada 10-year bond yields for first 6 months of prior year) + 1%**

> This formula reflects inflation adjustments tied to bond market performance.

### 6.4 **No Charges for Detention**
- No dock charges are due during detentions caused by:
  - Weather, ice, or tide.
  - Delay due to repairs to another vessel.
- **Exception**: If repairs are carried out during detention, charges apply.
