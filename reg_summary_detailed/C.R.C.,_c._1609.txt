## 2. **Prohibited Activities in Wildlife Areas (General Ban)**

Subject to permits under **Section 4 or 8.1**, **no person** may do any of the following in a wildlife area:

| Prohibited Activity | Explanation |
|---------------------|-----------|
| Introduce living organisms | Any organism that could harm wildlife or degrade habitat. |
| Hunt, fish, or trap | Unauthorized hunting or fishing is banned. |
| Possess hunting/fishing/trapping equipment | Even if not used, possession is prohibited. |
| Possess lead sinkers or lead jigs while fishing | Prohibited to reduce environmental contamination. |
| Possess wildlife, carcasses, nests, eggs, or parts thereof | Prevents illegal trade or disturbance. |
| Conduct agricultural activities | Grazing livestock or harvesting crops is banned. |
| Bring domestic animals with hooves into wildlife areas | Prevents spread of disease or disruption. |
| Allow domestic animals to run at large or keep them on a leash longer than 3 meters | Limits uncontrolled animal movement. |
| Carry out recreational activities | Includes swimming, camping, hiking, wildlife viewing, snowshoeing, cross-country skiing, skating. |
| Host group meals/events with 15+ people | Prevents overcrowding and disturbance. |
| Light or maintain fires | Fire risk to sensitive ecosystems. |
| Operate conveyances (except aircraft) | No vehicles, boats, or self-propelled devices. |
| Conduct aircraft take-offs/landings (including drones) | Includes remote piloted aircraft (drones). |
| Operate or set in motion autonomous/self-propelled devices | Prohibits drones, robots, or automated machinery. |
| Remove, damage, or destroy signs, fences, buildings, or structures | Preserves infrastructure and public messaging. |
| Sell or offer for sale goods/services | Prevents commercialization. |
| Carry out industrial activities | No mining, construction, or manufacturing. |
| Disturb soil, sand, gravel, or other materials | Prevents erosion and habitat destruction. |
| Dump or deposit waste or substances that degrade environment | Environmental protection. |
| Remove, damage, or destroy artifacts or natural objects | Protects cultural and ecological heritage. |
| Any other activity likely to disturb, damage, or destroy wildlife, habitat, or residence | Broad exception clause. |
