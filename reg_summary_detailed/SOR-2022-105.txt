### ðŸš« **3. Prohibited Activities (Without a Permit or Authorization)**

A person **must not**:
- Capture, kill, take, injure, or harass a migratory bird (including attempts);
- Destroy, take, or disturb an egg;
- Damage, destroy, remove, or disturb a nest, nest shelter, eider duck shelter, or duck box.

#### âœ… Exceptions to Nest/Structure Disturbance:
- A **nest shelter, eider duck shelter, or duck box** with **no live bird or viable egg** may be disturbed.
- A **nest not listed in Schedule 1** (i.e., not a protected species) may be disturbed if:
  - No live bird or viable egg is present.
- A **nest listed in Schedule 1** may be disturbed **only if**:
  1. Written notice is given to the Minister **at least X months in advance** (as per column 3 of the relevant table in Schedule 1);
  2. The nest has **not been used** by migratory birds since the notice was received.

> This promotes conservation while allowing limited access for research or land management.
