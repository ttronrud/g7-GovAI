### üîπ **Material and Structural Requirements**

- **Cards and postcards**:
  - Must be made of **cardboard or common printing material**.
  - Must **weigh at least 2 grams** to maintain structural integrity during automated sorting and handling.
- **Self-mailers**:
  - Must have **no outer envelope, wrapping, or cover** ‚Äî only the paper containing the message.
- **Envelopes or self-mailers**:
  - Must be **completely sealed** and **rectangular in shape**.
  - Cannot be open or partially sealed.

### üîπ **Customs and Duty Declarations (for High-Value Items)**

#### When to Apply:
- If the item may be subject to **customs duty** in the destination country.

#### For Delivery to the United States, its territories, and possessions:
- If the **value exceeds $500**, the sender must:
  1. Complete the **customs document required by Canada Post**.
  2. **Securely affix** this document to the address side of the item (if possible).

#### For Delivery to Any Country Other Than the United States:
- If the **value exceeds $500**, the sender must:
  1. Complete a **Customs Declaration Dispatch Note** (in the form specified in **Schedule III**).
  2. **Securely affix** this note to the address side of the item (if possible).

> ‚ö†Ô∏è These requirements are designed to ensure transparency and compliance with international customs laws.

### üîπ **Postcard-Specific Rules**

#### 1. **Mandatory Labeling**
- The words **‚ÄúPost card‚Äù**, **‚Äúpostcard‚Äù**, or **‚Äúcarte postale‚Äù** must appear on the **face** of all postcards **except illustrated postcards**.

#### 2. **Prohibited Attachments**
The following items **shall not be attached** to a postcard:
- Samples of merchandise or similar articles  
- Photographs  
- Cuttings of any kind  
- Fold-back sheets  
- Cloth, embroidery, spangles, or other decorative articles  

#### 3. **Prohibited Features**
- **No projecting or raised relief parts** (e.g., embossing, raised edges, 3D elements).

#### 4. **Permitted Attachments**
- Illustrations, stamps, labels, or address slips may be affixed **if**:
  - Made of **paper or very thin material**  
  - **Adheres completely** to the postcard  
  - For **illustrations or stamps**: affixed to the **back** or **left-hand portion** of the face  
  - For **labels or address slips**: affixed to the **back** or **left-hand portion or entire face**

#### 5. **Shape Requirements**
- All postcards must be **rectangular**.
- If not rectangular, they must be **enclosed in a rectangular envelope**.
