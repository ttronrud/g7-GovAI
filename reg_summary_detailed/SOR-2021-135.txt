## ðŸ“ **Safety Certificate Requirements**

### ðŸ”§ **For Safety Convention Vessels (Under Section 7)**
A vessel must hold **applicable safety certificates** issued under **Section 7** of the Act to engage in any voyage.

#### Required Safety Elements (Must meet under Part 4 of the Act):
- Structural integrity and machinery (main and auxiliary engines, boilers, pressure vessels)
- Watertight subdivision and load lines
- Structural fire protection, fire safety systems, and fire control plans
- Life-saving appliances (lifeboats, life rafts, rescue boats)
- Line-throwing appliances and radio equipment for life-saving operations
- Shipborne navigational equipment, pilot embarkation means, nautical charts
- Lights, shapes, and distress/sound signaling systems

#### Certificate Endorsement:
- The **authorized representative** of the vessel must ensure:
  - The vessel undergoes all required **inspections** under **Part B of Chapter I of SOLAS**.
  - If required, the **Minister endorses** the certificate after inspection to confirm ongoing compliance.

#### Exemptions:
- On application, the Minister may issue an **exemption certificate** if the **Marine Technical Review Board** has granted an exemption under **Section 28 of the Act**.

#### Continuous Synopsis Record (CSR):
- The authorized representative must apply for a **CSR** under **Regulation 5 of Chapter XI-1 of SOLAS**.
- Application must include information as per **Regulation 5.3**.
- Upon any change in vessel data (e.g., equipment, crew, modifications), the representative must:
  - Inform the Minister in writing
  - Submit a new application
  - Amend the CSR where required (Regulations 5.3.4 to 5.3.12)
- The CSR must **not be altered or defaced**.
- The record must be **kept on board** during:
  - Transfer to another flag
  - Sale
  - Change of operator
- After transfer to another state, the representative must **notify the Minister** of the new flag state so a copy can be forwarded.

  - Certificate must be **endorsed** after inspection to confirm continued compliance.
