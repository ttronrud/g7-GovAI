### 4. **Definition of "Control"**
Control is defined as **any actual or de facto influence** over a corporation â€” not just legal ownership.

- Ownership of securities
- Trusts, agreements, or arrangements

> **Key Implication**: Even if a non-Canadian does not own voting shares, if they exert **control through financial, contractual, or operational means**, the entity is deemed a **non-Canadian** and thus ineligible.

### 5. **Definition of "Independent Member"**
An independent member is:

- Free from conflicts of interest
