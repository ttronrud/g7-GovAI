### 1. **Mandatory Connection to Water/Sewer Mains**
> *Subsection 3(1):*  
> Every owner of a building on a lot that fronts or abuts a street with a water or sewer main **must connect directly** to that main.

#### Exceptions:
- Garages
- Detached structures designed to provide tourist accommodation
- Any building appurtenant to and improving the usefulness of a building already connected to a water/sewer main

  - Pay a **fee of $1 for every $1,000 or fraction thereof** of the estimated cost

### 1. **Owner Responsibilities**
- Pays **all costs** for the part of the connection **within their lot line**.
