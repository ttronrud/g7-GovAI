### ğŸš« **5. Prohibited Activities and Restrictions**

#### **(1) Building Height Restrictions**
No person may:
- Construct or erect a **building, structure, or object** (including additions to existing structures)
- Where the **highest point** of the object exceeds:
  - The elevation of the **approach surface** at that location
  - The elevation of the **outer surface**
  - The elevation of the **transitional surface**

> This ensures that no structure (e.g., towers, trees, buildings) blocks the line of sight or creates hazards during take-off or landing.

#### **(2) Interference with Signals or Communications**
No owner or lessee of land within the **outer surface** may:
- Permit any use or development that **interferes with signals or communications** to or from:
  - Aircraft
  - Aeronautical facilities (e.g., radar, radio towers, navigation systems)

> This protects the integrity of air traffic control and flight safety systems.

#### **(3) Natural Growth Restrictions**
No owner or lessee may allow **natural growth** (e.g., trees, shrubs, vegetation) to grow to a height that:
- Exceeds the elevation of any of the defined surfaces (approach, outer, transitional)

> This prevents vegetation from obstructing aircraft or interfering with radar and communication signals.

#### **(4) Bird Hazard Zone Prohibitions**
No owner or lessee within the **bird hazard zone** may:
- Permit activities or uses that **attract birds** (e.g., open fields, water sources, food waste, feeding areas)

> This reduces the risk of bird strikes, which can cause catastrophic damage to aircraft.
