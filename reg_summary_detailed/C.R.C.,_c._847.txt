## ðŸŒŠ II. Scope and Application

### Applies to:
- Canadian fisheries waters **in and adjacent to the Northwest Territories**
- Tidal waters of **Ontario and Manitoba**

### Does NOT apply to:
- Fishing carried out under the authority of **Aboriginal Communal Fishing Licences Regulations**

> This means that Indigenous communities may fish under separate, traditional or communal authority, outside the scope of these general regulations.
