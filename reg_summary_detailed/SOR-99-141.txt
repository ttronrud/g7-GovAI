### **3. When a Loan Is Considered Made**
- A loan is deemed made on the **day of the first disbursement** of funds by the lender.
- For **line of credit loans**, it is the day the line is **authorized**.

---

### **4. Borrower and Lender Relationship (Related Parties)**
A borrower is considered **related** if:
- One controls the other directly or indirectly (e.g., through shares >50% of voting rights);
- The other controls the first;
- They operate in partnership with each other;
- They share management, administrative, equipment, or overhead costs â€” **but not in a partnership**.

> âœ… **Exception**: Two businesses located at different premises are **not related** if neither derives more than **25% of actual or projected gross revenues** from the other.
