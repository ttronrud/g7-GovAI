**Detailed Summary of the Revenue Canada Technical Publication Subscription Service Fees Order**

**Regulation Title:**  
*Revenue Canada Technical Publication Subscription Service Fees Order*  
**Reference:** SI/93-8, 1993 Canada Gazette Part II, p. 181  
**Issued by:** The Minister of National Revenue  
**Date of Issuance:** February 1, 1993  
**Governing Order in Council:** P.C. 1992-2597, December 14, 1992  

---

### **Purpose of the Regulation**
This regulation establishes the fees that individuals or organizations must pay to the Receiver General of Canada when they receive subscription services from the Department of National Revenue (the "Department") for its technical publications related to taxation. The fees are designed to cover the administrative and operational costs associated with providing access to these publications.

---

### **Key Definitions**

1. **Department**  
   Refers to the *Department of National Revenue* (now known as *Revenue Canada*), which is responsible for administering Canada’s tax system and issuing official tax-related publications.

2. **Technical Publications**  
   Specifically includes:
   - *Interpretation bulletins*  
   - *Information circulars*  
   - *Advance tax rulings*  
   These documents provide guidance, clarification, and official interpretations of tax laws and regulations.

   > Note: The term excludes general administrative or non-tax-related materials.

---

### **Fees Applicable Under the Order**

The regulation sets out three distinct fee structures depending on the type of service requested:

#### **1. Subscription Services (Annual or Biennial)**
- **Service Description:**  
  A person may request a subscription to receive ongoing access to technical publications. The subscription covers publications issued in the **current year or the two years immediately following the request**.

- **Fees:**
  - **One-year subscription:** $20.00  
  - **Two-year subscription:** $35.00  

> This option allows users to receive regular updates to technical publications over a defined period, providing continuous access to evolving tax guidance.

#### **2. Complete Set of Current Publications (Prior to Request)**
- **Service Description:**  
  A person may request the *complete set of current technical publications* that were published **before the date of the request**.

- **Fee:** $65.00  

> This applies to a one-time, comprehensive delivery of all available technical publications up to that point in time. It is intended for users who need a full historical or current reference collection, such as tax professionals, legal advisors, or consultants.

---

### **Key Provisions and Implications**

- **Fee Payment Obligation:**  
  Any individual or entity receiving a subscription service or a complete set of publications must pay the prescribed fee directly to the **Receiver General of Canada**.

- **Request-Based Service:**  
  The fees are only applicable when the Department provides the service *at the request of the person*. No fee is charged if the service is provided without a formal request.

- **Timeframe for Publications:**  
  - For subscriptions: Covers publications from the current year or the next two years.  
  - For complete sets: Includes publications issued *prior to the date of request*.

- **Exclusions:**  
  The regulation does not cover:
  - Publications not classified as technical publications (e.g., general government notices).
  - Services provided free of charge under specific government programs or exemptions.

---

### **Legal Authority**
The regulation is made under the authority of **Order in Council P.C. 1992-2597**, which empowers the Minister of National Revenue to establish fees for services provided by the Department. This demonstrates a formal legislative basis for the fee structure.

---

### **Citation and Enforcement**
- The Order may be cited as: *Revenue Canada Technical Publication Subscription Service Fees Order*  
- It is published in the *Canada Gazette, Part II*, on February 1, 1993, making it publicly accessible and enforceable.

---

### **Summary Table of Fees**

| Service Type | Description | Fee |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| One-year subscription | Access to technical publications in current year or next two years | $20.00 |
| Two-year subscription | Access to technical publications in current year or next two years | $35.00 |
| Complete set of current publications (prior to request) | One-time delivery of all current publications before request date | $65.00 |

---

### **Conclusion**
This regulation provides a clear, transparent, and standardized fee structure for accessing Revenue Canada’s technical publications. It ensures that the Department can sustain the cost of delivering tax-related guidance while making it accessible to individuals and organizations that require such information. The fees are reasonable and tailored to different user needs—whether for ongoing access or a one-time comprehensive set.

The regulation remains a foundational administrative tool in managing the distribution of tax information and reflects a balance between public service delivery and financial sustainability.
