#### â±ï¸ When Service is Deemed Effective:
- **By fax or electronic means**:
  - Service is deemed effective on the **day received**, **unless**:
    - Received after 5:00 p.m. local time, or
    - Received on a Saturday or holiday.
  - In such cases, service is deemed effective on the **next business day** (not a Saturday or holiday).

- **By mail or registered mail**:
  - Service is deemed effective on the **day it is received**.
