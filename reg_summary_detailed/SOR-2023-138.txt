### **5. Multiple Violations (Ongoing Violations)**
- If a violation is **continued over time** (e.g., failure to comply for several days), each day of continuation constitutes a **separate violation**.
- This is indicated by an **"X"** in **Column 3** of a Part of Schedule 1.
- Example: A failure to report a hazard for 5 days results in **5 separate violations**, each subject to the applicable penalty structure.
