#### **2. Purpose of the By-law**
The original purpose of this by-law was to **establish clear criteria** under which member financial institutions (banks, trust companies, loan companies, etc.) could be subject to a **premium surcharge** imposed by CDIC.

A premium surcharge is an additional cost on top of the standard deposit insurance premium, charged to institutions that fail to meet certain regulatory or operational standards. The goal is to:
- Encourage compliance with deposit insurance policy requirements.
- Promote sound financial practices and governance.
- Protect depositors and maintain the stability of the financial system.
