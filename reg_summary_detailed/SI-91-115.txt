### 7. **Residency and Creditable Periods (Article VI)**

#### A. **For Canada Pension Benefits (Old Age Security Act):**
- If a person is subject to **CPP or provincial pension plans** during residence in the Netherlands:

- If a person is **obligatorily subject to Dutch legislation** during residence in Canada:
  - That period is **not counted** as residence in Canada (unless they are covered by CPP or provincial plans).

#### B. **For Dutch Legislation:**
- A person subject to Dutch legislation is considered to be **residing in the Netherlands**.
