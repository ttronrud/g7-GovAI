## âœ… **Eligibility Criteria for Operating a Duty-Free Shop**

### 1. **For Corporations** (Operating at a Border Crossing Point)
A corporation is eligible **only if**:
- It is **incorporated in Canada**;
- All shares are **beneficially owned by**:
  - A **Canadian citizen or permanent resident**, OR
  - Another **Canadian-incorporated corporation** where **all shares** are beneficially owned by a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, OR
  - A **combination** of both (a Canadian citizen/permanent resident + a qualifying Canadian corporation);
- The Canadian citizen or permanent resident must:
  - Be of **good character**;
  - Maintain their **principal residence in Canada**;
  - Have **resided in Canada for at least 183 days** in the year before the application.

> ğŸ” *Note*: This ensures that ownership remains within Canada and that key stakeholders have strong ties to the country.

### 2. **For Individuals** (Not Corporations)

- Of **good character**;
- Maintains **principal residence in Canada**;

## ğŸ›‘ **Grounds for Suspension or Cancellation**

The Minister may **suspend or cancel** a licence if:

### Suspension (Subject to Section 10)
- The licensee:
  - No longer owns or leases the premises;
  - Is subject to **receivership**;
  - Fails to comply with any law related to **import/export, customs, or excise**;
  - Acts **dishonestly** in business dealings (with customers, suppliers, customs brokers, carriers, or government);
  - Fails to respond to a **proposal made during the application process**;
  - The **volume of sales is no longer sufficient** to justify operation;
  - There is **no longer a need** for a duty-free shop in the area;
  - The **customs agency cannot provide services**;
  - The shop has been **altered in a way that**:
    - Contravenes storage/sale rules;
    - Impedes traffic flow;
    - Detrimentally affects immediate export of goods.

### Cancellation (Subject to Section 10)
- The licensee:
  - Made a **misrepresentation** in the application;
  - No longer qualifies under these regulations;
  - Is **bankrupt**;
  - Has **altered the structure** of the shop in a way that violates customs or traffic rules.
