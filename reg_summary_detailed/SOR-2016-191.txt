**Regulatory Authority and Basis:**

The *Restraint Systems and Booster Seats for Motor Vehicles Regulations* are issued under the authority of the *Canada Consumer Product Safety Act* (CCPSA), specifically pursuant to **Section 37** of that Act. This section empowers the Governor General in Council (acting on the recommendation of the Minister of Health) to make regulations that establish safety standards for consumer products, including motor vehicle restraint systems and booster seats.

**Regulatory Purpose:**

The primary objective of these regulations is to ensure the safety of children and other occupants in motor vehicles by establishing minimum performance and safety requirements for restraint systems and booster seats. These standards aim to reduce the risk of injury or death in the event of a vehicle crash by ensuring that all such products meet rigorous safety benchmarks.
