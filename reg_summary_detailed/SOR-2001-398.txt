## üìä Valuation of Interests in Real Property

### General Rule (Sections 8‚Äì12)
For the purposes of sections 8 to 12 of the TLC Act, the **book value** of an interest in real property is determined based on:
- **Real property**: Gross book value minus accumulated depreciation (as per the Superintendent‚Äôs accounting principles under subsection 313(4) of the TLC Act).
- **Securities or loans**: Book value as reported on the company‚Äôs balance sheet under the same accounting standards.

> This ensures consistency in valuation using standardized financial reporting practices.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Exemptions from Sections 464‚Äì466 of the TLC Act

Certain large or specially designated companies are **exempt** from the investment limits set out in sections 464 to 466 of the TLC Act, including:

1. **Widely Held Companies** with equity of **$5 billion or more**.
2. Companies with **$5 billion or more in equity** that are controlled by:
   - A **widely held bank holding company**;
   - A **widely held insurance holding company**;
   - An **eligible financial institution** (as defined in section 370 of the *Bank Act*, excluding foreign banks);
   - A **foreign bank** that is "widely held" (as defined in section 2.3 of the *Bank Act*).

> These exemptions recognize that such institutions are subject to different regulatory regimes and systemic importance, thus requiring different capital and risk management frameworks.

## üîê Prescribed Subsidiaries and Thresholds

- **Prescribed Subsidiaries** are subsidiaries of a company that are **excluded** from the scope of sections 464 and 465:
  - Insurance companies;
  - Securities dealers;
  - Subsidiaries of insurance companies or securities dealers.

> This means only certain subsidiaries (non-financial, non-insurance, non-dealer) are subject to investment limits.

### Percentage Thresholds:
- **Section 464 and 465**: The **prescribed percentage** is **70%**.  
  ‚Üí A company must not have more than 70% ownership in a related real property entity to be subject to investment limits.
- **Section 466**: The **prescribed percentage** is **100%**.  
  ‚Üí Applies when a company or its subsidiaries have full ownership in a related real property entity.

> These percentages define the threshold at which a company's exposure to real property interests becomes subject to regulatory scrutiny.

## üí∞ Valuation of Participating Shares and Ownership Interests

- The **value** of participating shares or ownership interests in related real property entities (held by the company or its subsidiaries) is equal to their **book value**, as reported on the **consolidated balance sheet** of the company.

> This ensures that the value used in calculations reflects actual financial reporting, not market speculation.

## üè¢ Interests of a Company in Real Property (Defined)

The following are considered **interests in real property** under the regulations:

### 1. Direct Ownership
- Real property beneficially owned by:
  - The company; or
  - A designated entity controlled by the company.

### 2. Debt Obligations for Real Property
- A debt obligation issued **for the purpose of acquiring or improving** real property, where:
  - The company or a designated entity is liable.

> Value = **Book value of debt minus book value of real property** (if positive).

---

## üîÑ Interests in Related Real Property Entities

### (a) Joint Ventures or Entities with Substantial Investment
- Real property beneficially owned by:
  - A **related real property entity** that is a joint venture; or
  - An entity where a designated entity has a substantial investment.

> Value = **Book value of real property**.

### (b) Shares/Ownership Interests in Related Entities
- Shares or ownership interests beneficially owned by the company or a designated entity in a related real property entity (excluding those in joint ventures).

> Value = **Book value of shares/interests**.

---

## üí≥ Debt Obligations and Loans (Including Guarantees)

### (a) Debt Obligations Issued by Related Entities
- Issued by a related real property entity and beneficially owned by the company or a designated entity.

> Value = **Book value of the debt obligation**.

### (b) Loans to Related Real Property Entities
- Made by the company or designated entity to a related real property entity.

> Value = **Book value of the loan**.

### (c) Loans to Entities Controlled by Dealers or Insurers
- Made to a real property entity where:
  - A securities dealer or insurance company controlled by the company has a substantial investment; or
  - The entity is controlled by a real property entity described above.

> Value = **Book value of the loan**.

### (d) Debt Obligations Guaranteed by the Company
- Issued by a related real property entity, beneficially owned by a **third party**, but **guaranteed** by the company or a designated entity.

> Value =  
> - If the related entity owns real property (an interest of the company):  
>   ‚Üí **Amount by which face value of guarantee exceeds value of real property** (under paragraph 9(2)(a))  
> - Otherwise:  
>   ‚Üí **Face value of the guarantee**

### (e) Loans Guaranteed by the Company
- Made by a third party to a related real property entity, and **guaranteed** by the company or a designated entity.

> Value =  
> - If the related entity owns real property:  
>   ‚Üí **Amount by which face value of guarantee exceeds value of real property**  
> - Otherwise:  
>   ‚Üí **Face value of the guarantee**
