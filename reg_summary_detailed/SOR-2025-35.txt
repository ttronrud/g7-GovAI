A person is eligible to receive a Canada Disability Benefit for any month **after May 2025** if they meet **all** of the following:

1. **Age Requirement**

   - Not more than 65 years old  

2. **DTC-Eligibility**  
   - Must be a *DTC-eligible individual* (as defined in subsection 146.4(1) of the *Income Tax Act*)  

3. **Residency Requirement**  
   - Must be a resident in Canada for income tax purposes and must be one of the following:

     - A protected person (under *IRPA*)  
     - An "Indian" as defined in *section 2(1) of the Indian Act*  

4. **No Incarceration**  
   - Not incarcerated due to a sentence of **two years or more** to be served in a **penitentiary** under any Act of Parliament  

5. **Filing of Income Return**  
   - Must have filed a **return of income** under the *Income Tax Act* for the **last taxation year ending before the start of the payment period** in which the month falls
