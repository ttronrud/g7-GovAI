### ðŸš« Disabilities That Render a Person Unfit for Pilotage Duties
A physician must determine whether the applicant or holder suffers from any of the following:

1. **Impairment causing unpredictable loss of consciousness** (not controlled by medication).
2. **Disorder preventing effective reaction** during pilotage duties.
3. **Disorder preventing strength, agility, or ability** to perform duties in all weather and sea conditions.
4. **Disorder preventing clear and prompt speech** during critical operations.
5. **Condition endangering others**, considering duration of duty and onboard conditions.
6. **Condition requiring emergency medical care**, not controlled by medication.
7. **Active psychiatric disorder**, including **drug or alcohol dependence or abuse**.

> These are strict, safety-focused criteria that prioritize the **safety of the vessel and crew**.
