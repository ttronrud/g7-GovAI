### ðŸ”¹ **Legal Authority and Purpose**
The regulation is enacted under two primary statutes:

1. **Section 38a of the Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions Act (OSFI Act)**  
2. **Section 39c of the Pension Benefits Standards Act, 1985 (PBSA)**

These provisions empower the Governor in Council (on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance) to establish rules for assessing pension plans based on their size, structure, and administrative burden.

> **Purpose**: To ensure that pension plan administrators contribute fairly to the administrative costs of regulating pension plans in Canada, particularly those governed by the *Pension Benefits Standards Act, 1985* and the *Pooled Registered Pension Plans Act (PRPPA)*. The assessment serves as a funding mechanism for the Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions (OSFI) to cover operational expenses related to plan registration, supervision, and inspection.
