### **Purpose of the Regulation**

The primary purpose of this regulation is to **designate the appropriate provincial or territorial authority** responsible for administering and managing student financial assistance programs within each province or territory of Canada. 

Under the Canada Student Financial Assistance Act, federal financial support (such as Canada Student Grants and Loans) is provided to eligible students, and the Act establishes a framework for how these funds are distributed and managed. This regulation clarifies which **provincial authority** is designated to act as the **designated body** for receiving, processing, and disbursing federal student financial assistance on behalf of students in each province.

This designation ensures **cooperative administration** between federal and provincial governments in the delivery of student financial aid, while maintaining consistency and accountability in the process.
