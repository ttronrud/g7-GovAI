### **Detailed Summary of the Regulation**

#### **1. Legal Authority and Issuance**
- **Issuing Body**: His Excellency the Governor General in Council.
- **Recommendation**: Made by the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development.
- **Legal Basis**: Pursuant to **paragraph 23(a) of the Territorial Lands Act**.
- **Purpose**: To withdraw certain lands from disposal (i.e., from being made available for sale, lease, or other forms of commercial development).

---

#### **2. Lands Affected**
- The lands described in the **schedule attached to this order** are being withdrawn from disposal.
- This includes:
  - All **mineral rights** (solid, liquid, gaseous).
  - All **construction materials** (e.g., aggregates, stone) that may be disposed of under the *Territorial Quarrying Regulations*.
  - All **timber** that may be disposed of under the *Forest Management Act (Northwest Territories)*.

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important Note**: This withdrawal does **not** prevent or affect existing rights or interests in the land ‚Äî those rights remain intact and are protected.

#### **6. Saving, Exempting, and Reserving Provisions**

- The order **does not affect** the rights or obligations of any party holding such leases or agreements.
