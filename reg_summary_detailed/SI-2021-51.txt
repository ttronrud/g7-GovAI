**Regulatory Summary: Designation of the Federal Economic Development Agency for Northern Ontario (FedNor) as a Department and Appointment of its President as Deputy Head**

**Effective Date:** August 12, 2021  
**Legal Authority:**  
- Public Service Employment Act (PSEA), specifically paragraph (b) of the definition “department” in subsection 2(1)  
- Public Service Employment Act, specifically paragraph (b) of the definition “deputy head” in subsection 2(1)  
- Statutes of Canada, 2003, Chapter 22 (S.C. 2003, c. 22, sections 12 and 13)

---

**1. Designation of the Federal Economic Development Agency for Northern Ontario (FedNor) as a Department**

Under paragraph (b) of the definition “department” in subsection 2(1) of the *Public Service Employment Act*, a department is defined as “a government agency that is established by or under an Act of Parliament and that is responsible for carrying out a public service function.”

On August 12, 2021, Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, acting on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, formally designated the **Federal Economic Development Agency for Northern Ontario (FedNor)** as a **department** for the purposes of the Public Service Employment Act.

**Significance of this designation:**
- FedNor is now subject to the full scope of employment rules, policies, and protections under the PSEA.
- This includes provisions related to hiring, promotion, job classification, pay equity, human resources management, and the rights and responsibilities of employees and managers.
- The designation ensures that FedNor’s operations are aligned with federal public service standards and accountability mechanisms.
- It affirms FedNor’s status as a core government entity with a defined public service mandate, rather than being treated merely as a government-funded or operating agency.

---

**2. Designation of the President of FedNor as Deputy Head**

Pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition “deputy head” in subsection 2(1) of the *Public Service Employment Act*, a deputy head is defined as “a person who, by virtue of office, is responsible for the day-to-day management and oversight of a department or agency.”

On the same date (August 12, 2021), the Governor General in Council designated the **President of the Federal Economic Development Agency for Northern Ontario** as the **deputy head** of the Agency for the purposes of the PSEA.

**Significance of this designation:**
- The President of FedNor now holds formal authority and accountability under the Public Service Employment Act as a deputy head.
- This includes responsibility for managing personnel, implementing policies, ensuring compliance with employment laws, and overseeing the Agency’s operations in accordance with federal public service standards.
- The designation enhances the institutional legitimacy and governance structure of FedNor within the broader federal public service framework.
- It establishes clear leadership accountability and ensures that the President is subject to the same employment and management regulations as other deputy heads in federal departments.
