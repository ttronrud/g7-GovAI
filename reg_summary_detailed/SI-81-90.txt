### **Regulation Summary: Assignment of Public Lands for Wildlife Research, Conservation, and Interpretation**

#### **1. Legal Authority and Basis**
- The regulation is issued under the **Canada Wildlife Act**.
- It is authorized by **subsection 4(1)** of the Act, which allows the Governor General in Council (on the recommendation of the Minister of the Environment) to designate public lands for **wildlife research, conservation, or interpretation**.
- Pursuant to **subsection 4(2)** of the same Act, the regulation assigns **administrative, managerial, and control responsibilities** over the designated lands to the **Minister of the Environment**.

> This means that the Minister now has the authority to manage and oversee the use, protection, and development of the specified lands for the purpose of supporting wildlife-related activities.
