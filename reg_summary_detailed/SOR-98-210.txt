## ðŸ¢ **Storage Requirements by Firearm Type**

### 1. **Non-restricted Firearm, Restricted Firearm, or Prohibited Handgun**

#### General Storage Conditions:
- Must be **unloaded**.
- Stored in one of the following:
  - A **securely locked cabinet, vault, or safe**
  - A **securely locked location**
  - A location **readily accessible only to the owner or an employee** of the business
- Must be stored on premises where:
  - An **electronic burglar alarm system** is in place
  - **All windows** (that can be opened) and **all exterior doors** can be **securely locked**

#### Exception (Approved Security):
- If the storage location has **security equal to or superior** to the above (e.g., reinforced concrete, armed security), and is **approved in writing** by the **Chief Firearms Officer (CFO)** who issued the business licence â€” storage is permitted.

#### Additional Requirement for Firearms Kept for Storage:
- Must be **separated from other weapons** used for sale or maintenance/repair.
- Must be stored in a place **not visible** to the public or non-employees.
