### 2. **Legal Basis: Section 745 of the Criminal Code**
Section 745 of the *Criminal Code* (Canada) allows a person convicted of a criminal offence to apply to the court for a **reduction in the period of imprisonment during which they are not eligible for parole** ‚Äî commonly known as the "parole ineligibility period."

- The application must be made **while the person is incarcerated** and is typically submitted after the original sentence has been imposed.
