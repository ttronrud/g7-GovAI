## ğŸ” **Overview of the Regulation**

The **Crib, Cradles and Bassinets Regulations** (S.C. 2010, c. 21) establish detailed safety standards for cribs, cradles, bassinets, sleep accessories, and related products in Canada. These regulations are designed to protect children from injury during sleep, play, and care by setting strict requirements on design, materials, structural integrity, and performance.

The regulations apply to all **cribs, cradles, bassinets, sleep accessories**, and **stands** (e.g., elevating devices) that are **designed or advertised for use with a child**, including those used for sleeping, changing, or play.

These standards are enforceable under the *Canada Consumer Product Safety Act*, and violations may result in product recalls, fines, or other penalties.
