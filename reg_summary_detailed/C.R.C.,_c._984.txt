## ğŸ’° Eligible Expenses for Removal Allowance (Section 40(2))

The following expenses are **prescribed as eligible** for reimbursement under section 40 of the Judges Act:

### 1. **Traveling Costs**
- Meals and lodging incurred in moving the judge, household member, survivor, or child from former to new residence.

### 2. **Transportation & Storage of Household Effects**
- Costs to transport or store personal belongings during the move.

### 3. **Temporary Stays**
- Meals and lodging for household members or survivors during temporary stays near former or new residence.
- Maximum: **30 days**, or up to a longer period determined by the Minister of Justice based on market conditions and family needs.

### 4. **Cancellation of Lease**
- Cost of canceling a lease on the former residence.

### 5. **Selling Costs**
- Costs associated with selling the former residence (e.g., real estate agent fees, advertising).

### 6. **Purchase and Transfer Costs**
- Legal fees related to buying the new residence.
- Taxes on transfer or registration of title to the new residence.

### 7. **Child-Care Expenses**
- Costs incurred while the judge and spouse/common-law partner, or survivor, are away searching for a new residence.

### 8. **Mortgage Interest Differential**
- Difference between mortgage interest on the new residence and the former residence, **up to $5,000 per year for a maximum of five years**.
- Only applies if the new mortgage interest is higher.

### 9. **Mortgage Default Insurance & Processing Fees**
- Eligible **only if**:
  - The former residence was owned by the judge, spouse, or child.
  - Equity in the former residence is transferred to the new residence.
  - Equity in the new residence is **less than 25%** of its cost.
  - Premium is paid in a single lump sum.

### 10. **Maintenance Costs of Former Residence**
- Costs of maintaining the former residence (mortgage interest, property taxes, insurance, heating) **for up to six months** after vacating it.
- Excludes rent received.

### 11. **Pre-Relocation Travel & Lodging (on Appointment/Transfer)**
- Travel and lodging costs incurred **before permanent residence is established** or for visiting household members still in the former residence, within **six months** of appointment or transfer.
