### ðŸ“‚ **5. Data Processing and Transformation Rules**

Determines what constitutes "transformation" of raw data:

- **Not considered transformation**:
  - Any process that retains phase information (e.g., producing **Single Look Complex (SLC)** output).
  
- **Considered transformation**:
  - Any process that **rectifies errors, distortions, or artifacts** via pixel aggregation, averaging, or resampling **if** it:
    - Radiometrically calibrates the data; **or**
    - Geocodes the data (resamples to Earth features).

> This distinction is critical for regulatory compliance and data classification.

### ðŸ’° **6. Fees and Services for Ministers**

When a minister provides a service to a licensee (e.g., priority access to data or support), the fee must not exceed:

- The amount agreed upon in a **prior agreement** between the minister and licensee; **or**
- If no agreement exists: an amount **proportional** to what the licensee would have received for a **comparable service** provided on a **priority basis** to any other entity in the previous 12 months.

> Ensures fairness and prevents overcharging.
