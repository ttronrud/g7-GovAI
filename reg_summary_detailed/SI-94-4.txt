## ðŸ“Œ **Core Principles of the Agreement**

### 1. **Residency and Eligibility**
- Any person who is **subject to the legislation of any Party**, and their **dependants and survivors**, shall:
  - Be **subject to the obligations** of the legislation of **any other Party** while residing there.
  - Be **eligible for benefits** under that legislation under **the same conditions** as a resident of that jurisdiction.

> This ensures that individuals are not disadvantaged by moving between jurisdictions.

---

### 2. **No Reduction or Suspension of Benefits**
- Benefits under the Agreement shall **not be reduced, modified, suspended, or cancelled** solely because the person resides in another Party.
- Benefits are **paid in the territory of the other Party** where the person resides.

> This protects individuals from benefit erosion due to cross-border residence.

---

### 3. **Benefit Payment for Multiple Party Exposure**
- If a person is subject to the legislation of **more than one Party**, or if dependants/survivors are covered by multiple systems:
  - Benefits shall be paid in a **third State** (i.e., not in the jurisdiction where the person is primarily covered).
