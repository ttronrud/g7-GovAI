### Step-by-Step Process:

#### Step 1: Calculate the Actuarial Present Value (APV)
- Determine the APV of:

  - Any **supplementary benefits under Part IV** of the Act
- **Exclude** the death benefit **if** the MP would have received it had they died on the election date (this is a key exception)

#### Step 2: Convert the APV into Two Components

1. **Immediate Single Life Annuity (for the former MP)**

   - Includes supplementary benefits under Part IV

2. **Joint and Survivor Benefit Package (for MP and spouse)**  
   Composed of:
   - **Immediate annuity for the former MP** (during their lifetime), where a **percentage (40%, 50%, or 60%)** of the total benefit is allocated to the spouse

     - Begins on the **day after the former MP dies**
     - Equal to the **same percentage (40%, 50%, or 60%)** of the MPâ€™s annuity
     - Also includes supplementary benefits under Part IV

> The percentage (40%, 50%, or 60%) is chosen by the former MP at election time.

#### Step 3: Adjust the First Payment

- Subtract the **first monthly installment** of the spouseâ€™s annuity (from component 2)
