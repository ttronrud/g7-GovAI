This provision outlines specific **exemptions or justifications** for insider trading by insiders (e.g., directors, officers, or key employees) who purchase or sell securities **after acquiring confidential information**.

2. **Pursuant to participation in an automatic dividend reinvestment plan, share purchase plan, or similar automatic plan**
